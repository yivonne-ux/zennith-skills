## PROVEN PURCHASE FORMULA (every Pinxin winner shares this DNA)
1. **Multiple dishes visible** — 6-16 dishes shown (grid, collage, or flat lay), NOT just 1
2. **Clear promo math** — 买X送X, RM价格, percentage off
3. **Urgency trigger** — 限时, X天限定, 最后XX套 (brand-created > festival-dependent)
4. **ORDER_NOW CTA** → pinxinvegan.com/collections/promotional-combo
5. **9:16 vertical** (1080x1920) for feed placement

**Why:** Data from March 2026. TNG Day RM72 CPA, 元宵 IMAGE 1 RM91 CPA, weekly calendar RM92 CPA. ALL follow this formula. EGC gimmick videos = RM310 spent, ZERO purchases.

**How to apply:** When producing new Pinxin creatives, ALWAYS use combo-showcase format. Never prioritize gimmick/entertainment over product showcase. The image IS the selling — show the food, show the value, show the deadline.

## FAILED FORMAT: EGC gimmick video
- 28 EGC video concepts in Notion (Spiderman, rolling on floor, catching dishes, hammering boxes, dancing)
- Same 4 copy templates rotated across 28 hooks
- Deployed: Batch2 (RM183, 0p), Gimmick-Shorts (RM127, 0p)
- **Why they fail:** gimmick IS the content, product is afterthought. Videos don't showcase food variety.
- NOT saying video never works — saying GIMMICK video without food showcase doesn't convert

## URGENCY HOOKS (ranked by performance)
1. **Partnership events** (TNG Day = RM72 CPA) — brand-created, controllable
2. **Festival tie-in** (元宵 = RM91 CPA) — works but expires, can't reuse
3. **Evergreen urgency** — "限时优惠", "最后XX套", "本周限定" — untested but recommended
4. **Raya** — ends March 24 2026, too late to build around

## AD COPY STRUCTURE (from working LowestCost ads)
- **Primary text (message):** Product name + speed (15分钟) + price (RM12++) + personal endorsement + ingredients + promo + CTA
- **Headline (name):** Short, punchy (e.g. "今天的晚餐", "买8送8 · 限时优惠")
- **Description:** Product category + key benefit + shipping (e.g. "品馨冷冻料理包 · 15分钟上桌 · 免运费")
- **NEVER leave headline/description blank** — LowestCost ads need all 3 text fields
