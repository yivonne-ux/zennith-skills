"Design can be variable, branding is 1."

- Characters: mascots, girls, boys, imaginary creatures, abstract shapes — ALL welcome
- Layouts: comics, memes, photo hybrids, illustrated scenes, big type, grids — ALL welcome
- But EVERY post must be unmistakably Bloom & Bare:
  - Palette locked: cream #F5F0E8, yellow #F0D637, blue #9DD5DB, green #7DC591, coral #F09B8B, lavender #B8A0C8, orange #E88A3A
  - Typography feel: DX Lactos (display) + Mabry Pro (body)
  - Tone: warm, playful, intentional, parent-friendly
  - Quality: world-class, sophisticated, NOT generic/cheap
- Do NOT limit to only mascot shapes — that's too narrow
- The feed should have visual VARIETY but feel like ONE brand
- Analogy: Headspace uses blobs, humans, abstract — but always looks like Headspace
