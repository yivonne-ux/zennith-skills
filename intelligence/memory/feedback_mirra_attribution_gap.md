## The Gap
- Meta tracks: ad → WhatsApp conversation (click-to-message)
- Google Sheet tracks: WA/IG/FB + EN/CHI + NEW/REPEAT + amount
- NO link between specific ad and specific sale
- 97% of WA conversations don't convert to orders (3% conversion rate)

## Impact on Optimization
- Cannot calculate per-ad ROAS
- Kill decisions based on conversation cost are directionally correct but not precise
- Food MOFU ads may appear "expensive" per conversation but serve funnel role
- CN ads appear to generate conversations but 5x fewer actual sales per RM spent

## What to Do
1. Conservative kills only (RM15+/convo AND 0 conversions AND RM15+ spend)
2. Set up attribution: UTM per ad set → WA welcome message → tag in Google Sheet
3. Or: Meta CAPI offline conversion upload (match phone numbers)
4. Until attribution exists, use language-level ROAS as the best proxy

**Why:** User's exact feedback: "how u get conversion data to kill? wa no data. only the google sheet sales." Every future session must remember this gap exists.

**How to apply:** Before recommending kills, always caveat that we're using conversation cost as proxy, not actual revenue data. Prioritize setting up attribution over aggressive optimization.
