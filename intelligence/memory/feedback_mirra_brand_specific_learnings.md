## MIRRA BRAND-SPECIFIC LEARNINGS (v2 — SPARKLE GLAMOUR)

### Brand Identity
- Mirra is a PLANT-BASED BENTO DELIVERY brand — NOT frozen food
- Target: women 25-32, Malaysian, girlboss queen energy
- Voice: unapologetic, first-person, no exclamation marks
- Organic social = engagement-first (follow > save > share), NOT conversion
- **CORE DNA: SPARKLE GLAMOUR** — every Mirra post should feel like it was dipped in pink glitter

### Palette + TEXTURE (not just hex codes)
- Mirra pink = MUTED blush (#F8BECD), dusty rose (#EBAABD), salmon (#F7AB9F)
- NOT neon hot pink (#FF69B4) — never
- Never: green/sage/nature backgrounds (off-brand)
- **TEXTURE IS MANDATORY** — never flat solid color backgrounds:
  - Glitter dust overlay (fine sparkle, 1-3px bright specks)
  - Rose petal scatter (blush/dusty pink petals, soft focus)
  - Holographic shimmer on surfaces
  - Warm light leaks (golden-pink, candlelit glow)
  - Satin/velvet fabric textures
  - Blush watercolor wash gradients
- **Mirra warmth = CANDLELIT, not sunlit.** Rosy soft-focus, not bright airy.
- Dark-warm WITH sparkle IS on-brand. The darkness + sparkle = the sophistication.
- Flat, clinical, "clean and bright" = OFF-BRAND. That's a wellness brand, not Mirra.

### Atmosphere Keywords (use in EVERY prompt)
- "pink glitter dust overlay, holographic shimmer"
- "luxe feminine energy, queen aesthetic"
- "warm candlelit glow, not sunlit airy"
- "sparkle accents throughout, tiny bright specks catching light"
- "satin/velvet texture feel"
- "girlish sophistication — glamorous but not tacky"

### Food Rules
- ALL food plant-based vegan — no meat, fish, eggs, dairy EVER
- Real Mirra bento photos = SACRED — never AI-generate the food
- Bento photos at: `01_assets/photos/food-library/` (top-view PNGs)
- **Food photos need EDITORIAL ENHANCEMENT before layout** (brightness +12%, warmth +8%, color +8%, sharpness +15%)
- **GLAMOUR goes on BACKGROUND only, NEVER on food** — sparkle bg, elegant labels, warm glow AROUND the bento
- **NEVER add bling, gems, pearls, glitter ON the food** — food is SACRED and UNTOUCHABLE
- **NEVER use luxe food refs (jewel toast, pearl pizza) as Image 1 for food posts** — they add bling ON food
- Use background/mood refs for food posts: pink gradient, warm bokeh, elegant serif typography
- User approved: B2-27 background style (pink-lavender gradient), B2-12 font variation, B2-30 background treatment
- Calorie comparison: Mirra WINS on volume — "1 grab order = 2 Mirra bentos"
- Comparison "other side" food = REAL stock photos only (never AI food)
- No pricing in organic ("RM19" = ads only)
- No health claims in organic ("380 cal" = ads only)
- No CTAs in organic ("text us on whatsapp" = ads only)

### What Passes (approved formats — ALL with sparkle glamour treatment)
- Calorie volume comparison with REAL photos + sparkle bg
- Labeled ingredient breakdown with glamorous labels + glitter-dusted blush bg
- THIS OR THAT with sparkle divider + warm glamour lighting
- Korean webtoon illustrations with SPARKLE ATMOSPHERE (not flat bg)
- Glitter/marquee/neon signs from REAL sparkle photography refs
- Cat memes with HEAVY sparkle/glitter overlay (think: cat in glitter hoodie, cat in rhinestone blanket)
- Typography with DESIGN AESTHETIC — not text-on-gradient but glamorous props + sparkle + texture

### What Fails (rejected)
- Pretty bento + generic poetic copy = no value
- Product showcase grids = ad, not organic
- "Same calories" where Mirra ties = no catch
- Green/sage backgrounds = off-brand
- "Frozen food" framing = WRONG
- AI-generated food = fake
- **FLAT/CLINICAL/CLEAN outputs = OFF-BRAND. Mirra is NEVER minimalist.**
- **"Bright and airy" outputs = OFF-BRAND. That's a wellness brand.**
- **Text-on-plain-gradient typography = NOT Mirra. Typography needs DESIGN CONTEXT (props, texture, sparkle).**
- **Labeled bentos that look like nutrition infographics = OFF-BRAND. Need glamour treatment.**

### Illustration Style (Locked — with atmosphere fix)
- Korean semi-realistic (warmcorner.ai / gunelisa)
- NOT Disney, NOT Pixar, NOT chibi
- Mature face 26yo, dark brown straight-wavy hair
- **NEVER flat solid-color backgrounds** — use sparkle-dusted, textured, atmospheric backgrounds
- Rich background textures: blush watercolor, rose gold shimmer, soft bokeh lights, glitter particles
- Multiple "Mirra girls" — NOT one locked character
- All illustrations must be based on VIRAL FORMAT references
- **Sparkle/glow = PRIMARY design element, not afterthought**

### Logo
- PIL smart placement: cleanest zone (BR, BL, BC, TR)
- Black logo on light bg, white logo on dark bg
- 110px wide, 90% opacity
- NANO renders ZERO brand text (anti-brand prompt)
- Single logo rule — never double
