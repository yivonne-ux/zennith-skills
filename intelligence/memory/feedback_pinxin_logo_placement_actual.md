Studied actual Pinxin past work from Notion database. TWO logo placement modes:

**Mode A — Promotional/BOFU ads:**
- Asset: `P̌INXĪN` **serif wordmark** (NOT the ginkgo logomark)
- Position: **top-center** or **top-left** above the headline
- Size: ~15% canvas width, modest
- Color: gold or cream depending on background
- Used on: price ads, bundle promos, campaign ads

**Mode B — Social/organic content:**
- Asset: **Ginkgo logomark only** (NOT the wordmark)
- Position: **top-right**
- Size: ~40-50px, subtle whisper
- Color: gold on dark, beige on light
- Used on: feed posts, lifestyle content, brand awareness

**NEVER both on same design.** They alternate.

**Why:** User corrected twice. v1 batch had wrong logo asset AND wrong position. Must match actual brand usage.

**How to apply:** Check content type before placing logo. BOFU = wordmark top-center. Social = ginkgo top-right. PIL places the real PNG — NANO never renders any brand text.
