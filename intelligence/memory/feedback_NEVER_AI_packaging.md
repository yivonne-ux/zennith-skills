**ABSOLUTE RULE: NEVER let AI generate packaging, product photos, or food imagery.**

All of the following MUST come from real photography sourced from GDrive or local-cache:
- Frozen food packaging (vacuum bags, boxes, labels)
- Product labels and branding on packaging
- Food plating and dishes
- Product combo shots
- Any real-world branded material

**Why:** W3-WA-03 (Pinxin split-transformation ad) used an AI-generated frozen packet that looked nothing like real Pinxin packaging. This is:
1. **Misleading** — customers expect what they see in the ad
2. **Brand inconsistent** — AI invents packaging designs that don't exist
3. **Detectable** — savvy consumers and Meta reviewers can spot AI packaging

**How to apply:**
1. **Before any production run:** Check if the concept requires packaging or product shots
2. **Source from GDrive first:** `GoogleDrive-love@huemankind.world/My Drive/Pinxin/Photo Request/` has real product photos
3. **Copy to local-cache:** All production assets must be in `01_assets/local-cache/` before running
4. **Audit step:** Every post-production audit must verify: "Is any packaging/product shown AI-generated? If yes → REJECT and replace with real photo."

**Pinxin packaging assets now in local-cache:**
- `pkg-frozen-01.jpg` through `pkg-frozen-05-cn.jpg` — combo packaging photos (EN + CN)
- `pkg-topview-curry.png` — top-view packaging label (Classic Curry)

**This applies to ALL brands** — Pinxin, Mirra, Bloom & Bare, Jade Oracle. If a concept requires showing real product/packaging and we don't have the photo, SKIP the concept or source the photo first. Never generate it.
