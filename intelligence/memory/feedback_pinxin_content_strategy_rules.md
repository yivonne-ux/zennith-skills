## PX CONTENT PLANNING RULES (from strategy docs + engagement research)

### 10 Content Categories (use ALL of them, not just 2)
- CAT-01: Heritage Story (传承故事) — gold frames, cultural, founder
- CAT-02: Dish Hero (招牌主角) — single dish spotlight, editorial photography
- CAT-03: Benefit/USP Education (健康卖点) — carousel, myth-busting, ingredients
- CAT-04: Social Proof (口碑见证) — testimonials, Tatler badge, press
- CAT-05: Recipe/How-To (食谱教学) — step-by-step carousel, "3 ways to upgrade"
- CAT-06: Promo/Bundle (促销优惠) — price, urgency, CTA
- CAT-07: Festival/Cultural (节庆文化) — cultural moments, heritage
- CAT-08: Character/Illustration (家庭角色) — illustrated comics, quotes, family scenes
- CAT-09: Raw Ad/Lo-Fi (原始广告) — ugly ads, screenshot-style (Meta ads only)
- CAT-10: XHS/Lifestyle Native (小红书生活) — diary tone, bento shots, lifestyle

### ACCA Funnel Ratios (target for content mix)
- Awareness: 15%
- Consideration: 50%
- Conversion: 30%
- Advocacy: 5%

### Grid Rhythm
BOLD → PHOTO → CLEAN → repeat. Never 3 dark posts in a row.

### 6 Personas (rotate, never >2 consecutive same)
1. 忙妈妈 (Busy Mum) — PRIMARY
2. 三高阿公 (3-Highs Uncle)
3. 素食信仰者 (Religious Vegetarian)
4. 打工人 (Office Worker)
5. 健康养生族 (Health-Conscious)
6. 新加坡华人 (Singapore Chinese) — expansion, ads-only

### High-Shareability Formats (from 2026 research)
Tier 1 (highest DM send + save):
- Recipe step-by-step carousel
- "Send this to someone who..." relatable meme
- Myth vs Fact educational carousel
- Before/after transformation (frozen → plated)
- Expectation vs Reality

Tier 2 (strong engagement):
- This or That interactive carousel
- Tier list / ranking carousel
- Behind-the-scenes process
- User-generated content repost
- Food + lifestyle pairing

### Format Mix for Algorithm
- 40% Carousels (highest save rate, 6.9% engagement)
- 35% Reels (highest reach, 2.25x)
- 15% Stories (retention, polls)
- 10% Single image (grid aesthetic)

### What Makes Content SHAREABLE (not just likeable)
- Shareable = "I need someone to see this" (humor, identity, debate, utility)
- Likeable = "I appreciate this" (pretty photo)
- Gap = UTILITY + IDENTITY
- DM sends > likes for algorithm reach in 2026

### Content Planning Pre-flight
Before generating any content batch:
1. Check festival calendar (is this PX's festival?)
2. Map to ACCA funnel (is the ratio right?)
3. Rotate personas (who is this post for?)
4. Check grid rhythm (BOLD/PHOTO/CLEAN)
5. Vary categories (using at least 5 different CATs in any week)
6. Include high-shareability formats (at least 2 carousels per week)
