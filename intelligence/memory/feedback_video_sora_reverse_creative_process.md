## The Breakthrough (March 16, 2026)

Stop fighting the tool. Start designing FOR it.

**Old approach (failed V1-V6):** Reference video → force AI to replicate human UGC footage → fight character consistency, iPhone aesthetic, hair length → mediocre results

**New approach (Sora Masterpiece):** What does Sora do BEST → design the creative AROUND that → mix with real library footage → brand it with Remotion typography

## What Sora Actually Excels At
- Surreal/dreamlike continuous camera movements through impossible spaces
- Cinematic macro: food textures at microscopic detail, steam particles, liquid physics
- Atmospheric mood: golden light, volumetric shafts, particle dust, lens flares
- Material transformations, abstract beauty
- One continuous impossible camera move (crane through wall into kitchen into plate)
- Slow-motion beauty at levels no phone captures
- Environmental storytelling WITHOUT humans (space tells the story)
- Floating/suspended objects in golden atmospheric space

## The Reference Image Pipeline
```
Real Mirra food PHOTO
  → EDIT to editorial level (color grade, lighting, composition, crop)
  → Feed to Sora i2v with ultra-detailed cinematography prompt
  → Sora adds: camera motion, steam, light play, atmosphere
  → Output: cinematic food video that's unmistakably Mirra
```

**Why:** The reference image IS the art direction. Phone-shot photo → casual video. Editorial hero shot → cinematic video. Edit the image FIRST.

## Brand Constant, Format Variable — Applied to Video
Every video should be DIFFERENT in format/style but UNMISTAKABLY the same brand:
- Cinematic Sora food journeys (atmospheric, impossible beauty)
- UGC montage lifestyle (real/generated people, rapid cuts)
- Calculator/format hijack concepts (Remotion UI components)
- Talking head KOL testimonials (library + InfiniteTalk)
- Stop-motion/animation style (AI-generated or mixed media)
- Mixed media (real footage + animated overlays + doodles)
- Ambient ASMR food (no narration, pure visual + sound)

**ALL share:** warm amber-gold-blush palette, serif + Mabry Pro typography, warm grade, food integrity, "smart girlfriend" voice, Malaysian condo/kitchen/office world.

## Three Video Moods (user direction)
1. **So creative, so high hook** — format hijack, pattern interrupt, impossible visuals
2. **Sophisticated girlboss story** — narrative, transformation, aspiration
3. **Just food and vibes** — atmospheric, ASMR, sensory beauty, no narrative needed

## How to apply
Before every video: "Is this playing to Sora's strengths?" If you're forcing Sora to do something a human camera does better, you're doing it wrong. Use library/real footage for human moments, Sora for impossible beauty, Remotion for brand design.
