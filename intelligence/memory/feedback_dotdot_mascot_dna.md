## DOTDOT MASCOT — CHARACTER DNA

### Source
@business.shorts IG account (204K followers). Client's video reference from brief page 8.
User confirmed: use this as the DotDot mascot direction.

### Character Spec (EXACT)
- **Shape**: ROUND/SPHERICAL like a mochi or dumpling. NOT pill/capsule. NOT oval. ROUND.
- **Body**: Soft white/cream (#FAF5F0), slightly off-white. Head IS the body — one continuous round shape. No neck.
- **Limbs**: Tiny stub arms/legs. Sometimes no visible limbs. Limbs are simple, short, same color as body.
- **Face**: Two small black dot eyes (positioned HIGH on face, in upper 40%). Pink/peach blush on both cheeks. Tiny line mouth — can be: straight line, small open circle, tongue out, frown.
- **Outline**: THIN BLACK OUTLINE stroke on body edge. This character HAS outlines (unlike our flat editorial style which is strokeless).
- **Accessories**: NONE in original. No scarf, no hat, no clothing. Pure mochi shape. For DotDot: can add minimal brand element (tiny teal dot accent or small German flag) but keep it minimal.
- **Expression**: Conveyed by eye POSITION (looking up/down/side), blush INTENSITY, mouth SHAPE, body TILT. Not by eyebrows or complex facial features.
- **Scale**: Often appears in CROWDS of identical characters. One protagonist in focus, many background copies. Creates a "sea of mochi" visual.
- **Interaction**: Can hold objects (hearts, hammers, pottery tools), sit at tables, lie in hospital beds. Objects are in the same art style.
- **Palette**: Almost monochrome — white/cream bodies, pink blush, pink/cream accent bg. Minimal color. NOT colorful.

### What I Got WRONG in v7 (never repeat)
- ❌ Pill/capsule shape → ROUND mochi
- ❌ Teal scarf → NO accessories
- ❌ Strokeless flat editorial → has thin black OUTLINES
- ❌ Single mascot → often in CROWD of same characters
- ❌ Complex expressions → MINIMAL face, expression through position

### DotDot Adaptation
When integrating into DotDot posts:
- Keep the mochi shape and outline style
- Can add minimal DotDot brand touch (tiny teal bow/ribbon MAX, or dotdot.. dots as the eyes)
- The mascot posts should feel like the mascot lives IN the DotDot world
- Can coexist with the flat editorial illustration style for humans — but mascot itself keeps its outline style
- Background characters (the crowd) can appear but are optional for DotDot posts

### Prompt DNA for NANO
```
"A round mochi/dumpling-shaped character. Pure spherical white/cream body, head is the body,
no neck. Two small black dot eyes positioned high on face. Pink blush on both cheeks.
Tiny line mouth. Thin black outline on body edge. Tiny stub arms and legs.
No accessories, no clothing. Cute, minimal, expressive through body position and eye direction."
```

**How to apply:** Use this exact character description in ALL mascot-integrated posts. Never deviate from the round mochi shape. The character's charm is in its SIMPLICITY.
