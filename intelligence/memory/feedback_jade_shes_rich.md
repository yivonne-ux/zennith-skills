Jade is rich. Not aspirational luxury. ACTUAL wealth.

**Why this matters:** Rich people don't try to look rich. They don't need logos. They don't need to prove anything. The brands actual wealthy people choose: Brunello Cucinelli, Loro Piana, The Row, Bottega — zero visible branding. The MATERIAL speaks. The QUALITY is the statement.

**How to apply:**
- Brand should feel EFFORTLESS, not designed
- No performance of luxury — she just IS
- Materials over logos (real jade, real gold, thick stock)
- Her IG feels like she just lives this way, not curating
- Oracle cards are museum-quality OBJECTS, not "products"
- She wouldn't compromise on anything — but she'd never mention it
- The brand collateral should feel like it costs more than competitors' entire business
- Think Loro Piana cashmere: no logo visible, $3000 sweater, only people with taste know
