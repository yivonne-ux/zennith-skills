# Pinxin — The Brand Soul (from founder input, 2026-03-26)

## The Origin Story
A mother who loves to cook for her children. A woman who loves to cook for others. This is where Pinxin started — not from a business plan, but from love expressed through food.

## The Emotional Core
- **Connection through food** — with family, with friends. This is the feeling PX wants to evoke.
- **Family, mothers, culture** — these are the vibes, not just "premium" or "luxury"
- **Home familiar + street familiar** — the taste of what your mother would cook, or what you'd find at your favourite kopitiam
- **Cultural blend** — local, Nyonya, Chinese, heritage. Not one tradition, but the intersection of Malaysian-Chinese food culture

## Ingredient Philosophy
- Plant-based ingredients, but framed as "possibly ingredients a household would use"
- Low sodium salt, organic cane sugar — things a caring mother would choose
- Not "health food" — it's "food made with love and better ingredients"

## Content Direction (from user)
1. **Product viral trending posts** — still need conversion
2. **Comics / illustrations** — people, conversations, advice, happenings in Pinxin's "style"
   - This is the Pinxin Family character system
   - Illustrated conversations, cultural moments, relatable scenarios
   - Think: warm, cultural, family-oriented comics with brand integration
3. **High conversion is non-negotiable** — every post must sell

## How This Reshapes the Character
The "Penang Aunty" character is RIGHT but needs warmth dial turned up:
- Less "opinionated food critic" → More "your mum's friend who cooks amazing food"
- Less "surprising/disrupting" → More "familiar, comforting, like coming home"
- Keep: proud, cultural, educational
- Add: nurturing, generous, "eat more lah" energy

## The Feeling to Evoke
"Sitting at a family dinner table. Someone cooked with love. The food tastes like home. You didn't even realize it was plant-based because it didn't matter — it just tasted right."

## Platform Expression
- IG: warm editorial + illustrated comics + family moments
- TikTok: "mum's cooking" energy, cultural humor, taste tests
- XHS: diary-style "家的味道" (taste of home), detailed food stories
- FB: community, recipe sharing, family conversations
