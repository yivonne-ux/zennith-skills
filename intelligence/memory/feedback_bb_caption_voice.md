# BB Caption Voice Rules

User explicit instructions (2026-03-21):

1. **REAL** — not corporate, not generic
2. **Quirky** — humor welcome, make parents feel seen
3. **Evoke emotion** — wonder, nostalgia, the chaos of parenting
4. **Community building** — "us" not "we the brand"
5. **Malaysian KL context** — Bukit Jalil, local references
6. **Bilingual** — EN primary + CN translation at end (not direct translate)
7. **Every post has PURPOSE** — a message or emotion to deliver
8. **Don't be so flourish you sound default** — people just scroll past generic
9. **Never offensive about kids/parents** — warmth always
10. **Audio for posts** — user always picks from IG. Fun/easy-going OR slow/melancholic depending on post. Need "ear vision skills."

Example good voice (Hari Raya):
"Somewhere right now, a toddler is smearing kuih lapis on their baju raya and their mak is choosing to laugh instead of cry. That's the energy."

Example BB existing posts voice:
"We know they need messy. We know you hate the cleaning up."
