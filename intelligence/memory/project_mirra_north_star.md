## Mirra North Star (2026-03-13)

**Revenue target:** RM800,000/month
**ROAS target:** 4-5x minimum

### The math
- Average meal price: RM19
- Meals needed/month: ~42,105
- Meals needed/day: ~1,404
- If avg subscriber orders 20 meals/month: need ~2,105 active subscribers
- At ROAS 4x: ad spend = RM200,000/month (~RM6,667/day)
- At ROAS 5x: ad spend = RM160,000/month (~RM5,333/day)
- At 30+ ads/day: each ad needs to justify its existence against this target

### What this means for every decision
- No vanity metrics. Every ad is judged by: does this get us closer to RM800K/month?
- Creative that gets likes but no orders = worthless
- A "beautiful" ad that converts at 2x ROAS = not good enough
- An "ugly" ad that converts at 6x ROAS = run it harder
- Every new creative concept must answer: "will this drive purchases from 25-35 Chinese-speaking Malaysian women in KL/Selangor?"

### The obsession
- Convert like Glossier
- Retain like HelloFresh
- Feel like it belongs on Xiaohongshu
- Revenue like a real business: RM800K/month

**Why:** This is not a side project. This is a business that needs to hit RM800K every month. The ads are the engine. Every decision flows from this number.

**How to apply:** Before producing ANY ad creative, ask: "Does this ad have a clear path to driving a purchase?" If the answer is unclear, rethink the concept. Pretty is not enough. Smart is not enough. It must SELL.
