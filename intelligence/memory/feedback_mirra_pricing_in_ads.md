| Bestie Tone Up (2 pax) | RM21.40 | RM19.85 | RM18.65 |
| Fit Fam (3+ pax) | RM20.35 | RM18.95 | RM17.75 |

**How to apply:**
- Use **"from RM19++"** in all ads — honest, Malaysian convention, old winners used it
- RM19++ covers Solo 40-meal (RM19.45) truthfully
