name: AI-generated look = rejected, need art mastery
description: Grid/chart designs (alignment chart, this-or-that) can look generic AI. Must have editorial art direction, hand-drawn texture, intentional imperfection.
type: feedback
---

Designs like 3x3 alignment charts and split comparisons easily look AI-generated and generic. The user rejects these unless they have genuine art mastery:
- Hand-drawn/textured illustration style, NOT smooth vector clip-art
- Intentional imperfection (risograph grain, visible brush texture)
- Editorial-quality composition, not template-grid-fill
- Each cell/panel must feel individually art-directed
- "Lack of art sense and art mastery" = instant reject
- The design must look like an illustrator drew it, not like AI filled a grid
