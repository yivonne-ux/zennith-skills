The complete playbook for creating marketing content across all GAIA brands. Covers everything from concept to 分镜 (storyboard) to final output. Every agent involved in content creation reads this.

## 1. Content Personas & Brand Voice

## 2. Hook Templates (First 3 Seconds)

The hook determines 90% of engagement. Use these formulas:

### Curiosity Hooks
- "You've been eating [product] wrong your entire life"
- "This is the [product] that changed how I cook"
- "I can't believe this is vegan"
- "POV: you discover [brand] for the first time"

### Problem-Solution Hooks
- "Struggling to eat healthy? Watch this"
- "If you hate meal prep, you need this"
- "The vegan [dish] that actually tastes like the real thing"

### Social Proof Hooks
- "Why 10,000 Malaysians switched to this"
- "The product everyone in KL is talking about"
- "My honest review after 30 days"

### Trend Hooks
- "The [trending sound] but make it vegan"
- "Replying to @[commenter] — here's the recipe"
- "Things in my kitchen that just make sense"

### Numbers/List Hooks
- "3 vegan meals under RM15"
- "5 reasons to try plant-based this month"
- "I tested every vegan rendang in Malaysia"

## 3. Copywriting Formulas

### AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
```
Attention: Bold claim or surprising fact
Interest: Why this matters to YOU
Desire: Paint the picture of having it
Action: Clear CTA (Shop now / Link in bio / Swipe up)
```

### PAS (Problem → Agitation → Solution)
```
Problem: "Tired of tasteless vegan food?"
Agitation: "Every meal feels like a compromise..."
Solution: "GAIA Eats rendang — the one that actually tastes like mak's cooking."
```

### BAB (Before → After → Bridge)
```
Before: "I used to think vegan food = boring salads"
After: "Now I make restaurant-quality vegan meals in 15 minutes"
Bridge: "Here's the ingredient that changed everything → [product link]"
```

### Platform-Specific Copy Length
| Platform | Caption Length | Hashtags | CTA Style |
|----------|---------------|----------|-----------|
| IG Feed | 150-300 chars | 10-15 | "Link in bio" |
| IG Reels | 50-100 chars | 5-8 | "Follow for more" |
| TikTok | 50-100 chars | 3-5 | "Check link in bio" |
| Facebook | 100-250 chars | 2-3 | "Shop now" button |
| Xiaohongshu | 200-500 chars (CN) | 5-10 | "点击链接" |

## 5. UGC (User Generated Content)

### What is UGC
Content created by real people (or AI-generated to look authentic) that promotes products. Highest-converting ad format on Meta + TikTok.

### UGC Types
| Type | Description | Duration | Platform |
|------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Unboxing** | Open package, react | 15-30s | TikTok, IG Reels |
| **Product Review** | Honest opinion, demo | 30-60s | TikTok, YouTube |
| **Recipe/How-to** | Use product to make something | 30-90s | TikTok, IG, YouTube |
| **Day in My Life** | Product integrated naturally | 60-180s | TikTok, YouTube |
| **Before/After** | Transformation using product | 15-30s | IG Reels, TikTok |
| **Comparison** | Brand vs competitor | 30-60s | TikTok, YouTube |

### UGC Creator Brief Template
```
Brand: [GAIA Eats / Delulu Club / etc.]
Product: [specific product + link]
Platform: [TikTok / IG Reels / Both]
Duration: [15-30s / 30-60s]
Format: [Unboxing / Review / Recipe / Day-in-life]

Hook: [specific opening line or action]
Key messages:
  1. [main benefit to highlight]
  2. [secondary point]
  3. [CTA / where to buy]

Do:
  - Show real reaction
  - Good lighting (natural preferred)
  - Include product close-up
  - Mention price if it's a selling point

Don't:
  - Don't script word-for-word (should feel natural)
  - Don't use competitor names
  - Don't make health claims we can't back up

Deliverables:
  - Raw footage (vertical 9:16)
  - 2 takes minimum
  - B-roll of product (close-ups, texture, packaging)

Payment: RM [amount] per video
Usage rights: 6 months paid ads + organic
```

### UGC Payment Norms (Malaysia)
- Micro (1-10K followers): RM 100-300 per video
- Small (10-50K): RM 300-800
- Medium (50-200K): RM 800-2,000
- Large (200K+): RM 2,000-5,000+
- AI UGC: RM 0 (but review costs time)

## 6. A-Roll vs B-Roll

### A-Roll (Primary Footage)
The main content — the person talking, the recipe being made, the main action.
- **Talking head** — person speaking to camera
- **Main demo** — cooking, unboxing, using product
- **Interview** — Q&A format
- **Tips:** Eye contact, good audio, stable camera, rule of thirds

### B-Roll (Supporting Footage)
Cutaway shots that add visual interest and cover edits.
- **Product close-ups** — texture, packaging, label, pouring
- **Lifestyle shots** — kitchen scene, table setting, hands
- **Process shots** — ingredients, mixing, steam, sizzle
- **Environment** — store shelf, delivery, unboxing moment
- **Tips:** Variety of angles (overhead, side, close-up), slow motion for food, natural lighting

### Shot List Template (For Product Video)
```
A-Roll:
  □ Host intro (facing camera, waist up)
  □ Product reveal (holding product)
  □ Demo action (cooking/preparing)
  □ Tasting/reaction
  □ CTA (facing camera)

B-Roll:
  □ Package close-up (label visible)
  □ Ingredients laid out (flat lay, overhead)
  □ Pouring/mixing action (close-up)
  □ Steam/sizzle shot (slow-mo if possible)
  □ Finished dish (beauty shot, styled)
  □ Brand logo (on product or overlay)
```

## 7. 分镜 (Storyboarding) for Video Ads

### What is 分镜
分镜 (fēn jìng) = shot-by-shot breakdown of a video before production. Essential for consistent quality and efficient shoots.

### 分镜 Template

| Shot # | Duration | Shot Type | Description | Audio | Text Overlay |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | 0-3s | Close-up (hand) | Hand picks up product from shelf | Trending sound | "Wait for it..." |
| 2 | 3-6s | Medium (face) | Reaction shot — surprise | Sound continues | Product name |
| 3 | 6-10s | Close-up (product) | Open package, show contents | SFX: satisfying peel | "100% Plant-Based" |
| 4 | 10-15s | Medium (cooking) | Demo: add to pot, stir | Sound + sizzle SFX | Recipe step |
| 5 | 15-18s | Close-up (food) | Beauty shot of finished dish | Sound climax | — |
| 6 | 18-20s | Medium (face) | Tasting reaction + thumbs up | Sound outro | "Link in bio" |

### Video Ad 分镜 by Type

**5-Second Story Ad:**
1. Hook visual (0-1s) → 2. Product (1-3s) → 3. CTA + price (3-5s)

**15-Second Reel:**
1. Hook (0-3s) → 2. Problem (3-6s) → 3. Solution/demo (6-12s) → 4. CTA (12-15s)

**30-Second UGC:**
1. Hook (0-3s) → 2. Context (3-8s) → 3. Demo (8-20s) → 4. Result (20-25s) → 5. CTA (25-30s)

**60-Second Tutorial:**
1. Hook (0-3s) → 2. Intro (3-8s) → 3. Step 1 (8-18s) → 4. Step 2 (18-28s) → 5. Step 3 (28-38s) → 6. Result (38-50s) → 7. CTA (50-60s)

## 8. Visual Design System

### Hero Image Types
| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Product Hero** | Single product, clean background | Listings, ads |
| **Lifestyle Hero** | Product in context (kitchen, table) | Social, brand |
| **Flat Lay** | Overhead, ingredients/products arranged | IG feed, Pinterest |
| **Action Shot** | Product being used (cooking, pouring) | Reels thumbnail, ads |
| **Comparison** | Before/after or side-by-side | Ads, social proof |

### Design Elements Checklist
- **Logo:** Consistent placement (bottom-right for ads, center for brand content)
- **Typography:** Max 2 fonts (header + body). GAIA uses clean sans-serif.
- **Color Mood:** Pull from brand palette. GAIA Eats = sage green + gold + cream.
- **Banner Specs:** Shopee (1200x628), Lazada (750x500), TikTok (1200x628), IG (1080x1080)
- **Masthead:** Full-width banner with hero image + headline + CTA. Keep text under 20% of image area (Meta rule).
- **Animation:** Subtle motion for digital (Ken Burns on stills, text fade-in). No seizure-inducing effects.

### Vibes / Mood Board Categories
- **Fresh & Natural:** Light greens, whites, soft shadows, daylight
- **Bold & Energetic:** Saturated colors, dynamic angles, high contrast
- **Premium & Minimal:** Black/white + gold accent, lots of whitespace
- **Cozy & Warm:** Earth tones, soft textures, candlelight
- **Fun & Playful:** Bright colors, rounded shapes, casual typography

## 9. AI Video Generation (Kling / Runway / Pika)

### When to Use AI Video
- Quick social content that doesn't need a real shoot
- Concept visualization before investing in production
- B-roll generation (product spinning, ingredient falling, abstract brand vibes)
- 分镜 animatics (rough motion from storyboard)

### Best AI Video Tools (2026)
| Tool | Best For | Input | Output |
|------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Kling AI** | Realistic motion, product demos | Image + text prompt | 5-10s video |
| **Runway Gen-3** | Creative effects, transitions | Text/image prompt | 4-16s video |
| **Pika** | Quick social clips, animations | Text prompt | 3-8s video |
| **HeyGen** | Talking head / avatar videos | Script + avatar | 30s-5min |
| **Midjourney** | Still images (hero, lifestyle) | Text prompt | Images |
| **NanoBanana** | Product shots, flat lays | Product image + prompt | Styled images |

### AI Generation Workflow
```
1. Define 分镜 (storyboard with shot-by-shot plan)
2. Generate hero images (Midjourney/NanoBanana)
3. Animate hero images to video (Kling/Runway)
4. Add text overlays + music (CapCut/Premiere)
5. Review against brand guidelines (art-director skill)
6. Export per platform (9:16 for Reels/TikTok, 1:1 for Feed, 4:5 for Ads)
```

### Prompt Engineering for Product Video
```
Kling prompt example:
"Close-up of hands opening a premium food package, revealing golden vegan rendang inside.
Soft natural lighting, kitchen background, steam rising from the dish.
Camera slowly pushes in. Warm color grading, cinematic quality."

Runway prompt example:
"Product flat lay animation: vegan food items arrange themselves into a beautiful
composition on a marble surface. Stop-motion style, satisfying movement.
Brand colors: sage green and gold accents."
```

## 10. Music & Audio

### Music Selection Guide
| Content Type | Music Mood | BPM | Source |
|-------------|------------|-----|--------|
| Product showcase | Upbeat, modern | 110-130 | Trending TikTok sounds |
| Recipe tutorial | Chill, acoustic | 80-100 | Royalty-free libraries |
| Brand story | Emotional, cinematic | 60-80 | Licensed music |
| Flash sale | Urgent, energetic | 130-150 | Trending sounds |
| Unboxing | Satisfying, ASMR-ish | 80-100 | SFX + light music |

### Audio Tips
- Always include captions/subtitles (80% of social video is watched on mute)
- Use trending TikTok sounds when possible (algorithm boost)
- SFX > music for product close-ups (sizzle, crunch, pour sounds)
- Voice-over should be natural, not radio-announcer style

## 11. Meta Ad Upload Checklist

Before uploading any creative to Meta Ads Manager:

```
Pre-Upload:
  □ Correct aspect ratio for placement (1:1, 4:5, 9:16)
  □ File size under 4GB (video) or 30MB (image)
  □ Text overlay under 20% of image area
  □ No prohibited content (claims, logos of other brands)
  □ CTA matches landing page

Upload:
  □ Primary text (125 chars max visible)
  □ Headline (40 chars ideal)
  □ Description (30 chars ideal)
  □ CTA button selected
  □ Landing URL set and tested
  □ UTM parameters added

Post-Upload:
  □ Preview on all placements (Feed, Stories, Reels, Audience Network)
  □ Check mobile rendering
  □ Verify pixel/CAPI is firing on landing page
```
