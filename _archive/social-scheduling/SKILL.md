# Social Scheduling

Optimal posting times and queue management for social media.

## Owner
Iris

## What It Does
1. Determine optimal posting times by platform (IG, FB, TikTok)
2. Queue management for scheduled posts
3. Malaysia timezone aware (MYT UTC+8)
4. Track post performance by time slot
5. Adjust schedule based on engagement data

## Peak Times (Malaysia)
- Lunch: 12:00 - 14:00 MYT
- Evening: 19:00 - 22:00 MYT
- Weekend: 10:00 - 12:00 MYT (additional window)

## Platform-Specific
- Instagram: Best 12pm, 7pm MYT (Tue/Thu highest)
- Facebook: Best 1pm, 8pm MYT (Wed/Fri highest)
- TikTok: Best 7pm-9pm MYT (daily, weekends stronger)
