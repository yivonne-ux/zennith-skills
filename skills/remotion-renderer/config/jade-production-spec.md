## TEXT OVERLAY RULES

- TEXT OVERLAYS ARE MANDATORY (most viewers watch muted)
- Key phrases in *emphasis* (gold/accent color)
- Position: lower_third (540px from bottom on 1920h)
- Font: serif for headings, clean sans for body
- Colors: white text, gold accent (#C9B037), jade green (#00A86B)

## SOUND RULES

- Card reveal: beat drop or chime SFX (never silent)
- Background: warm ambient / lo-fi / guzheng / singing bowl
- ASMR elements: card shuffle, tea pour, crystal placement
- First 3 seconds: strong voiceover OR attention-grabbing music
- AVOID: heavy bass, EDM, generic spa music, horror/suspense
