## 8. Funnel Content Matrix

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | CTA | Price Point | Jade Outfit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **TOFU** (Awareness) | Reading demo reels, relatable spiritual content, birth year hooks | "Follow for daily guidance" | Free | Casual: tank + jeans |
| **MOFU** (Interest) | $1 intro reading promo, educational carousel, QMDJ explainers | "Get your $1 reading" | $1 | Smart casual: wrap top |
| **BOFU** (Decision) | Full reading testimonials, transformation stories, comparison content | "Book your full reading" | $29-97 | Professional: black v-neck |
| **Retention** | Mentorship value content, exclusive readings, behind-the-scenes | "Join the inner circle" | $497 | Intimate: silk cami, home |

### Content Ratio
- TOFU: 60% (builds audience)
- MOFU: 25% (converts followers to buyers)
- BOFU: 10% (converts buyers to high-ticket)
- Retention: 5% (retains whales)
