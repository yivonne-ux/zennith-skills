## 12. Character Evolution Log

This history informs all future character creation decisions. The journey from fantasy CG to warm editorial Korean woman took 4 days and ~15 iterations.

### Key Lessons from the Journey

1. **CG fantasy does NOT convert for personal services.** People buy readings from someone they trust. Trust requires approachability.
2. **Photorealism is non-negotiable.** The moment we pivoted from CG to "real person on iPhone," everything clicked.
3. **Korean beauty aesthetic hits the sweet spot** — aspirational but relatable, warm but polished.
4. **The jade pendant** anchors brand identity across all images. Without it, Jade is just another Korean woman.
5. **Male characters don't work** for psychic/spiritual brands targeting women. Master Wei Lian was abandoned immediately.
6. **Purple/cosmic = generic.** Every competitor uses this palette. Warm earth tones differentiate Jade.
7. **Western settings, Eastern wisdom.** The brand lives in NYC/LA, not in a temple. Spirituality is woven into modern life.
