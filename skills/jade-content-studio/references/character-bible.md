### 2.5 Brand Visual Rules

- **PHOTOREALISTIC** — like a real person's Instagram, NOT editorial, NOT cinematic, NOT CG
- iPhone/mirrorless candid quality, natural depth of field
- Natural light: golden hour, candlelight, morning sun. NO flash, NO studio strobe
- Warm tones, intimate mood — like a friend's Instagram, not a magazine
- Real skin texture with pores (but K-beauty smooth, not gritty)
- 35mm or 50mm f/1.8, shallow depth of field, eye-level or slightly above
- Western city settings ONLY — NYC/LA/Melbourne lifestyle
- Korean beauty editorial meets cozy home studio
