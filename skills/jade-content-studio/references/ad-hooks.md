## 7. Ad Hook Generation

Proven hooks from content pipeline, scored by engagement potential.

### 7.1 Winning Hooks (Production-Tested)

| Hook | Platform | Score | Why It Works |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| "Your tarot reader can't do math" | TikTok | 8.8/10 | Pattern interrupt, positions QMDJ as superior |
| "12 signs vs. 1,080 gates" | IG Carousel | 9.6/10 | Specificity, creates curiosity gap |
| "23,847 women started with a $1 question..." | Meta Ad | 9.0/10 | Oddly specific number reads as real data |
| "Your birth year reveals your hidden power" | TikTok | 8.5/10 | Personalization, curiosity |
| "I stopped reading tarot. Here's why." | Reels | 8.3/10 | Controversy, unexpected pivot |

### 7.2 Hook Engineering Rules

1. **Oddly specific numbers > round numbers** — "23,847" reads as real data, "thousands" reads as BS
2. **Question formats outperform statements** for curiosity gap
3. **Comparison hooks** (old way vs Jade way) convert best on carousels
4. **First 3 seconds = make or break** — hook must land in 3 seconds for video
5. **Pattern interrupt > gentle intro** — stop the scroll, then explain
6. **Specificity = credibility** — "1,080 gates" beats "many combinations"
7. **Birth year hooks** are the #1 engagement driver — always triggers comments

### 7.3 Hook Templates

**Comparison (Carousel):**
```
[OLD THING] gives you [WEAK RESULT].
[JADE'S THING] gives you [STRONG RESULT].
Here's the difference in [TIME_PERIOD]...
```

**Pattern Interrupt (Video):**
```
"Your [TRUSTED THING] can't do [UNEXPECTED SKILL]."
[PAUSE — let confusion land]
"But [JADE'S METHOD] can. Here's how..."
```

**Oddly Specific Number (Ad):**
```
"[SPECIFIC_NUMBER] women started with a $1 question last month.
[FRACTION] of them booked a full reading within 48 hours.
Here's what they found out about [BIRTH_YEAR]..."
```

**Birth Year (TikTok):**
```
"If you were born in [YEAR], stop scrolling.
The next 90 days are about to change EVERYTHING for you.
Your Qi Men chart shows [SPECIFIC PREDICTION]..."
```

### 7.4 Script Formula (Psychic Samira Pattern, Adapted)

```
0-3s:   HOOK — pattern interrupt, question, bold claim
3-8s:   PAIN POINT — "You've been relying on [generic method] and wondering why..."
8-15s:  TEASE — "What if I told you your birth year alone reveals..."
15-45s: VALUE — actual QMDJ insight, mini reading, specific prediction
45-60s: CTA — "Comment your birth year" / "Link in bio for your $1 reading"
```
