## The 10 Laws of World-Class Brand Design

1. **Simplicity wins longevity.** The simpler the mark, the longer it endures.
2. **Consistency builds recognition.** Brand recognition increases up to 80% with consistent color.
3. **Restraint signals luxury.** White space, limited palette, fewer elements.
4. **The eye rules, not the ruler.** Optical alignment overrides mathematical alignment.
5. **Systems beat artifacts.** A logo is not a brand. A system IS a brand.
6. **Strategy precedes aesthetics.** Every visual decision must answer "why?" first.
7. **Test in the worst case first.** Black and white before color. Smallest size before largest.
8. **The product IS the brand.** For the greatest brands, product experience outweighs graphic identity.
9. **Craft is in the details.** Kerning, overshoot, ink traps, clear space -- the invisible work.
10. **Know the rules, then break them intentionally.**
