# Identity Gimmick Promo — Creative Method

## What It Is

Same offer for everyone, but identity-based hooks make each person feel it's THEIR special deal. Not a discount strategy — it's a **pattern interrupt + personalization** strategy.

## Why It Works

1. **Pattern interrupt** — "TNG users special" stops scroll because it feels targeted
2. **Personalization** — "IC ending 168" makes viewer think "that's ME"
3. **FOMO** — "Only for [identity]" creates exclusivity (even though everyone gets it)
4. **Andromeda diversity** — 10 identity hooks = 10 visually distinct Entity IDs = 10 auction tickets
5. **Proven** — Pinxin TNG-PROMO: RM33 CPA, 5 purchases in 1 day, 4.78x ROAS

## When to Use

- Brand has a standing offer/promo (buy X free X, discount, bundle)
- Need to scale ad volume without creative fatigue
- Current ads are all "same look" and Andromeda is penalizing
- Want to test which audience segment responds best

## The Formula

```
HOOK: [Identity group] 专属优惠 / [Identity] Special
BODY: Same product benefits + same offer
CTA:  Same landing page / WhatsApp
```

## Identity Categories (pick 8-12 per brand)

### 1. Payment Method
- TNG users, GrabPay users, credit card users, online banking users

### 2. Personal Numbers
- IC ending in 8/6/168, phone number ending in lucky numbers
- Birthday month (April babies 🎂)

### 3. Life Stage
- Busy moms, working professionals, retirees, newlyweds
- Students, first-time homeowners

### 4. Geographic
- KL residents, Penang people, JB locals, Selangor families
- "住在 [area] 的你"

### 5. Behavior
- Grab Food users ("放下Grab"), cooking haters, meal prep lovers
- Online shoppers, health conscious

### 6. Cultural/Religious
- 初一十五 families, vegetarian since birth, recent converts
- Festival-specific (CNY, Raya, Deepavali)

### 7. Health
- 三高 diagnosed, cholesterol watchers, low sodium seekers
- Fitness enthusiasts, weight management

### 8. Timing
- Payday special ("刚出粮？")
- Weekend only, lunch special, midnight snack

### 9. Relationship
- "帮妈妈买的", "送给阿嬷", "surprise for wife"
- Family size (2人份, 全家人)

### 10. Experience
- First-time buyers ("第一次试")
- Repeat customers ("又来回购")
- Referred by friend ("朋友推荐来的")

## Production Process

1. Pick 8-10 identity hooks for the brand
2. Write copy: same body, different hook headline
3. Visual: same product photo/layout, different hook text
4. Each hook = 1 ad = 1 unique Andromeda Entity ID
5. Launch all at once — let CBO/Lowest Cost find the best hook
6. Kill bottom 50% at Day 7, create 5 new hooks
7. Repeat weekly

## Copy Template (Chinese)

```
Hook: [Identity] 专属优惠来了！

Body:
品馨懒人包
[Key benefit 1]
[Key benefit 2]
[Key benefit 3]

[Offer: 买X送X]
[Price: 一盒从RMX++]
[Urgency: 限时/限量]

📲 [CTA]
```

## Measurement

- Compare CPA across identity hooks — find which identity resonates most
- Winner identity = your core audience signal (feed back into targeting)
- Best hooks often reveal audience insights you didn't know
