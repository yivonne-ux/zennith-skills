# Prompt DNA Blocks — Yivonne's Proven System (18/18 success rate)

Include these blocks in EVERY NanoBanana prompt. Proven across Mirra, Bloom & Bare, Pinxin, Jade Oracle.

---

## 7 Universal Blocks (REQUIRED in every prompt)

### 1. ANTI_RENDER
Prevents NANO from rendering hex codes, font names, pixel values, or technical terms as visible text in the image. Only text in quotes should be rendered.

**Include:** `Do NOT render any hex codes, font names, pixel measurements, or technical terms as visible text. Only render text that appears inside quotation marks in this prompt.`

### 2. LOGO_SAFE
Top 12-15% of canvas must be completely clear for logo placement in post-processing.

**Include:** `The top 12-15% of the canvas MUST be completely clear — no text, no illustrations, no elements. This is the logo safe zone.`

### 3. GRID_SAFE
Most important visual element centered horizontally. Nothing critical in outer 32px left/right (IG crops these in profile grid view).

**Include:** `Center the most important visual element horizontally. Keep all critical content within the center 1015px of the 1080px canvas — nothing important in the outer 32px on each side.`

### 4. FONT_DNA
Describe the brand's typography in SENSORY terms, never by font name (NANO can't use real fonts). Reference real-world analogues.

**Examples by brand:**
- Mirra: `Typography is elegant and warm — thin serif headlines with dramatic thick-thin stroke variation like a luxury magazine masthead. Body text is clean geometric sans-serif, small but perfectly spaced.`
- Jade Oracle: `Typography is mystical and refined — flowing serif with gold accents, like an ancient scroll meets modern editorial. Clean and spacious.`
- Luna: `Typography is effortlessly cool — mixed serif headings with handwritten italic accents, like a vintage bookshop meets indie magazine.`
- Pinxin: `Typography is warm and grounded — friendly rounded sans-serif headlines, comfortable and approachable like a neighborhood café menu board.`

### 5. TYPE_MASTER
Controls text size hierarchy and spacing.

**Include:** `Headlines are MASSIVE — filling 40-60% of the width. Tight line spacing on display text. Comfortable spacing on body text. ALL CAPS headings use wide letter-spacing. Maximum 2 typefaces and 3 size levels in the entire design. The text hierarchy must be readable in 3 seconds.`

### 6. LAYOUT_MASTER
Controls composition and breathing space.

**Include:** `[RATIO — e.g., Portrait 4:5 (1080x1350) for feed, or 9:16 (1080x1920) for stories/reels/carousels]. Generous margins of at least 80px on all sides. 40-60% of the canvas should be breathing space. Maximum 4-5 visual elements total. ONE clear focal point. The design must communicate its message in a 3-second glance.`

### 7. COLOR_MASTER
Controls palette discipline.

**Include:** `Maximum 2-3 colors plus background color and text color. Follow the 60-30-10 rule: 60% dominant color (usually background), 30% secondary (usually accent), 10% pop (usually CTA or highlight). Colors should feel [BRAND ADJECTIVE — e.g., chalky and Scandinavian for Bloom & Bare, warm and editorial for Mirra, mystical and jade-toned for Jade Oracle].`

## 6 Category-Specific Blocks (use based on content type)

### 8. MASCOT_HERO_DNA
For character-focused posts where mascot/illustration is the star.

**Include:** `The character fills 60-80% of the canvas. Solid flat background color — ZERO patterns, ZERO textures, ZERO gradients in the background. ONE headline only. Maximum negative space around the character. This should look like it was produced by a $50K creative agency.`

### 9. CHARACTER_DNA
For any character/illustration designs.

**Include:** `Characters can be any type — mascots, abstract shapes, imaginary creatures, simple character illustrations. What MUST be consistent: the brand's color palette, simple distinctive facial features, bold geometric shapes, flat colors with subtle texture, premium quality rendering. NOT realistic humans. NOT clip-art. NOT generic.`

### 10. COMIC_DNA
For comic/panel-style content.

**Include:** `Clean panels with thin dividers. Cream-colored frame border. Captions positioned above or below panels — NOT cluttered speech bubbles. Risograph printing texture quality — tactile, handmade, editorial. Simple stylized characters. The overall feel should be editorial magazine quality, not webcomic quality.`

### 11. MEME_DNA
For meme/humor content.

**Include:** `Internet-native humor but executed at agency level. Brand illustration style — NOT stock imagery. Clean intentional typography — NOT Impact font or default meme fonts. The joke must land in under 2 seconds of viewing. Premium execution of a casual concept.`

### 12. ILLUSTRATED_DNA
For editorial illustration designs.

**Include:** `Editorial illustration quality — like a feature piece in The New Yorker or Monocle Magazine. Risograph or woodblock printing texture. Limited color palette (3-4 colors max). Bold geometric shapes as the structural foundation. The illustration is the hero — filling 60-70% of the canvas. ONE headline integrated naturally into the composition.`

### 13. PHOTO_DNA
For designs featuring real photography.

**Include:** `SACRED RULE: The photograph must be kept EXACTLY as-is — full bleed, no cropping, no color manipulation by AI. The photo IS the design. Overlay elements (text, brand mark) should be in a semi-transparent band or clean bar — NEVER floating over the photo's subject. The photo's original color temperature and mood are the design's mood.`

## The 8 DNA Qualities (editorial vs amateur)

Every image must demonstrate ALL 8:

1. **Directional Lighting** — Soft warm light from a specific direction with real shadows
2. **Camera Craft** — 30-45 degree angle, shallow DOF, 85mm f/1.4 lens compression feel
3. **Rich Background Color** — Never pure white/clinical. Warm tones, texture, depth
4. **Premium Props** — 2-3 lifestyle props creating editorial context (not empty space)
5. **Surface Texture / Two-Tone Depth** — Colored wall meeting cream surface, material variety
6. **World-Class Typography** — High-contrast serif with dramatic thick/thin variation
7. **Shadows & Dimension** — Every object casts soft shadow, slight elevation, depth
8. **Radical Simplicity** — If you can remove something and it still works, REMOVE IT
