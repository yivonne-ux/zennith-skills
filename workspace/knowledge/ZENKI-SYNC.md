## What Zenki Learned (Feedback)
- 1688.com requires CAPTCHA bypass — tw.1688.com works better for scraping
- Use Chinese search terms (already in MASTER-70-PRODUCTS.csv column "1688 Search Term")
- Alibaba International has better English support but higher prices
- 1688 domestic prices are 30-70% cheaper than Alibaba International
- Best 1688 product categories: jewelry, beauty tools, health devices (well-established supply chains)
- Supplements/custom formulas: better sourced via Alibaba International (need certs for export)
