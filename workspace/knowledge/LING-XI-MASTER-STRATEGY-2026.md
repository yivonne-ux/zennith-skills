## PART 11: KEY STRATEGIC PRINCIPLES

1. **QMDJ + BaZi + 数字 = the moat.** Nobody else combines all three. Protect this positioning.

2. **Diaspora-first, China-never.** China banned AI fortune-telling. Serve Chinese speakers globally from Malaysia. Safer and more profitable.

3. **English commands premium.** Joey Yap charges RM238 English vs RM188 Chinese. FateTell charges $39.99 to Western users. Lead with English for higher ARPU.

4. **Baby naming is the cash cow.** Highest emotional urgency, highest margin, highest referral potential, natural repeat (second child, nieces, nephews).

5. **CNY is Black Friday.** Build entire marketing calendar around Chinese New Year. Annual forecast is the gateway product. Campaign starts November, peaks January-February.

6. **AI for scale, human for soul.** 80% AI-generated, 20% human review + personal audio note. 10x scalable, 10x warmer than pure AI.

7. **WhatsApp-first delivery (SEA).** Malaysia runs on WhatsApp. Deliver via WhatsApp messages + voice notes + PDF attachment. This alone differentiates from every AI-only competitor.

8. **Never make harmful predictions.** The #1 trust-killer is specific negative forecasts ("diabetes at 48"). Frame challenges as "awareness points" with remedies. Always end with hope.

9. **Show the methodology.** Users hate "conclusions without reasoning." Show how you arrived at insights. Transparency builds trust.

10. **Content-led growth.** Celebrity BaZi analysis is the #1 growth hack. Zodiac forecasts drive seasonal spikes. Day Master personality content is shareable.
