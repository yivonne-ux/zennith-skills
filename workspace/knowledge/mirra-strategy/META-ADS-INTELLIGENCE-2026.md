## 1. ANDROMEDA — Meta's New Ad Delivery Engine (Global since Oct 2025)

**How it works now:**
- Andromeda works in REVERSE from the old system
- Instead of starting with advertiser-defined audiences, it evaluates your CREATIVE first using computer vision and AI audio analysis, then predicts which users will engage
- Scans millions of ads and picks ~1,000 candidates per user impression
- Assigns an **Entity ID** (semantic fingerprint) to each ad based on visual pattern

**Entity ID system (CRITICAL):**
- If you upload 30 ads that look too similar (same background, same style), they get the SAME Entity ID and are treated as ONE ad
- Similarity score above 60% triggers retrieval suppression
- Aim for fewer than 40% shared visual/audio features between ads
- 8-20 genuinely different creative concepts outperform traditional A/B testing

**Performance impact:** 20-35% higher ROAS for advertisers following Andromeda best practices

## 4. LEARNING PHASE — Official Rules

### Exit Requirements
- 50 conversions per ad set per week (some types now 25)
- Typically 7-14 days to exit

### Budget Formula
```
Minimum daily budget = (Target CPA × 50) / 7
```
Example: CPA RM10 → minimum RM71/day per ad set

### Significant Edit Triggers (RESETS learning)
- Budget change >20% in either direction
- Any audience modification
- Switching optimization event
- Adding/removing creative
- Changing bid strategy
- Pausing for 7+ days

### Rules During Learning
1. Do NOT touch anything for 7 days
2. Budget scaling: max 20-30% every 24-48 hours
3. Batch edits — 5 small edits = 5 resets; 5 at once = 1 reset
4. Check weekly, not daily

## 5. CREATIVE — The New Targeting

**Creative quality = 50%+ of Meta Ads performance in 2026**

### Volume Requirements
| Daily Budget | Recommended Active Creatives |
|-------------|------------------------------|
| Under $300/day | 10-15 creatives |
| $300-$1,000/day | 20-30 creatives |
| Above $1,000/day | 30-50+ creatives |

### Creative Fatigue
- Sets in within 7-14 days (down from weeks/months)
- Signal: frequency >2-3 for cold audiences + CTR decline
- Refresh every 2-3 weeks
- 90% of Meta inventory is now vertical (9:16)

### 3:2:2 Testing Method (Charley Tichenor, $500M+ spent)
- 3 Creatives × 2 Primary Texts × 2 Headlines = 12 combinations
- Use Dynamic Creative to test
- Test creative concepts, NOT audiences

### Ben Heath's Diversity Framework
- Different formats (static, video, carousel)
- Different angles (each = different reason to buy)
- Different tones and archetypes (radically different, not variations)

## 7. CTWA / WhatsApp Ads
- 3x conversion rates vs landing pages
- 90-98% open rates
- CAPI now tracks CTWA conversions (Pixel CANNOT track in-chat)
- General-purpose chatbots BANNED since January 2026
- Optimize: Engagement objective → Conversations
- Audience: broad targeting, 2-10M size
- Minimum daily budget: 10x CPC
- Forrester/Meta study: 94% lift in conversion rates, 92% drop in CPL

## 8. CAPI IS NON-NEGOTIABLE
- Pixel-only setups miss over 60% of actual conversions
- Meta now requires dual tracking (Pixel + CAPI)
- For CTWA: CAPI is the ONLY solution for tracking in-chat conversions
- Without CAPI, Meta cannot optimize for actual sales — only conversations

## 9. CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE — 2026 Expert Consensus

## 10. RAYA / HOLIDAY STRATEGY — MALAYSIA SPECIFIC

### CPM During Raya
- CPC ~125% increase during Raya vs off-season (RM1.80 vs RM0.80)
- CPM range MY: RM8-RM50, festive periods push high end
- BUT: spike concentrated in Malay-audience segments

### Chinese-Malaysian Audience (Mirra's Advantage)
- NOT in balik kampung mode — home for 4-day weekend
- More time on phones, less targeted by Raya ad flood
- Mirra's non-Raya ads may see LOWER competition and cheaper delivery

### What Successful Advertisers Do
- Never fully pause — 20% reduction maximum
- Non-Raya brands treat it as low-competition opportunity
- Days 1-2: presence/soft messaging
- Day 3+: resume conversion campaigns
- Post-Raya (Day 5-7): "diet reset" / back-to-routine messaging

### Post-Raya Window (Mar 24-26)
- High-intent period — people return to routine, need meals
- "Diet reset" aligned with Mirra's low-cal positioning
- Historically low-CPM, ideal for testing new creatives
- 84% of Malaysians increase spending during Ramadan, 34% use food delivery

## 11. MEAL SUBSCRIPTION SPECIFIC

- Scale in 20% steps: RM1,500 → RM1,750 (wait 4 days) → RM2,000

- Meta favors consistent daily spending over irregular bursts
- $30/day for 30 days beats $300 for one weekend
