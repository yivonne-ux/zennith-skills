## 5. TEMPLATE SYSTEM — The "M" and "B" Naming Convention

### Decoded naming: `M{batch}{variant}-{funnel}-{lang}-{persona}-T{template}-{format}-{hash}`

**Example**: `M3B-MOFU-en-Office Girls–T082-Img-20170a88`
- M3 = Batch 3 (production wave)
- B = Variant B within that batch
- MOFU = Middle funnel
- en = English
- Office Girls = Persona
- T082 = Template reference number
- Img = Static image

### Template Numbers Referenced (T001–T130+):
Most frequently used templates: T008, T025, T033, T034, T049, T050, T056, T062, T069, T070, T071, T079, T082, T084, T085, T105, T106, T108, T109, T110, T111, T115, T116, T117, T121, T125, T128, T130

This is a **130+ template library** with systematic persona × template × language matrix production. Each production wave (M1, M2, M3, M4, B2, B4) covers:
- 3-5 personas per wave
- 4-6 templates per persona
- 2 languages (EN/CN) where applicable
- Multiple variants (A, B, C, D, E, F)

**This is industrial-scale creative production.** A single wave can produce 50-100 ad variants.

## 6. ACCA FRAMEWORK — How Mirra's Funnel Actually Works

### Awareness (TOFU)
- **Format**: Girlboss quotes, lifestyle vibes, memes, self-care content
- **Voice**: Empowerment, aspiration, relatability
- **Examples**: "Dont be a lady, be a legend", "Loving yourself is a rebellious act", "Divas dont do drama, we do business", "Podcasts for Girl bosses"
- **Platform**: Instagram + Facebook organic
- **Purpose**: Build brand universe. NOT selling. Building the tribe of women who identify with Mirra's values.
- **No product mention.** This is pure identity content.

### Consideration (MOFU) — THE MAIN BATTLEFIELD
- **Format**: KOL reviews, persona-based static ads (M-batches), "what I eat in a week", Animation, Carousel
- **Voice**: First-person, specific pain point, solution-aware
- **Copy Structure**:
  1. **Hook** = specific pain ("Sitting 8 hours? Your belly knows.")
  2. **Agitate** = consequences ("By 3pm you're bloated, sluggish, reaching for snacks")
  3. **Solution** = Mirra introduction ("Low-cal bento, delivered to your office")
  4. **Proof** = KOL/testimonial ("Lost 5kg in my first month")
- **KOL is the primary proof mechanism** — 30+ KOLs, each with multiple cuts
- **Template system** enables massive A/B testing at this stage

### Conversion (BOFU)
- **Format**: Price-driven statics, Sales Boom Boom compilations, Raw ads, countdown urgency
- **Voice**: Direct, urgent, deal-focused
- **Copy Structure**:
  1. **Offer** = RM17++/meal, free delivery, group discounts
  2. **Urgency** = "Last 12 slots", "倒数48小时", "8 spots left"
  3. **Social Proof** = compilation of happy customers/KOLs
  4. **CTA** = WhatsApp direct (CTWA)
- **"Sales Boom Boom"** is the conversion engine — high-energy montage of food + testimonials + price

### Advocacy (Post-Purchase)
- **Format**: Testimonials, reply SOP images, menu updates, weekly dish photos
- **Channels**: WhatsApp groups, Facebook page
- **Purpose**: Retention + referral ("Order with your Jimui, save up to RM60!")
- **Menu updates** are a retention mechanism — weekly/biweekly new dishes keep subscribers engaged

## 13. WHAT DIES / WHAT LIVES — Kill Patterns

### Concepts that stopped after 1-2 appearances (killed):
- Beauty Tea content (Oct 2024) — pivot to tea didn't stick
- Quinoa-specific content — too ingredient-focused
- Generic health education ("10 detox teas", "benefit of quinoa")
- Weekend vibe / afterwork chill lifestyle (too vague for conversion)
- Ingredient close-up carousels

### Concepts that get CONTINUOUS investment (winners):
- Sales Boom Boom (12+ months running)
- KOL compilations (12+ months)
- "From L to S" / size transformation hooks
- RM17++ price anchor
- Raw/lo-fi ad formats
- Persona × template matrix system
- Group ordering discount ("闺蜜一起订")
- Free delivery messaging

### The Pattern:
**Winners are specific, emotional, and format-flexible.** They can be adapted to new personas, languages, and formats while keeping the core hook. Generic educational content dies. Lifestyle vibes die. But "I went from XL to M eating Mirra bentos" runs forever because it's a specific, believable, emotional transformation story.

## 15. KEY STRATEGIC INSIGHTS FOR NEXT CAMPAIGN

### What the data says to DO:
1. **Double down on persona-specific targeting** — the more specific the persona, the more variants produced (= the more it works)
2. **Sales Boom Boom is immortal** — keep iterating, never kill it
3. **Raw/lo-fi formats are the growth vector** — whiteboard, holding paper, boomerang, phone gallery
4. **KOL is MOFU's backbone** — maintain 30+ KOL relationships, 3 cuts per KOL
5. **CN BOFU is underserved vs production volume** — CN production is 60% but the ad account doesn't reflect this
6. **Group ordering hooks work** — "闺蜜一起订" / "Order with besties" consistently produced
7. **Named character personas (小雅, 静静) outperform generic personas** — make them real people, not demographic segments
8. **Wedding/event-specific campaigns = seasonal bursts** — pre-plan 6 weeks ahead
9. **"No more stress" concept has legs** — 5 versions means it's being optimized as a winner
10. **Animation is the newest format bet** — Oct 2025 production spike suggests this is testing well

### What the data says to STOP:
1. Generic educational content (ingredient spotlights, nutrition facts)
2. Vague lifestyle TOFU for paid ads (save for organic only)
3. Over-polished brand content (the shift to "Raw" is intentional and data-backed)
4. Single-version concepts (every winner needs 3+ iterations)
5. Ignoring WhatsApp-first CTA (WhatsApp = 82% of orders)

### What's MISSING (gaps to fill):
1. **Carousel ads** — research shows -30-50% CPA but only a few Album/carousel entries in production
2. **Advertorial/editorial format** — Trifecta's 13.2x ROAS format not yet attempted
3. **Before/after transformation stories** — only cat meme version exists, need real customer transformations
4. **Calculator/comparison interactive** — screenshot ad format research shows 42% more effective
5. **AG1-style long-form copy** — benefit-first → bulleted outcomes → social proof → offer → CTA template not yet applied
6. **WhatsApp conversation flow optimization** — CTWA is primary channel but auto-greeting/follow-up sequences not documented in production
