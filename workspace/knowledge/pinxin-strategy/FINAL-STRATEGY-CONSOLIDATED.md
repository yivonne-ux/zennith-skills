### Cross-check Formula (verify CAPI accuracy)
```
Meta says: X purchases at RM Y ROAS
Shopify says: Z orders at RM W revenue

If X ≈ Z (±15%): CAPI is accurate ✅
If X >> Z: CAPI over-counting (dedup issue) ⚠️
If X << Z: CAPI missing events ⚠️
```
