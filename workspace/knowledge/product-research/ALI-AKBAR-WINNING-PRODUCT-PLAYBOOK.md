## 1. PRODUCT RESEARCH METHOD

### Free Method
- Go to **Facebook Ad Library** → All Ads → search **"free shipping"**
- Scroll through active ads to find dropshipping stores you could model
- Slower, but free

### Paid Method (What Ali Actually Uses)
- **WinningHunter** → Store Ads → Meta Ads Library
- **Key indicator:** Sort by businesses running the MOST ads
- High ad count = first signal that gets him to investigate further
- Scroll through 5-6 rows — look for what catches your eye
- His exact quote: *"My main indicator of success is I'm going to look at this top line — I'm going to try and see what businesses are running the most amount of ads"*

### What Catches His Eye
- **162+ ads running** = massive validation signal
- All ads going after **ONE particular product** (not a general store)
- **AI ads being used** = you can enter without UGC/influencers
- Branded single-product store > general store (always)

| 10 | **AI ads usage** | Are they using AI-generated ads? = low barrier for you to enter | All UGC/influencer = higher barrier |

### His Revenue Rule of Thumb
> **Number of active ads × $1,000 = estimated monthly revenue**
> - 54 active ads = ~$54K/month
> - 162 total ads seen = heavy spender, deeply validated

### Sourcing Validation
- Screenshot the product → reverse image search on AliExpress
- Check for **US warehouse** options (2-3 day delivery)
- If no US warehouse, regular AliExpress shipping ~$10 with fast delivery
- **$10 source → $70 sell = validated margin**

## 3. THE "NICHE > PRODUCT" PHILOSOPHY

This is Ali's #1 insight from the $800K/month ring business:

### Core Principle
> **"The focus of your marketing should be on the PERSON, not on the product. Because you can sell them anything, but once you focus in on WHO you want to sell to, that's when everything changes."**

### The Ring Example
- Product: Acumagnetic ring with magnets. Source cost: **$1**. Sell price: **$39.99**
- What beginners see: "Just a ring" → they fail
- What Ali sees: "A solution for women going through menopause" → $800K/month

### Key Insights
- **Don't sell products, sell solutions to problems**
- If you think "it's just a [product]" you will FAIL
- **Own the solution** in the customer's mind — they won't even check Amazon
- Jewelry/personal/fashion items = people DON'T comparison shop
- Once you own a niche, you can sell them rings, supplements, gadgets, digital products — ANYTHING
- *"In the mind of the customers, you're the only person providing this solution"*

### Why Personal/Fashion Products Win
- People buy emotionally, not rationally
- They don't think "I'll find this cheaper on Amazon"
- They think "I don't want to lose this page, it's only $39.99, I want to try it"
- Ali's own story: *"The first real money I made was in the jewelry niche because it's so personal"*

## 4. CUSTOMER AVATAR DEEP RESEARCH

### Build a Named Avatar
Ali literally names his customer and creates a full profile:

**Example — "Linda" (menopause ring):**
- Mid-career professional (teacher, nurse, manager, entrepreneur)
- Core pain points: hot flashes, mood swings, weight gain, loss of confidence
- Psychological driver: mistrust in pharma
- Wants: safe, fashionable, non-pharmaceutical solution

### Research Sources (His Exact Process)
1. **Amazon reviews** — Read 5-star, 3-star, and 1-star reviews
   - 5-star = what people LOVE (use in ad copy)
   - 3-star = what's MISSING (your product fills this gap)
   - 1-star = what people HATE (your product solves this)
2. **TikTok scrolling** — Type niche name, scroll endlessly
   - Pick up one-word key phrases, sentences, emotional triggers
   - *"You're going to pick up on things you didn't even know"*
   - **Write everything down** — these become your ad angles
3. **Customer testimonials** — Read what real users say
4. **Videos of your target audience** — Watch them going through the problem/suffering

### Why This Matters Now
> *"With Facebook's new Andromeda Meta update, it's leveled the playing field. The person that can come in with the BEST ANGLE now wins. Not the person that can tinker with settings and flood Facebook with variations."*

## 5. AD ANGLE CREATION

### The Winning Formula
- Core pain point → Ad angle that speaks to that pain point → Collect traffic
- **Best angle wins** (not most variations, not most budget)
- Connect EACH pain point to a specific ad angle

### Examples from $800K Ring Business

| Pain Point | Ad Angle | Ad Copy |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Age anxiety | Call out demographic | "At 50, my menopause symptoms were taking over until I discovered this" |
| Anxiety | Emotional hook | "Say goodbye to anxiety this Halloween" |
| Seasonal | Holiday alignment | "Boo. Menopause symptoms are creeping in" |
| Weight | Bold promise | "Lose 30 lbs in 90 days or your money back" (seasoned accounts only!) |
| Stubborn fat | Relatable slang | "FUPA killer" |
| Hot flashes | Vivid imagery | Photo of sweat imprint on pillow + story of hot flashes |
| Daily stress | Empowerment | "Why women over 40 are finally fighting back against daily stress" |

### Safe vs. Bold Claims
- **Beginners:** Use softer language ("go down 3 jean sizes" vs "lose 30 lbs")
- **Seasoned ad accounts:** Can push harder claims without getting banned
- Same message, different packaging — always find a compliant way to say it

### Fashion + Medical Positioning
- The ring page combines fashion aesthetics with medical credibility
- Clinically backed + doctor recommendations + "us vs them" + FAQ
- This combo builds both desire AND trust

## 6. AI AD CREATION SYSTEM

### Step-by-Step (from the Nebulizer Video)

**Step 1: Harvest Reviews**
- Find the product (or similar) on Amazon
- Copy-paste **5-star reviews** into ChatGPT
- Copy-paste **3-star reviews** into ChatGPT
- Copy-paste **1-star reviews** into ChatGPT

**Step 2: The ChatGPT Prompt**
> *"Act as a direct response copywriter who's learned from the hands of Dan Kennedy and Gary Halbert and write the ad for me."*
>
> Then add:
> - Use information from the reviews above
> - 5-star reviews = what people are happy with
> - 3-star reviews = what's missing
> - 1-star reviews = what people were unhappy with
> - Our product solves all those problems
> - We are primarily targeting [your avatar]
> - Keep the script to 1-2 minutes max

**Step 3: Rewrite as First-Person Story**
- Ask ChatGPT to rewrite as a first-person story
- This will be read by your AI avatar

**Step 4: Generate AI Video Ad (TopView.ai)**
- Go to TopView.ai → Dashboard
- Select avatar (doctor, mom, professional — match your niche)
- Upload product image → merge with avatar holding product
- Paste your first-person story script
- Select voice → generate

**Step 5: THE KEY TRICK — Layer Clips Over AI Avatar**
> *"If you show the AI avatar for too long, it will look very fake. So what I do is: AI avatar as the BASE LAYER talking through the story, then every other second I layer on a clip."*
- Base: AI avatar reading the story
- Overlay: Real clips (from TikTok, AI-generated, product demos) every 1-2 seconds
- Result: AI avatar isn't visible long enough for anyone to think it's AI
- This is how he creates **UNLIMITED** ad variations

**Step 6: Generate More Angles**
- Go back to ChatGPT → generate more ad angle scripts
- Have AI avatar read each new angle
- Layer clips over each one
- Result: Unlimited AI ads for launch

## 7. STORE BUILDING (MODEL, DON'T REINVENT)

### His Core Philosophy
> *"If this page has made them over six figures a month in sales, I'm going to build a very similar page for myself that's also going to make me six figures. I'm not going to overcomplicate this."*

### The Process
1. **Clone the structure** — Put text where text goes, images where images go
2. **Match the layout** section by section — don't redesign
3. **Swap branding** — Different colors, your brand name, your images
4. **Generate AI images** — Use ChatGPT/AI image tools to create product images in YOUR branding
5. **Change copy** — Same messaging structure, your words

### Winning Page Structure (What Converts)
From the $800K ring page:
1. Hero image + product name
2. Main positioning statement + benefits
3. How it works (visual)
4. 4 main benefit points
5. Clinically backed / doctor recommendations
6. Us vs. Them comparison
7. FAQ section
8. Bundles + testimonials

### Tools
- **ShopFunnels** — His funnel/store builder (integrates with Shopify)
- **ChatGPT** — Image generation for product shots in your branding
- ShopFunnels AI Shop → Essential Image Generator

## 10. THE "SMELL MONEY" SIGNALS

### Compiled from Both Videos — The Full Checklist

**STRONG BUY signals (product is a winner):**
- [ ] **High ad count** (54+ active, 162+ total = heavily validated)
- [ ] **Single product focus** (branded store, not general store)
- [ ] **Months of consistent ads** for the same product
- [ ] **AI ads being used** (you can enter without UGC budget)
- [ ] **$1-15 source → $40-70+ sell** (massive margins)
- [ ] **Niche with emotional/personal problems** (menopause, allergies, pet love)
- [ ] **Product not easily found on Amazon** (fashion, personal, novel solution)
- [ ] **US/UK/AU targeting** (proven dropship-friendly markets)
- [ ] **Professional landing page** (they've invested in conversion)
- [ ] **Room for improvement** in their images/copy (you can do better)
- [ ] **Seasonal angle available** (holidays, allergy season, back-to-school)
- [ ] **Revenue tracker shows $100K+/month**

**BONUS signals:**
- [ ] They "own" a new solution/category (acumagnetic, portable nebulizer for babies)
- [ ] Product is fashion-forward or wearable (no Amazon comparison shopping)
- [ ] Multiple ad angles running simultaneously (testing = they're scaling)
- [ ] Mix of active + inactive ads (healthy testing cycle)
- [ ] They sell to a niche where customers are emotionally invested

### The Anti-Signals (AVOID)
- General stores with 100+ random products
- Products easily found cheaper on Amazon
- No active ads or very few (<10)
- Only been running ads for <2 weeks
- Commodity products with no emotional hook
- Can't source reliably on AliExpress
- No US warehouse option for US market
