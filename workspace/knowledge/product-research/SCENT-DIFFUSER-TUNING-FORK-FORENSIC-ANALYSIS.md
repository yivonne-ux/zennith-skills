## 3. MATERIAL DEEP DIVE

### 316L Stainless Steel (RECOMMENDED PRIMARY MATERIAL)

- **Composition:** Iron, chromium (16-18%), nickel (10-14%), molybdenum (2-3%)
- **Grade:** Surgical/medical grade
- **Hypoallergenic:** Yes - the "L" designation means low carbon, reducing sensitization risk
- **Tarnish resistance:** Excellent - will not rust, corrode, or tarnish
- **Weight:** Moderate - substantial feel without being heavy
- **Plating options:** Silver, gold, rose gold, black PVD, rainbow PVD
- **Cost from Alibaba:** $1.50-3.00 per pendant with chain
- **WINNER for mass market DTC product**

### Titanium

- Superior hypoallergenic properties
- Lighter than stainless steel
- More expensive ($3-6 per unit from Alibaba)
- Harder to engrave/customize
- Premium tier option for upsell

### Sterling Silver (925)

- Beautiful but TARNISHES
- Requires maintenance
- Higher cost ($5-12 per unit)
- Customer complaints about tarnish = returns
- NOT recommended for mass market

### Rose Gold Plating (over 316L SS base)

- Extremely popular aesthetic
- Plating can wear over time
- IP (Ion Plating) or PVD coating lasts longer than standard plating
- Cost: +$0.50-1.00 per unit over base stainless steel
- RECOMMENDED as a color option alongside silver

## 4. HOW IT WORKS (Technical Deep Dive)

### The Felt Pad Mechanism

1. Locket opens via magnetic closure (or twist/hinge)
2. Felt pad (usually cotton/polyester blend, 22-25mm diameter) sits inside
3. User applies 2-3 drops of essential oil directly to pad
4. Pad absorbs oil and slowly releases through evaporation
5. Scent escapes through laser-cut pattern holes in locket face
6. Scent duration: 1-3 days depending on oil type and ambient temperature

### Oil-Specific Scent Duration

| Essential Oil | Duration on Felt Pad | Classification |
|--------------|---------------------|----------------|
| Lavender | 2-3 days | Middle note |
| Peppermint | 1-2 days | Top note (evaporates faster) |
| Eucalyptus | 1-2 days | Top note |
| Frankincense | 3-4 days | Base note (longest lasting) |
| Sandalwood | 3-5 days | Base note |
| Ylang Ylang | 2-3 days | Middle note |
| Tea Tree | 1-2 days | Top note |
| Lemon | 1 day | Top note (fastest evaporation) |

### Lava Stone Mechanism

1. Porous volcanic stone absorbs oil into micro-cavities
2. Oil slowly wicks to surface and evaporates
3. Darker oils can stain light-colored lava stone
4. Cannot easily switch scents (oil residue remains)
5. Duration: 1-2 days, but oil transfer to skin/clothing is a risk

## 3. WEIGHTED vs. UNWEIGHTED (Critical Knowledge)

### Weighted Tuning Forks

- **Appearance:** Metal weights on the ends of the prongs (tines)
- **Use:** Placed DIRECTLY ON THE BODY (stem touches skin/bone/acupressure points)
- **Vibration:** Stronger, deeper, longer-lasting vibration felt through the body
- **Sound:** Quieter audible tone, stronger physical sensation
- **Therapeutic use:** Joint pain, muscle tension, nerve groups, acupressure points, bone healing
- **Best for:** Massage therapists, Reiki practitioners, body workers

### Unweighted Tuning Forks

- **Appearance:** Standard prongs, no weights
- **Use:** Held NEAR the body in the energy field (biofield), near ears
- **Vibration:** More audible, less physical
- **Sound:** Louder, clearer tone with rich overtones/harmonics
- **Therapeutic use:** Meditation, relaxation, stress reduction, energy work, ASMR
- **Best for:** Meditation, energy healers, ASMR creators, general wellness

### Material Comparison

| Property | Aluminum Alloy | Stainless Steel |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Weight | Lighter | Heavier |
| Sustain (ring time) | **20% longer** | Shorter |
| Overtones | **Rich, complex harmonics** | Minimal overtones |
| Tone quality | Purer, clearer | Brighter but shorter |
| Bone conduction | Lower efficiency | Higher efficiency |
| Corrosion resistance | Good (anodizing possible) | Excellent |
| Cost | Standard | Slightly higher |
| **Best for therapy** | **YES - WINNER** | Medical diagnostics |

### VERDICT: Aluminum alloy is the clear winner for sound healing/therapy. Longer ring time, richer overtones, lighter weight. Stainless steel is only better for medical bone conduction tests.
