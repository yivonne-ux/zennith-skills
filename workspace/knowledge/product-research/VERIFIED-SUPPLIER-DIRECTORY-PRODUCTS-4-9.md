# PRODUCT 4: GUA SHA + JADE ROLLER (刮痧板 / 玉石滚轮)

## Industry Overview
- **Raw material hub**: Xiuyan County, Anshan City, Liaoning Province ("Chinese Jade Township") -- source of Xiuyan jade
- **Assembly/manufacturing hub**: Dongguan and Huizhou, Guangdong Province -- this is where jade rollers/gua sha are actually manufactured and assembled
- **Crystal materials hub**: Donghai, Jiangsu Province -- rose quartz, amethyst, and other crystal materials

> **KEY INSIGHT**: While Xiuyan provides the raw jade material, the actual jade roller factories are concentrated in Dongguan/Huizhou, Guangdong. Factories there purchase raw material and zinc frames, then assemble finished products.
