## TIPS FOR SENDING THESE MESSAGES:

1. **On Alibaba:** Use the "Contact Supplier" or "Chat Now" button on their store page
2. **Include these in the chat:** Copy-paste the relevant message above
3. **Ask for WeChat:** After initial contact, say "Can we continue on WeChat for faster communication? My WeChat ID is: [your ID]"
4. **Request samples first:** Never commit to a production order without testing samples
5. **Compare 3-5 suppliers per product:** Don't go with the first quote
6. **Use Trade Assurance:** Alibaba's buyer protection — always pay through the platform for first orders
7. **Negotiate:** First quoted price is NEVER the best price. Always counter-offer 20-30% lower.
