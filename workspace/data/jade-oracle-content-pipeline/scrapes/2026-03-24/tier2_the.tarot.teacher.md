## 2. Content Themes Dominating This Niche

**What they cover:**
- Card of the Day (daily pull, evergreen, habit-building)
- "How to read [specific card]" tutorials
- Tarot spreads for specific problems (career, love, full moon)
- Myths about tarot ("You don't need a gift," "Anyone can learn")
- Beginner series ("Start here if you're new to tarot")
- Deck reviews and recommendations

**Dominant formats:**
- **Carousels (saves machine):** "5 things The Tower card is actually telling you" — drives saves, shareability
- **Reels (reach machine):** Card reveals, shuffle rituals, aesthetic deck showcases
- **Quote graphics:** Single text overlaid on moody background, low effort / high share
- **Stories:** Polls ("Which card resonates?"), daily pulls, student testimonials

**Visual style:**
- Dark academia / cottagecore: candlelight, crystals, dried botanicals, linen
- Or clean white educational flat lays for legibility
- Highly curated, earthy palette — rust, forest green, cream, deep purple
