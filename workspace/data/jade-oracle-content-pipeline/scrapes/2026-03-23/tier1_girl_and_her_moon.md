## 5. Viral Post Examples (Pattern-Level)

These post archetypes consistently overperform in their niche:

**#1 — "Moon in [Sign] Survival Guide" carousel**
8–10 slides. Each slide = one insight. Final slide = CTA to save. Gets 5–10x average saves. Evergreen — reshared every lunar cycle.

**#2 — "Your [chart placement] is why you [relatable struggle]"**
Single image or short carousel. High share rate because it validates a behavior people feel shame about ("Your Saturn return is why you're exhausted — not a character flaw").

**#3 — "What [current transit] means for each sign" breakdown**
Timely content. All 12 signs = comments flood in as people tag their sign. Drives reach via mentions.

**#4 — Affirmation graphic with moon imagery**
"You are allowed to rest. The moon doesn't apologize for her cycles." Clean, shareable, gets saved to phones. Very low effort to produce, high distribution.

**#5 — Founder/personal story Reel**
Authentic talking head about her own spiritual journey or a vulnerable moment. Drives deep comments and DMs. Builds trust that converts to membership.
