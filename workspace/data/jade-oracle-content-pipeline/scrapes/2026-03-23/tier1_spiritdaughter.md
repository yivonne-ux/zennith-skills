## 3. Top Performing Content Patterns

### Hooks That Work For Them
- `"[Sign] season means ___"` — identity-trigger
- `"The [planet] is about to change everything about how you ___"`
- `"Most people misunderstand [concept]. Here's what it actually means."`
- `"Your [moon sign] is telling you ___"` — personalization hooks
- `"This [moon phase] is the most powerful time to ___"`

### What Drives Saves
- **Reference content** — moon phase calendars, retrograde dates, birth chart explainers (people save to return to)
- **Journal prompts** tied to timing (save before the new moon, use later)
- **"How to" rituals** — step-by-step, actionable (save = intention to do it)
- **Yearly/seasonal guides** — eclipse calendars, annual astro overviews

### CTAs That Work
- `"Save this for [upcoming event]"` — explicit save prompt tied to timing
- `"Which [sign/placement] are you? Tell me below"` — comment driver
- `"Tag someone who needs to hear this"` — share driver
- Links to free workbook (lead magnet via link-in-bio)

## 4. Engagement Tactics

### Comment Drivers
- **Identification prompts**: "What's your moon sign?" / "Are you a projector or generator?"
- **Prediction hooks**: "Tell me what you're releasing this full moon"
- **Polarizing takes**: Gentle astro controversy ("unpopular opinion about Scorpio season")
- **Fill-in-the-blank captions**: `"The thing [sign] season always teaches me is ___"`

### Stories Strategy
- Daily Stories (higher volume than feed)
- **Poll stickers**: "Full moon in Pisces — are you feeling it? YES / NOT YET"
- **"Share your altar"** UGC prompts
- Countdown stickers to upcoming moon phases or workshops
- Behind-scenes of Jill's own practice — humanizes the brand

### Reels Approach
- Voiceover on top of B-roll (candles, nature, journaling hands)
- 30–60 seconds, educational but calming pacing
- NOT trending audio — uses ambient/meditation music (brand consistency over virality)

### Community Building
- Strong email list integration — funnels IG to newsletter and app
- Monthly live workshops (promotes via Stories)
- Workbooks tied to each moon cycle — ongoing purchase cadence
- **Spirit Daughter App** — creates a loyal subscriber ecosystem off-platform

## 5. Likely High-Performing Posts (Examples)

**Post 1 — Eclipse Season Carousel**
> "Everything you need to know about Eclipse Season [Year]"
> 8-slide breakdown: what eclipses mean, the nodal axis, what to do/avoid, journal prompts
> *Why it works*: saves spike before eclipses, timely reference content, high perceived value

**Post 2 — "Your Moon Sign Needs ___" Single Graphic**
> One image per sign in a series, or a carousel of all 12
> *Why it works*: personalization = shares ("omg this is so me"), tagging friends

**Post 3 — New Moon Ritual Reel**
> 45-second walkthrough: candle + journal + intention setting
> Soft voiceover, no music or ambient only
> *Why it works*: aspirational, saves as "I want to try this", Reel reach extends audience

**Post 4 — Human Design Explainer Carousel**
> "What your Human Design type says about your energy"
> 5 types, each with a slide, simple language
> *Why it works*: HD is a growth topic, very high save rate, brings in new audience from HD hashtags

**Post 5 — Mercury Retrograde "Survival Guide"**
> Recurring format every retrograde (3–4x/year)
> Practical do/don't list + journal prompts
> *Why it works*: search-driven, timely, high share to friends who "blame" Mercury retrograde
