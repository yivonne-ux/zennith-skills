## @notes_from_your_therapist — Competitive Intelligence

**Creator:** Allyson Dinneen, licensed MFT (Marriage & Family Therapist), Great Barrington MA. Specialty: Childhood Emotional Neglect (CEN) and grief. Started 2015 after her husband died — literally photographed handwritten notes from her grief journal.

---

### 2. Content Themes & Formats

**Dominant themes:**
- Grief (especially ambiguous grief — grieving someone still alive, grieving what you never had)
- Childhood Emotional Neglect — her clinical niche, very "this is exactly my experience" content
- Boundaries ("you are allowed to…" framings)
- Normalizing painful emotions
- Self-compassion and vulnerability in relationships

**Format: Almost 100% single handwritten note images.** This is the entire content unit — a photograph of a physical handwritten note on paper, Post-it, or napkin. Intentionally imperfect, crumpled, analog.

**No Reels. No carousels. No video.** She predates the Reels era and never pivoted.

**Visual style:**
- Raw, analog, no design software
- Warm cream/paper tones, blue or black ink
- No branding overlays or logos
- Looks like something slipped under your door — structurally intimate

---

### 3. Top Performing Content Patterns

**What drives saves:**
- "Therapy insight I need to reread" — the note format is reference material, not entertainment. Save-rate is likely very high relative to likes.
- CEN-specific content ("the recognition hit") — people screenshot and save for personal processing
- Grief reframes — especially non-linear grief, disenfranchised grief

**What drives shares/DMs:**
- Relationship and friendship notes — people send these to a *specific person*
- The format makes DM-sharing frictionless: "this is literally you"

**What drives comments:**
- Grief posts → people share their own stories for the first time
- CEN posts → people describe their childhoods publicly, often with "I've never told anyone this"

**Hook strategy:** She has no hook. The note IS the hook. The image does the emotional work before anyone reads the caption. Captions are short, often just one sentence or nothing.

**CTAs:** Minimal, pull-based. No "DROP YOUR ANSWER BELOW" urgency. People comment because they feel seen, not because they were asked to.

---

### 4. Engagement Tactics

- **Parasocial intimacy by design** — handwritten format creates the sensation of a private message, not a content post
- **Emotional validation as the core product** — every note validates a painful experience
- **Daily cadence = ritual** — followers check in like a morning habit, not an algorithm discovery
- **No controversy, no polarization** — clean reputation (unlike Dr. LePera / @the.holistic.psychologist who faced therapist backlash)
- **Book as community artifact** — deepened loyalty in 2021
- **Passive community model** — loyal but not actively orchestrated. No polls, Q&As, challenges, or multi-part series.

---

### 5. High-Performing Post Archetypes

1. **The CEN mirror post** — "You learned to stop asking for things because no one came." People tag others, flood comments with "this was my childhood." Max saves.

2. **The grief permission slip** — "You're allowed to grieve what you never had." Shares spike when this hits ambiguous/anticipatory grief.

3. **The boundary reframe** — "Saying no IS a complete sentence." Basic but enduringly saveable. These get reshared by other accounts.

4. **The relationship note to self** — "The right person won't make you feel crazy for having needs." DM shares to partners and ex-partners.

5. **The emotional neglect checklist-as-note** — "Signs you grew up with emotional neglect:" in handwriting format. High save/share due to educational + emotional combo.

### 6. Weaknesses — What Jade Oracle Can Exploit

**Format lock-in (critical gap):**
The handwritten single-image format has become a growth ceiling. Instagram's algorithm is now video-first. Her reach-per-post is algorithmically penalized. Growth has nearly stalled (97K in 4 years vs. 330K in the prior 6).

**No video/Reels presence:**
Zero Reels, zero TikTok crossposting. She is invisible in the video discovery layer — where most new followers come from in 2025-2026.

**Passive engagement model:**
No polls, no Q&A, no "series," no community challenges. The audience feels seen but is not activated. Low comment velocity relative to follower count.

**No searchability:**
Her format produces almost no indexable text for Instagram search or SEO. Competitors using text carousels get keyword-driven discovery she doesn't.

**Narrow CEN niche:**
Deep but narrow. "Childhood Emotional Neglect" as a consistent hook limits top-of-funnel reach vs. accounts using "anxiety," "boundaries," or "self-love" as broader entry points.

**No next vehicle:**
The 2021 book was a growth catalyst that's spent. No course, Substack, membership, or community product in evidence. No new reason to follow or upgrade.

**No mystical/spiritual layer:**
Purely psychological framing. Zero astrology, intuition, spiritual integration, or "inner knowing" language. The entire spiritual-psychology crossover space is uncontested for her audience.
