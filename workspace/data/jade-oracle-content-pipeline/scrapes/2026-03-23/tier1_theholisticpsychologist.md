## 3. Top Performing Content Patterns

### Hooks That Drive Saves
- **"X things [unhealthy dynamic] looks like"** — list format, people check themselves
- **"If you grew up with [X], you probably..."** — identity recognition trigger
- **"This is not [normal thing]. This is [reframe]."** — paradigm shift
- **"No one talks about this but..."** — controversy/exclusivity signal
- **"Signs your nervous system is stuck in survival mode:"** — self-diagnosis content

### Why Content Gets Saved
1. **Self-recognition content** — "This is literally me" saves for later reference
2. **"Send to someone" content** — describes a toxic dynamic so precisely people DM it to friends
3. **Reframe content** — changes how you see something permanently; people save to re-read
4. **Actionable micro-tools** — "3-step process to regulate your nervous system right now"

### CTAs She Uses
- "Save this for when you need it"
- "Share with someone who needs to hear this"
- "What resonates? Drop it below 👇"
- "Tag someone healing alongside you"
