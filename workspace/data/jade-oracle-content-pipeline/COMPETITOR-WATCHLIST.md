# Jade Oracle — Competitor Watchlist
> **Account:** @the_jade_oracle
> **Purpose:** Monitor 20 accounts daily to learn patterns, benchmark performance, and identify gaps
> **Updated:** 2026-03-22
> **Review frequency:** Weekly deep review, daily light scan

---

## How to Use This Watchlist

1. **Daily (5 min):** Scan top 5 accounts for new posts. Note any viral content.
2. **Weekly (30 min):** Review all 20 accounts. Update engagement estimates. Log winning patterns.
3. **Monthly (1 hr):** Full audit. Compare Jade's metrics to competitors. Update strategy.

When monitoring, capture:
- Post frequency (daily? 3x/week? inconsistent?)
- Engagement patterns (which posts get 10x their average?)
- Content formats (Reel-heavy? Carousel-heavy? Mixed?)
- Growth trajectory (gaining or losing momentum?)
- What Jade can clone or adapt (respectfully, never copy)

## Tier 1: Direct Competitors (Same Niche, Similar Size Target)

These are accounts doing what Jade does (or close to it). Learn from them, differentiate from them.

### 1. @mysticbbyg
- **Niche:** Astrology, tarot readings, spiritual guidance
- **Followers:** ~15K (estimated)
- **Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week
- **Top format:** Reels with text overlays + voiceover readings
- **Engagement style:** High save rate on weekly readings
- **What they do well:** Consistent posting schedule, strong visual brand (dark/moody aesthetic)
- **Gap for Jade:** They're dark/witchy — Jade's warm/editorial Korean aesthetic is entirely different. No direct overlap.
- **Adaptation opportunity:** Clone their Reel structure (hook card reveal + interpretation) but with Jade's warm lighting and QMDJ system

### 2. @astro.bebs
- **Niche:** Pop astrology, zodiac memes, relatable astrology content
- **Followers:** ~25K (estimated)
- **Posting frequency:** Daily
- **Top format:** Meme-style graphics, zodiac sign content
- **Engagement style:** Very high shares on zodiac memes
- **What they do well:** Massive shareability. Every post is a "tag your Virgo friend" moment.
- **Gap for Jade:** Their content is surface-level zodiac humor. Jade offers genuine depth and real readings.
- **Adaptation opportunity:** Create "Your element when..." relatable content — same shareability, deeper substance

### 3. @the.tarot.teacher
- **Niche:** Tarot education, card meanings, reading technique
- **Followers:** ~12K (estimated)
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4x/week
- **Top format:** Carousels (card meaning breakdowns), educational Reels
- **Engagement style:** Extremely high saves on educational content
- **What they do well:** Authority positioning through consistent teaching. Their audience trusts them.
- **Gap for Jade:** They teach Western tarot only. Jade can own the QMDJ/BaZi education space entirely.
- **Adaptation opportunity:** Clone their carousel educational format for Five Elements, BaZi basics, QMDJ explainers

### 4. @divinelyaligned_
- **Niche:** Spiritual coaching, energy readings, manifestation
- **Followers:** ~8K (estimated)
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Top format:** Text overlay quotes, personal stories, Reels
- **Engagement style:** High comments on emotional/personal posts
- **What they do well:** Raw vulnerability. Their personal stories drive deep connection.
- **Gap for Jade:** They're heavy on manifestation language ("raise your vibration") which Jade avoids. Jade's warmth is more grounded.
- **Adaptation opportunity:** Match their vulnerability level but with Jade's grounded, practical wisdom

### 5. @selenereads
- **Niche:** Oracle card readings, weekly energy forecasts
- **Followers:** ~6K (estimated)
- **Posting frequency:** 2-3x/week
- **Top format:** Reel readings (card pulls on camera)
- **Engagement style:** Comments filled with "can you read mine?" requests
- **What they do well:** Live-feeling readings that create FOMO for personal sessions
- **Gap for Jade:** They don't have a structured system — just intuitive pulls. Jade has QMDJ (4,000-year lineage).
- **Adaptation opportunity:** Position QMDJ readings as "the system behind the intuition" — more structured, more credible
