## Key Takeaways for Content Generation
1. **Hands are everything** — close-ups of hands with cards/crystals = most engaging
2. **Candlelight > daylight** for oracle content (creates intimacy)
3. **Imperfect is better** — slightly messy ritual space > perfectly styled
4. **One action per scene** — pulling a card, not pulling a card + pouring tea + meditating
5. **Texture matters** — fur, linen, wood surfaces. Not glass/metal/plastic
6. **Gold accents** — small gold elements (jewelry, candle holders, card edges) elevate everything
7. **Plants as props** — trailing plants, small succulents add life without competing
