## MIRRA ILLUSTRATION STYLE — CALIBRATED

### Style DNA
**Korean semi-realistic digital illustration** ("manhwa/webtoon" aesthetic). Painterly middle ground between flat anime and photorealism.

### 7 Qualities
1. **Semi-realistic Korean style** — soft painterly rendering, not flat cel-shaded
2. **Multiple "Mirra girls"** — NOT one locked character. Different girls, same style family
3. **Dark brown wavy/messy hair** — warm brown eyes, natural skin, subtle blush as consistent traits
4. **Flat solid-color backgrounds** — from Mirra palette (blush, dusty rose, cream, salmon, crimson)
5. **Lineless or soft-line** — forms defined by color/value shifts, not hard outlines
6. **40-50% negative space** — breathing room above character
7. **Sparkle/glow elements FREQUENT** — star accents, glowing clothing, shimmer overlay. Connects to Mirra's existing glitter/sparkle DNA

### Color Rules
- Background colors = Mirra palette: blush (#F8BECD), dusty rose (#EBAABD), cream (#FFF9EB/#FAF7F4), salmon (#F7AB9F), deep salmon (#E88A7E), crimson (#AC374B for dark moods)
- NO mint, NO lavender, NO black bg (those were ref colors, not Mirra colors)
- Warm skin against brand-color bg = temperature contrast

### Content Rules
- Illustration posts are **character/mood/vibing** — NOT food-focused
- Food as prop only when the content requires it (e.g., "her lunch break" scene)
- Food hero posts are a SEPARATE pipeline (photography, not illustration)
- Content curation = research-based, NOT imagination

### Mood Range
- Dreamy contemplative (scrolling, daydreaming, journaling)
- Playful confident (peace sign, winking, main character energy)
- Cozy intimate (cozy clothing, tucked in, quiet moment)
- Girlboss energy (walking, headphones, "I chose this" attitude)

### Production Pipeline (from PX compound learning)
- MAX 2 reference images per NANO call
- Image 1: character style ref (from 10-illustration-refs/)
- Image 2: second character ref OR style ref
- Food = TEXT DESCRIPTION ONLY if needed (no cutout images in illustration posts)
- Full brand palette in prompt (all Mirra colors)
- Sparkle/glow instruction in every illustration prompt
- "mirra." wordmark rendered by NANO, not PIL
- Post-processing: resize 1080x1350 → grain(0.016) → DONE
