v2.1 batch (15 designs, 10/15 at 8.5+) — user feedback on remaining gaps:

1. **Chinese first** — focus CN ads. EN can come later.
2. **Logo still overlapping** — placement conflicts with other elements. Need exclusion zone.
3. **Logo size wrong** — they don't use a tiny 45px ginkgo. It's either the WORDMARK (P̌INXĪN) or the EMBLEM (ginkgo fan) at a **proper visible size**. Not whisper-small.
4. **Lack design variation**:
   - Need designs WITH human elements (hands, lifestyle, relatable human moments)
   - Need Ramen Talk aesthetic (art × food, cultural elevation)
   - Need more REACHABLE designs — "this could be your meal at home right now"
   - Not all dark moody editorial — need warmth, home kitchen, daylight
5. **Food photo too over-contrasted** — editorial mood is good but too much contrast makes food look unnatural/unappetizing. Dial back.
6. **Grain too weak** — user wants STRONGER grain. Increase from 0.018 to 0.025-0.030.
7. **Lack food variety** — only used 5-6 dishes (rendang, BKT, classic curry, green curry, sambal petai, black vinegar) when there are 18+ frozen dishes. Need to show the RANGE of flavors. Multi-dish shots showing variety.
8. **"You have your own audit"** — I should be catching these issues proactively. Not just following instructions.

**Why:** The north star is RM500K/month. Ads need to reach ACTUAL Malaysian Chinese families, not design professionals. Relatability + sophistication = the sweet spot.

**How to apply:**
- Logo: wordmark or emblem at VISIBLE size (not 45px whisper). 80-120px. Check exclusion zone.
- Food variety: use ALL 18 dishes across the batch. Multi-dish shots showing range.
- Contrast: dial back editorial_grade desat and contrast. Food should look NATURAL and appetizing.
- Grain: increase to 0.025-0.030 (closer to Mirra v4 photography grain).
- Human elements: hands, chopsticks, serving moments, family table — NOT AI-generated full-body humans.
- Relatability: some designs should feel like "my kitchen table at 7pm" not "museum gallery at midnight"
- Ramen Talk: art × food elevation for heritage content
- Self-audit: before submitting, check every design against these 8 points myself.
