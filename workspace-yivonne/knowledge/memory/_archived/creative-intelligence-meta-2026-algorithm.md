## Critical 2026 changes (quick recall):
1. **Andromeda** = creative-first delivery. Algorithm reads your ad to find the audience, not the other way around
2. **Entity ID** = visually similar ads get same ID, treated as ONE ad. Need <40% similarity, 8-20 distinct concepts
3. **20% budget rule** = changes >20% reset learning phase. 50 conversions/week to exit learning
4. **CAPI non-negotiable** = Pixel misses 60%+ conversions. For CTWA, CAPI is the ONLY way to track sales
5. **Advantage+** = 22% higher ROAS vs manual. Detailed targeting REMOVED Jan 2026
6. **Creative fatigue** = 7-14 days now. Refresh every 2-3 weeks. 90% inventory is 9:16
7. **Flexible Ads** replaced DCO. Up to 10 assets, 25-40% improvement
8. **Campaign structure** = 1 CBO campaign, 1-2 ad sets, broad targeting, 10-20 unique ads
9. **CTWA** = 3x conversion vs landing pages. Broad 2-10M audience. 10x CPC daily budget minimum
10. **Raya/holidays** = maintain budget, Chinese-MY audience LESS contested, post-Raya = high intent window
