## FESTIVAL / CALENDAR BRAND FIT — UNIVERSAL

### The Rule
Before building any festive content, ask: **"Is this the brand's festival or the brand's audience's festival?"**

| Fit level | Action | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Core festival** (brand + audience celebrate) | Go deep: 3-5 posts, themed campaign, product tie-in | CNY for Chinese food brand |
| **Cross-cultural** (national, but not brand's audience) | 1 respectful greeting max, move on | Hari Raya for Chinese brand |
| **No connection** | Skip entirely | Christmas for halal brand |

### Pre-flight Questions
1. Does the brand's CORE audience celebrate this?
2. Does the brand have a PRODUCT tie-in? (not just vibes)
3. Would a multi-post campaign feel authentic or performative?
4. Is this a commerce event or a cultural event? (different treatment)

### Commerce Events
- Double-day sales (4.4, 11.11) — verify relevance for specific MARKET (MY vs CN vs SG)
- Don't assume global e-commerce events apply to all markets
- Check if brand's audience shops on these platforms

### Calendar Research
- Always verify exact festival dates for the current year (Islamic dates shift yearly)
- Check school holidays (affects family content timing)
- Check if festival is a public holiday vs cultural observance (affects posting time)
