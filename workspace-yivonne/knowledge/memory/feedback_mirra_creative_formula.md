## PROVEN PURCHASE FORMULA (every Mirra winner shares this DNA)
1. **Fake UI format** — Notes app, iMessage, WhatsApp, Instagram DM, iPhone lock screen, horoscope forecast
2. **First-person voice** — written as if the viewer wrote it ("things i need to fix", "mirra update — week 6")
3. **Specific numbers** — RM19, 500 cal, 4kg, 6 weeks, RM352 savings, 50+ dishes
4. **Real food photo** — actual bento box visible (never AI food)
5. **MIRRA wordmark bottom center** — consistent brand placement
6. **CTA: text us on whatsapp** or "from RM19/meal"

**Why:** Data from March 2026 7-day window. S19 Transformation RM3.9/c (151 convos), BX08 iMessage RM4.1/c (47 convos), M3B NewMums RM2.4/c, S01 JeansDontFit RM2.4/c. ALL use fake phone UI that looks like organic content.

**How to apply:** When producing new Mirra creatives, ALWAYS use fake UI formats as primary. Notes app journal, messaging conversations, phone notifications. The format tricks the scroll — user processes it as "someone's real phone" before recognizing it's an ad. Food-forward statics work as MOFU support, not as primary closers.

## FAILED FORMATS
- **Data visualizations** (weight tracker graph = RM83/c, calorie receipt = RM16/c)
- **Abstract motivational** (sticky notes = RM20.9/c)
- **Scarcity/limited slots** — doesn't match Mirra brand
- **Generic lifestyle video** without real person (TiredGrabLunches = RM11.7/c)
- **Single food hero with weak copy** — food alone doesn't convert without context

## VIDEO PERFORMANCE
- KOL/real person videos WORK: SalesBoomBoom (RM4.0/c CN!), M3B-NewMums (RM2.4/c!!), Sunny (RM5.6/c)
- Generic/abstract videos FAIL: TiredGrabLunches (RM11.7/c), DropASize (0 convos), Mom-65to50 (RM12.4/c)
- Key: video needs REAL PERSON telling REAL STORY, not lifestyle montage

## EN vs CN
- EN consistently outperforms CN: RM6.1/c vs RM8.0/c (30% cheaper)
- Same creative concepts, EN gets better CPAs
- SalesBoomBoom is exception: best CN performer at RM4.0/c

## ATTRIBUTION GAP (March 21 critical discovery)
- Conversations ≠ sales. 97% of WA conversations do NOT convert to orders
- Conversation-to-sale rate: EN 6.3%, CN 1.6%
- Google Sheet tracks channel+language+customer type but NOT which ad drove the sale
- Kill decisions must use BOTH conversation cost AND other signals, not conversation count alone
- Food MOFU ads (F05-NasiLemak, F10-MonToFri, F09-4Calories) serve CONSIDERATION role — show what Mirra IS — they should NOT be killed just because they're not top closers

## EN vs CN EFFICIENCY (confirmed by sales data)
- EN: RM101/sale, 91% of orders, 93% of revenue
- CN: RM540/sale (5x worse), 9% of orders, 7% of revenue
- CN ads generate conversations but NOT sales
- Reduce CN budget significantly until attribution or creative improves

**Why:** User correctly challenged kill methodology. Conversation cost alone is misleading — an ad might generate cheap conversations that never convert, while a "expensive" MOFU ad builds consideration that converts through another ad later.

**How to apply:** Never kill based on single metric. Need: (1) conversation cost, (2) volume, (3) creative role in funnel (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU), (4) language efficiency. Set up proper ad-to-sale attribution as priority.
