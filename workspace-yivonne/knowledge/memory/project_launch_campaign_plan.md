## Launch Campaign Plan (2026-03-13)

**File:** `/Users/yi-vonnehooi/Desktop/mirra-workflow/MIRRA-LAUNCH-CAMPAIGN-PLAN.md`

**Key decisions:**
- 3 launch concepts: RM19 Lunch (product-value), She Gets It (social proof), Your Week Handled (convenience)
- 18 existing statics mapped to concepts (from cn-ads-v4 + ads-v1)
- 9 NEW carousels needed (27-45 cards total)

- Respond.io for WhatsApp API + attribution (ctwa_clid → CAPI)
- MIRRA50 promo code for 50% off first order
- Week 4: introduce ASC + Flexible Ads with proven winners

**What must be built:**
1. mirra_carousel_batch.py (card-by-card carousel production)
2. Copy doc (all 27 ad units in CN + EN)
3. Respond.io WhatsApp flow
4. Billplz payment integration (FPX + TnG)

**Budget ramp with gates:**
- Week 1-2: RM150/day (TEST only)
- Week 3: RM250/day (add SCALE) — gate: CPA < RM80
- Week 4: RM350/day (add ASC) — gate: ROAS > 1.5x
- Week 8: RM900/day — gate: ROAS > 2x
- Week 12: RM2,500/day — gate: ROAS > 3x blended

**Why:** Every decision in this plan is derived from 24 research documents (150+ sources). The 3-3-3 method is industry standard (Pilothouse). CTWA is 5x lower CPA than website. Budget gates prevent burning money before creative is proven.
