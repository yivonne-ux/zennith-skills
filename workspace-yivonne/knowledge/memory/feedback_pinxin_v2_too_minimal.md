v2 test batch killed. User feedback:
1. **Too clean/quiet** — feels like art, not a food ad. Missing food presence.
2. **Lack of food** — the hero dish needs to be appetizing, visible, make you hungry
3. **Lack of elements** — needs enough elements to be a COMPLETE ad (food + copy + packaging + benefit), not a museum piece
4. **Must convert** — RM500K north star means every ad SELLS. Sophistication serves conversion, not the other way around.

**Why:** We overcorrected from v1 (too cluttered) to v2 (too empty). The sweet spot is in the middle — Pinxin's best existing ads (8.5/10 moody food shots with copy and benefits) upgraded with world-class typography, texture, and color craft.

**How to apply:**
- Food hero must be prominent and appetizing (45-55% canvas, not 30-35%)
- Packaging shown ALONGSIDE food (cup + plated dish), not packaging alone with lid closed
- Benefits/copy present on BOFU ads — just executed with premium type hierarchy
- The design aesthetic serves the food, not the other way around
- Reference: Pinxin's own best work ("初一十五吃素不能将就", "忙妈妈" dark moody ads) = the structural baseline. Upgrade the CRAFT, not the structure.
- Logo: wordmark (P̌INXĪN) top-center for BOFU, ginkgo top-right for social. Match actual past work.
- Think "Arome bakery ad" — premium food visible + premium type + dark luxury mood. NOT "Lai Ching Heen menu cover" (no food).
