### Brand Summary
HK-based German collagen supplement for knee/joint health. Founded by Danny Tsang ("Fat Brother" / "Hong Kong Knee Guy"). 14 years at HK Brands Expo, 140K+ users. Retail at YATA stores + Fortress Hill flagship + online/WhatsApp.

**Why:** Handling social media ONLY (not ads, not website, not offline).

### Key Facts
- Market: Hong Kong (Cantonese-speaking)
- Languages: Traditional Chinese (written) + Cantonese (spoken/video)
- Products: German Collagen (flagship, orange), Collagen EX, Silicon (teal), German Apple (pink), Misha (yellow), Glacier Milk (teal)
- Palette: Teal #4DBFB8 + Orange #E8752A + Pink #D4637A + Gold #D4A843 + White + Black
- Logo: Two black dots (●●) + "dotdot.." lowercase + "GERMANY"
- Current social: 837 IG followers, 13K FB likes — massive growth opportunity
- Mascot: Has brand mascot (need to obtain from client)

### Volume
- IG: 90 posts/month (3/day, 1080x1350)
- Video: 30/month (1/day, 9:16, cross-posted to Reels/TikTok/Shorts)
- XHS: 30/month (1/day, infographic carousel)
- Total: 150 pieces/month

### 3 Pipelines
- Pipeline A: IG Static → NANO edit-first
- Pipeline B: Video → 3D Pixar-style mascot animation (Kling/Hailuo)
- Pipeline C: XHS Infographic → NANO + carousel assembly

### Video Direction
Client wants @business.shorts + @thegoodsuniverse style: 3D Pixar animated character explaining health topics. @thegoodsuniverse got 3.5M views on a talking blueberry.

### XHS Direction
Medical education infographics — hand-drawn/illustrated anatomy style per PDF reference images (meniscus guides from 糖果测评 account).

### Unique Considerations
- Elderly audience (55-80+): large fonts, high contrast, WCAG AAA, simple layouts
- Medical compliance: no "cure" claims, HK Trade Descriptions Ordinance
- Cantonese voice: warm uncle tone, not clinical
- Video production is NEW territory (no existing pipeline for animated mascot videos)
- 150 pieces/month = 3.3x our usual volume — template-heavy batch production critical

### How to apply
Read strategy docs in `02_strategy/` before any production work. Check brand-DNA.md for palette, products, compliance rules. Elderly accessibility is non-negotiable — every design must pass readability test.
