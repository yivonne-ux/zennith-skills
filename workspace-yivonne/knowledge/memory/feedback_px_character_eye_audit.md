## PX CHARACTER EYE AUDIT — CRITICAL CHECK

### CORRECT eyes (approved outputs: GW06, VR03, VR04, PXW-01, PXW-02, PXW-03, PXW-05, PXW-06, PXW-07, PXW-08, PXW-12):
- **Soft curved arcs** — gentle crescent shapes when smiling
- **Often CLOSED or half-closed** — serene, warm expression
- **Simplified** — no detailed iris/pupil/catchlight rendering
- **Small relative to face** — proportional to a real 40-year-old woman
- **Warm, gentle** — eyes express emotion through shape, not detail

### WRONG eyes (failed: PXW-04, PXW-09, PXW-10):
- **Too open/wide** — shows full iris with detailed rendering
- **Too realistic** — visible pupil, catchlight dots, detailed eyelashes
- **Too large** — anime/Disney proportions, looks 20 not 40
- **Staring** — direct gaze with fully open realistic eyes breaks the style

### AUDIT RULE
When reviewing ANY PX output, check eyes FIRST:
1. Are they soft curves / closed / squinted? → PASS
2. Are they fully open with detailed iris/pupil? → FAIL — regen
3. Do they look like a 20-year-old? → FAIL — too young
4. Do they match GW06/PXW-02 eye style? → PASS

### ALSO CHECK
- **Age consistency**: Character must look ~40, not 20-25. Subtle smile lines, mature jawline.
- **Vegan gate**: No eggs, fish, meat in ANY part of the image (PXW-11 had egg on recipe side)
- **Character drift**: If face starts looking like a "young girl" instead of "Malaysian mother ~40", it's WRONG

**How to apply:** Add to vision audit checklist. Eyes are the #1 indicator of art style match. ALL PX posts.
