All ad/social creatives use 1080x1920 (9:16) canvas with 4:5 safe zone.

- **Full canvas**: 1080 × 1920 (9:16 ratio) — works for Reels, Stories
- **Safe zone**: 1080 × 1350 (4:5 ratio) centered — top 285px and bottom 285px are bleed
- **All critical content** (text, CTA, pricing, logo) must be within the center 1080×1350 safe zone
- **Top/bottom 285px**: extended background/bleed only — no essential info

This means one creative works across Feed (4:5 crop) AND Reels/Stories (9:16 full).

**Why:** Latest format for Mirra and Pinxin. User explicitly corrected — do NOT use 1080x1350 output anymore.
**How to apply:** Generate at 9:16 aspect ratio. Place all important content within y=285 to y=1635 (the 4:5 safe zone). Logo, CTA, pricing all inside safe zone.
