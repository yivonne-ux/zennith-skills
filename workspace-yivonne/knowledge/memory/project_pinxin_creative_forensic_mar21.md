## Creative Forensic Results (March 21, 2026)

### PROVEN FORMULA — Static combo-showcase
Every Pinxin ad with purchases shares this DNA:
1. **Multiple dishes visible** (6-16 dishes shown, not just 1)
2. **Clear promo structure** (买X送X / RM price math)
3. **Urgency trigger** (限时, 只限元宵前, 10天, TNG Day)
4. **ORDER_NOW CTA → website**
5. **9:16 vertical format** (1080x1920)

### DISPROVEN: EGC gimmick videos do NOT convert to purchases
- 28 EGC video shorts exist in Notion DB
- All are gimmick UGC (Spiderman, rolling on floor, catching dishes, hammering boxes, dancing transitions)
- Same 4 copy templates rotated across 28 different gimmick hooks
- Deployed ones: Batch2 (RM183, 0 purchases), Gimmick-Shorts (RM127, 0 purchases)
- **Why they fail**: gimmick IS the content, product is afterthought. No dish showcase.
- Videos don't show multiple dishes — static combo layouts do

### 元宵 hook is dead, FORMULA lives
- 元宵 IMAGE 1 works because of formula, not festival
- TNG Day proved same formula + different urgency = LOWER CPA (RM72 vs RM91)
- New urgency hooks needed: "限时优惠", "最后XX套", "本周限定", or brand-created events

### MY vs US differences
- CPM: RM10-20 (vs US $10-30 = RM45-135) → 75% cheaper
- Addressable audience: 2-5M (Chinese-MY vegan-curious) → MUCH smaller than US
- Frequency climbs faster → creative refresh every 2 weeks (vs US 3-4 weeks)
- Malaysian consumers respond to **promo/value hooks** (买X送X) > aspirational/lifestyle
- Learning phase still 10 conversions (Meta algorithm = same globally)
- Lower CPM = more impressions per RM = fewer ads is less penalty
- Most Meta ads playbooks (Ben Heath, Charley T, Depesh) are US-centric — adjust for smaller pools

### Pinxin geo
- Peninsular Malaysia ONLY (exclude Sabah, Sarawak, Labuan)
- Addressable: ~25M people, actual target: Chinese-MY vegan-curious online shoppers
