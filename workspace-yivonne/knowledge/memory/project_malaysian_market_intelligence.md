## Malaysian Market Intelligence for Mirra (2026-03-13)

### Cost Arbitrage
Malaysia Meta CPC: RM0.50-1.00 (F&B) = 75% cheaper than US. CPM: RM5-25 vs $8.17 global. F&B has highest Meta CVR at 2.02%.

**Why:** This makes 30+ ads/day economically viable. At RM0.50-1.00 CPC, Mirra can test aggressively.

**How to apply:** Budget allocation, kill criteria, and scaling rules should use Malaysian CPM/CPC benchmarks, not global ones.

### Target Customer "Michelle"
Chinese Malaysian woman, 27-33, KL/PJ/Subang. M40-T20 (household RM8,000-15,000). Pilates, XHS in Mandarin, IG in English. Loosely counts calories. Spends RM15-22/day on lunch. Wants effortless slim, not "dieting."

### Language (CRITICAL for ad copy)
- Simplified Chinese + English code-switch = how they actually talk
- Example: "午餐不知道吃什么？Low cal还这么好吃，order了再说lah"
- Manglish particles (lah, lor, meh) = warmth
- Pure mainland Mandarin = foreign. Pure English = misses cultural connection.
- Bilingual EN+CN = 27% higher CTR
- Cantonese for audio/video in KL. Written = always Mandarin.

### Visual Aesthetic
XHS-meets-Korean: warm natural light, clean composition, overhead flat-lay for bento, 45-degree for appetite shots. NOT fitness-bro. NOT overly polished. Japanese bento precision. Light film grain = premium.

### Conversion Path
Meta Ad → Click-to-WhatsApp (45-60% CTR) → chatbot + human → order → FPX/TnG/GrabPay payment link → WhatsApp confirmation

### Payment: FPX mandatory (37% of payments), TnG (62% preference), GrabPay (25M+ users)
### Offer: 50% off first order (capped RM10), promo code mandatory, free delivery first order
### Billing: 1st of month (after 25th-30th payday)
### Timing: Best = 11AM-1PM Wed-Fri. Worst = Sunday. Best day = Wednesday.
### Seasonal: Jan = reset, Post-CNY = "damage control", 11.11/12.12 = major promo, Payday 25th-3rd = peak conversion

### LEGAL: Health claims "burns fat" / "reduces obesity" = RM10K fine or 2 years jail. Safe: "low calorie," "portion-controlled," "balanced nutrition."

### XHS: 2.5-3M MAU, 88% female, 83% purchase from XHS reviews. Mirra's untapped discovery channel.

### Unit Economics (CRITICAL)
- Contribution margin: 37-50% at RM19/meal (food RM5.50-7.50, delivery RM1.50-3.00 own fleet)
- WARNING: Third-party delivery (Grab/Panda) = 30-35% commission = margin-destroying
- CAC via Meta: RM50-80 per subscriber (5-10x cheaper than US)
- Payback: ~12 days at RM60 CAC
- LTV at 30% churn: RM1,357 revenue / RM543 contribution = 9.1x LTV:CAC
- Need 632 new subscribers/month at 30% churn for RM800K
- Month 1 ROAS = 0.3-0.8x (NORMAL, expected). Blended 4-5x from Month 6+
- 45-50% churn in Month 1 is normal even for HelloFresh
- Budget ramp: RM5K → RM15K → RM40K → RM80K → RM160K over 12 months

### WhatsApp Commerce Stack
- Respond.io Growth ($159/mo) for WhatsApp Business API
- ctwa_clid = attribution solved (fires CAPI Purchase events)
- CTWA = 5x lower CPA than website conversion ads
- Billplz for payment links (RM0.70-1.10 per FPX txn)
- Curlec for recurring billing (e-Mandate)
- Monthly ops cost at 100 orders/day: ~RM6,860

### Creative Production System
- 3-3-3 method: 3 concepts × 3 hooks × 3 formats = 27 ads/cycle (weekly)
- 80-90% of concepts fail (10-20% winners) — this is normal
- Kill: CTR < 0.5% at 24h, CPA > 2x target at 3 days, final at 7 days
- Scale: 20% budget increase every 48-72h using ORIGINAL Post ID
- Lifespan: 14-21 days. After 4.2 exposures, conversion drops 45%
- Andromeda: must change message frame, authority, proof type, voice, composition, OR format — not just headlines
- Mirra pipeline: 15 templates × 14 foods × 50+ headlines × 3 colors × 2 languages = 200+ variations

### XHS Strategy
- CES formula: Follow 8x, Comment/Share 4x, Save/Like 1x
- Content lives months (vs IG 48hrs) — XHS is a search engine
- Video 1.7x photos, carousels (4-8 images) +30% vs single
- Cannot cross-post IG↔XHS — different aesthetic. Shoot once, edit twice.
- KOC at RM100-300 + free product. Critical mass = 30-50 reviews
- 6-month budget: RM51-87K. Conversion: Content → DM → WhatsApp
