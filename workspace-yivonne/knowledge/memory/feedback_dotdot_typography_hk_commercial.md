## DOTDOT TYPOGRAPHY — HK COMMERCIAL POSTER STYLE

### Why This Exists
v1-v5 used clean editorial typography (single-color bold TC headlines). User provided 5 HK/CN commercial design pins showing the actual target: **Chinese commercial poster typography** with multi-color headlines, graphic character treatment, badges, and extreme size hierarchy.

### The Typography DNA (v6+)

1. **Multi-color headlines** — Key words in brand teal #4DBFB8, action words in orange #E8752A, rest in near-black. 2-3 colors WITHIN one headline.
2. **Extreme size hierarchy** — Headline is 4-5x body text. Subtext intentionally tiny. Not uniform sizing.
3. **Characters as graphics** — TC characters treated as GRAPHIC ELEMENTS with fills, outlines, shadows, or dimensional effects. Not plain text.
4. **Numbered steps** — Large bold circled numbers (①②③) as visual anchors. Teal fill on white or cream.
5. **Badge/tag elements** — Key info in colored pill shapes, ribbons, stamps, or rounded tags. Not plain text.
6. **Text-illustration integration** — Text wraps around, overlaps, and is PART OF the composition. Not floating on top.
7. **Bilingual** — TC headline dominant, English subtitle at 30% size below (optional for DotDot).
8. **Still elderly-readable** — Color mixing doesn't mean low contrast. Maintain WCAG AAA 7:1 on key info.

### Anti-patterns
- ❌ Single-color flat headlines (too editorial/Western)
- ❌ Uniform text sizing (no hierarchy)
- ❌ Text floating on top of illustration disconnected
- ❌ Plain text for key info (should be in badges/tags)
- ❌ Monochrome typography (should have 2-3 color emphasis)

**How to apply:** Update ALL DotDot prompt typography blocks to specify multi-color TC headlines, extreme size hierarchy, badge elements, and text-illustration integration. Reference the YOTTO and Herbal Farmer pins as style anchors.
