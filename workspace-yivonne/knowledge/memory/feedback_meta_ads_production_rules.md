## Meta Ads Production Rules (2026-03-13)

### NORTH STAR: RM800,000/month revenue

### The Three Laws
1. **Creative > Targeting** — Creative = 75-90% of performance. Andromeda handles targeting. Your job = scroll-stopping creative.
2. **Authentic > Polished** — Lo-fi/ugly ads outperform studio by +72% ROAS. 40-50% of production should be phone-shot/screenshot/meme.
3. **Volume > Perfection** — 10+ genuinely distinct concepts per campaign. Refresh every 2 weeks. An ugly ad that converts at 6x > a beautiful ad at 2x.

### Non-negotiable per ad
- ONE message communicated in 0.5 seconds
- Specific number visible (RM19, 500卡, 50+)
- First-person copy where possible ("my RM19 lunch" not "your lunch")
- CTA is specific ("Order Your First Box" not "Learn More")
- Less than 40% visual similarity to other active ads (Andromeda rule)
- Real food only (never AI-generated)

### Format priority
1. Carousel (4.2x ROAS) — primary format for menu variety
2. Short Reels 9:16 sound-on (34.5% lower CPA) — cold prospecting
3. Static 4:5 (60-70% of conversions) — always-on workhorse
4. Text-led UGC (+38% vs talking head) — benefit overlays on customer video

### Copy rules
- PAS framework for cold audiences (highest converting)
- First 125 characters = only part visible. This IS the ad.
- Per-meal pricing always: "RM19/meal" never "RM570/month"
- Never lead with plant-based (it's a by-the-way)
- Never use exclamation marks (brand voice = calm confidence)

### Creative mix (per week, minimum)
- 3-4 lo-fi/UGC style (phone-shot, screenshot aesthetic, meme)
- 2-3 editorial/brand (NANO pipeline, typography-driven)
- 2-3 carousel (menu showcase, before/after, story sequence)
- 1-2 Reels/video (if available)
- = 8-12 genuinely distinct concepts per week

### Campaign structure
- ASC: 60-70% budget (~RM4,000-4,700/day)
- Testing: 20-30% (~RM1,000-2,000/day, RM20-40 per concept)
- Retargeting: 10-20%
- Scale winners by max 20%/day
- Don't touch during 7-day learning phase

### Kill criteria
- Frequency >2.5 → rotate creative
- CTR drops >20% from peak → refresh
- CPA exceeds 2x target after 10,000 impressions → kill
- No conversions after spending 2x target CPA → kill immediately

### Free conversion boosters
- Reply to EVERY ad comment (+22% conversion lift)
- WhatsApp CTA for bottom-funnel (highest converting in MY)
- Referral program triggered at order 3

**Why these rules:** Derived from analysis of AG1 (500 active ads), HelloFresh ($1.44B marketing), Glossier, Skims, 40,000+ ads studied by Motion, and 8 deep research streams. Not theory — data.
