## Rule: ALWAYS use OUTCOME_SALES objective for Mirra campaigns

**Why:** March 21 forensic revealed the performance drop (RM60-70/order → RM200/order) was caused by switching from SALES to ENGAGEMENT objective. ENGAGEMENT optimizes for conversations (chatters), not buyers. 97% of WA conversations don't convert to orders. SALES objective finds people who BUY, not people who chat.

**How to apply:**
- All new Mirra campaigns must use `OUTCOME_SALES` objective
- Old winners (Scalling SUPER WIN, CBO-EN) were SALES — that's WHY they worked
- TEST-EN and TEST-CN were ENGAGEMENT — that's WHY they cost 3x more per order
- Reactivated: Scalling SUPER WIN (RM158/day), CBO-EN (RM213/day)
- New SALES: MIRRA-SALES-EN-MAR26 (RM350/day, 15 cherry-picked ads)
- PAUSED: TEST-EN, TEST-CN (both ENGAGEMENT)
