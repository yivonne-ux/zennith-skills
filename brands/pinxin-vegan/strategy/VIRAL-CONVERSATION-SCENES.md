# Pinxin — Viral Conversation & Scene Concepts
## 38 Proven Formats for Illustrated Comics & Quote Posts

**Date**: 2026-03-27 | **Rule**: Brand is the BACKGROUND, life is the STORY.

---

## THE GOLDEN RULE
> The brand should NOT make these about Pinxin products. The brand should make these about LIFE, with Pinxin as the quiet constant in the background — a logo, a frozen pack on the counter, the food on the table. The product is present but the STORY is about family. That's what makes it shareable rather than an ad.

## TOP 10 — PRODUCTION PRIORITY (Score 10/10)

### 1. THE FRUIT CUTTING (Silent Love) 🥇
Kid studying. Mum appears silently with cut fruit. Didn't ask. She didn't announce. She just knew. She's already gone back to the kitchen.
**Caption**: "她从不说我爱你。她切水果。" (She never says I love you. She cuts fruit.)

### 2. THE KITCHEN LIGHT (Waiting Parent) 🥇
Late night. House dark. One light on: kitchen. Plate covered with note: "Heat 3 min." Mum asleep on sofa. She waited.
**Caption**: "厨房的灯，永远为你亮着。" (The kitchen light is always on for you.)

### 3. HAVE YOU EATEN? (Love Language) 🥇
Kid: "Mum, I got a new job!" / Mum: "你吃了吗？" Kid: "I'm getting married!" / Mum: "你吃了吗？"
**Caption**: "有一种爱，叫'你吃了吗'" (There's a love called "have you eaten")

### 4. MUM LEARNING YOUR DIET (Direct PX Alignment) 🥇
Kid says eating less meat. Next visit: mum's prepared entire plant-based spread. Not perfect — but she TRIED. Her YouTube history: "vegetarian cook easy delicious"
**Caption**: "她看了14个视频。只为你那一句话。" (She watched 14 videos. All for that one thing you said.)

### 5. GOODBYE BAGS (Sending Off With Love) 🥇
Leaving parents' house. Bag 1: Tupperware. Bag 2: Fruits. Bag 3: Snacks. Bag 4: "Your dad doesn't eat this." Already packing Bag 5.
**Caption**: "你来吃午饭，走时搬了个家。"

### 6. COME HOME MORE (Empty Table) 🥇
Mum: "No need come, very far." / "Don't waste money." / Kid: "Ok won't come." / Mum: "...you're not coming?" / Last panel: Mum alone at table set for two.
**Caption**: "她说别来。她煮了你爱吃的。"

### 7. GRANDMA'S PORTIONS (Love = Volume) 🥇
"Ah Ma, small portion please." → Mountain of rice, 6 dishes piled on. "Ah Ma I said SMALL—" / "你太瘦了！多吃！"
**Caption**: "阿嫲的'小份'= 别人的自助餐"

### 8. THREE GENERATIONS OF LOVE 🥇
Grandma: pushes food silently. Mum: "Eat more. Wear jacket." Our gen: "I love you mum!" Mum: "...你吃了吗？"
**Caption**: "三代人，三种说法，一种爱。"

### 9. MUM'S FOOD RATING SYSTEM (Malaysian) 🥇
"Okay lah" = acceptable. "Not bad" = quite good. "Can lah" = won't return. "Home cook better" = terrible. Silence while eating fast = AMAZING but will never admit.
**Caption**: "你妈妈是哪一级？"

### 10. BISCUIT TIN LIE (Shared Trauma) 🥇
Kid sees Danish cookie tin. Opens excitedly. Inside: sewing supplies. Since 1997. Grandma: "Good tin."
**Caption**: "每个人的童年都被这个铁盒骗过。"

## PRODUCTION FORMAT

- **3-5 panels** per comic (carousel for IG, single image for FB/XHS)
- **Warm PX palette**: cream bg, brown/green accents
- **PX illustration style**: semi-realistic digital painting (locked refs)
- **Bilingual**: CN primary text, EN secondary caption
- **PX branding**: subtle — logo corner, PX product in background, NOT the focus
- **Post as**: IG carousel (swipe) + XHS image note + FB single + TikTok static

## PLATFORM ROUTING

| Trigger Type | Best Platform | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Tag-your-mum comedy | IG + FB | Carousel comic |
| "This is us" couples | IG + TikTok | Single panel + caption |
| Malaysian-specific humor | FB + IG | Carousel comic |
| Emotional/tearjerker | XHS + IG | Single scene illustration |
| Save-worthy wisdom | XHS | Image note + long text |

## POSTING CADENCE
- 2x/week illustrated comic posts (from this concept bank)
- 1x/week quote post (food wisdom or relatable truth)
- Rotate through concepts — each one can be revisited seasonally
- 38 concepts = ~4.5 months of unique content at 2x/week
