## MY OWN FORENSIC ANALYSIS — What I should have caught

### CRITICAL GAP: Not enough FOOD-AS-HERO ads
Looking at the 40-ad set, I count:
- 20+ FORMAT/UI ads (Notes, Calculator, Receipt, etc.)
- 5 HUMAN reaction ads
- Only ~5-7 ads where FOOD is the actual hero (Macro, Feast, Abundance, Home Kitchen)

For a FOOD brand, this ratio is WRONG. The food should dominate at least 40% of the campaign.
Format ads stop the scroll. But FOOD ads create DESIRE and close the sale.

Mirra's data: food-forward ads drive 60-70% of conversion volume. UI/format ads drive awareness.

### MISSING AD TYPES (food-forward)
1. Single dish hero — one dish, beautifully lit, massive, making you drool. We have T03 (macro) but need MORE dishes
2. Multi-dish grid — like FA2 (menu grid) but better, with REAL Pinxin food
3. Before/after — frozen cup → plated meal transformation (was in earlier batches, lost in campaign-ready)
4. Packaging + plated food side by side — "this is what you buy, this is what you eat"
5. Overhead abundance with rice — family dinner table (T04 has it but food looks AI)
6. Ingredient close-ups — hericium mushroom texture, petai beans, curry leaves
7. Action shots — chopstick lifting food, sauce dripping, steam rising
8. Different dishes rotated — we keep showing rendang/BKT/green curry. Where's mei cai, namyu tofu, black vinegar, spicy asam, sesame ginger?
