## ARCHITECTURE: ACCA Funnel × Platform × Language

```
ACCA Funnel:
  A — AWARENESS (品牌认知): Identity, heritage, values. "Who is Pinxin?"
  C — CONSIDERATION (了解考虑): Product education, social proof, KOL. "Why Pinxin?"
  C — CONVERSION (购买转化): Price, urgency, bundles, CTWA. "Buy now."
  A — ADVOCACY (口碑推荐): Reviews, community, recipe sharing. "Tell others."

Distribution target (from Mirra learnings):
  TOFU/Awareness: 15%
  MOFU/Consideration: 50%
  BOFU/Conversion: 30%
  Advocacy: 5%

Language split: CN 65% / EN 35% (CN skews BOFU, from Mirra data)
```

## 10 CONTENT CATEGORIES

### CAT-01: Heritage Story (传承故事)
**Funnel**: TOFU — Awareness
**Purpose**: Brand world building. Penang heritage, founder story, 8-year journey, vegan philosophy.
**Visual style**: Gallery/Museum template. Gold frames, textile backgrounds, editorial food-as-art.
**Photography**: Moody dramatic, 45-degree, hero dish as centerpiece.
**Typography**: Massive CN brush calligraphy headline (60%+ canvas). Vertical text sidebar.
**Copy pattern**: Storytelling, cultural connection, emotional. "品味传承" energy.
**Frequency**: 2-3/month
**Platforms**: IG feed, XHS, FB
**Example**: Ahma's recipe reimagined as plant-based. Gold frame around rendang. Vertical text: "8年手工慢熬."

### CAT-02: Dish Hero (招牌主角)
**Funnel**: MOFU — Consideration
**Purpose**: Single dish spotlight. Make each dish irresistible. Show texture, ingredients, preparation.
**Visual style**: Full-bleed photo + float text. Dark mode (PX Green/brown bg).
**Photography**: 45-degree hero (65-75% frame), shallow DOF, steam visible, cream linen prop.
**Typography**: Dish name in CN display (FZChaoZhiTi, 80-100pt). English name below (Recoleta, 36pt).
**Copy pattern**: Dish story + 1 USP. "慢熬3-4小时 | 猴头菇比肉还嫩"
**Frequency**: 4-6/month (rotate all 18 frozen + 4 paste)
**Platforms**: IG feed, XHS, FB, TikTok cover
**Example**: Rendang Hericium close-up, dark wood surface, gold chopsticks, "南洋古早味仁当" in massive CN type.

### CAT-03: Benefit/USP Education (健康卖点)
**Funnel**: MOFU — Consideration
**Purpose**: Health + ingredient education. Lake Salt, organic cane sugar, hericium mushroom, allium-free.
**Visual style**: Clean card (light mode). Cream #F2EEE7 bg, dark text, single product shot.
**Photography**: Product + dish pairing (tin + plated food). Overhead or 45-degree.
**Typography**: Recoleta headline (EN) or FZDouSDYWTJW (CN). Bullet benefits with icons.
**Copy pattern**: Specific numbers: "50%低钠盐", "零胆固醇", "15分钟上桌", "慢熬3小时".
**Frequency**: 3-4/month
**Platforms**: IG feed, FB (EN + CN versions)
**Example**: Lake Salt Light infographic. Product jar + "50% Lower Sodium" in Gotham Bold. Clean, medical-grade trust.

### CAT-04: Social Proof / KOL (口碑见证)
**Funnel**: MOFU — Consideration
**Purpose**: Customer testimonials, KOL reviews, press mentions, awards.
**Visual style**: Full-bleed photo (customer/KOL shot) + gold frame overlay or quote card.
**Photography**: UGC-style or KOL content repackaged with brand treatment.
**Typography**: Quote in CN serif (FZFWZhuZi Mincho). Attribution below.
**Copy pattern**: Real quotes. Tatler Asia badge. NST "tastes better than non-vegan" pull quote.
**Frequency**: 2-3/month
**Platforms**: IG feed, FB, XHS
**Example**: Tatler Asia badge + gold frame + Asam Laksa hero. "全马唯一上榜的植物基叻沙."

### CAT-05: Recipe / How-To (食谱教学)
**Funnel**: MOFU — Consideration
**Purpose**: Show how easy it is. 15-minute meals. Recipe ideas using paste/frozen products.
**Visual style**: Step-by-step grid or carousel. Overhead photography.
**Photography**: Process shots (defrost → heat → serve). Bright, warm, clean.
**Typography**: Step numbers in gold. Instructions in HK Grotesk.
**Copy pattern**: "3步搞定" simplicity. Timer icon. Before/after (frozen pack → plated meal).
**Frequency**: 2-3/month
**Platforms**: IG carousel, XHS, TikTok, FB
**Example**: 4-panel carousel: "打开 → 加热 → 上桌 → 享受" with gold numbered badges.

### CAT-06: Promo / Bundle (促销优惠)
**Funnel**: BOFU — Conversion
**Purpose**: Drive purchase. Flash sales, bundle deals, free delivery, festive promos.
**Visual style**: Bold split composition. Dark top (headline) + food grid bottom. Gold accents.
**Typography**: MASSIVE price in gold (RM XX). CN headline (FZChaoZhiTi, 100pt+). Urgency badges.
**Copy pattern**: Price anchor + urgency. "买14送6", "免运费", "限时优惠". Specific RM amount.
**Frequency**: 4-6/month (higher during festivals/campaigns)
**Platforms**: FB ads, IG stories, WhatsApp blast
**Example**: Grid mosaic of 6 dishes. Gold "RM13+/盒" center overlay. "买越多省越多" headline.

### CAT-07: Festival / Cultural (节庆文化)
**Funnel**: TOFU/BOFU — Awareness + Conversion
**Purpose**: CNY, Nine Emperor Gods, Mooncake Festival, Merdeka, etc. Cultural relevance.
**Visual style**: Premium gallery. Burgundy #470E23 + gold. Baroque wallpaper backgrounds.
**Photography**: Feast spread, overhead, family table setting. Claypot, traditional vessels.
**Typography**: Brush calligraphy CN headline. Festival-specific decorative elements.
**Copy pattern**: Cultural hook → product relevance. "初一十五吃素积德" → "品馨帮你准备好了."
**Frequency**: Seasonal (6-8 festivals/year, 3-5 posts per festival)
**Platforms**: All platforms
**Example**: CNY Poon Choi spread. Burgundy damask bg. Gold frame. "年夜饭不必忙" in brush script.

### CAT-08: Character / Family Scene (家庭角色)
**Funnel**: TOFU/MOFU — Awareness + Consideration
**Purpose**: Use vector character family for relatable storytelling. Malaysian Chinese family dynamics.
**Visual style**: Character-forward with speech bubbles or scene setup. PX Green bg.
**Photography**: None (illustration-led). Characters from `assets/characters/`.
**Typography**: Speech bubble text in FZDouSDYWTJW. Headline in display.
**Copy pattern**: Dialogue-driven. Ahma scolding uncle for eating unhealthy → "品馨猴头菇仁当，阿公也爱吃."
**Frequency**: 2-3/month
**Platforms**: IG feed, FB, WhatsApp
**Example**: Angry Uncle character + speech bubble "又吃快餐?!" → Aunty presenting Pinxin dish.

### CAT-09: Raw Ad / Lo-Fi (原始广告)
**Funnel**: BOFU — Conversion (Meta ads primary)
**Purpose**: "Ugly ads" that convert. Screenshot-style, text-heavy, WhatsApp-native feel.
**Visual style**: Minimal design. White/cream bg, large text, product photo, price. No premium styling.
**Photography**: Simple product shots, unboxing, delivery box.
**Typography**: Bold sans-serif (Gotham/Dinot). Large RM price. Simple hierarchy.
**Copy pattern**: Direct-response. "RM17.75/餐", "Free Delivery 10km", "现在订购". First-person voice.
**Frequency**: 8-12/month (bulk for Meta ads testing — 3×3×3 framework)
**Platforms**: FB/IG ads (Meta), WhatsApp
**Example**: Photo of delivery box + "Free Delivery West Malaysia Above RM250" + WhatsApp CTA.

### CAT-10: XHS/Lifestyle Native (小红书生活)
**Funnel**: MOFU — Consideration
**Purpose**: XHS-native content. Lifestyle, aesthetic, relatable. "My vegan meal prep" energy.
**Visual style**: Bright, lifestyle, slightly saturated. Chinese text overlay with emoji-style badges.
**Photography**: Lifestyle 45-degree or overhead. Bright natural light. Hands visible.
**Typography**: CN text in rounded/friendly font. XHS-native badge style (colored pills with text).
**Copy pattern**: First-person diary style. "今天的素食便当🍱", "打工人的15分钟晚餐". Hashtag-ready.
**Frequency**: 4-6/month
**Platforms**: XHS primary, IG secondary
**Example**: Bento box lifestyle shot, hands holding chopsticks, "一个人的素食晚餐，15分钟搞定."

## IG GRID STRATEGY

Row rhythm (every 3 posts):
```
Position 1: BOLD — Dark mode, massive CN type, PX Green bg (CAT-01, CAT-02, CAT-07)
Position 2: PHOTO — Full-bleed food photography, warm tones (CAT-02, CAT-05)
Position 3: CLEAN — Light mode card, cream bg, benefits/social proof (CAT-03, CAT-04)
```

Grid thumbnail safety: IG crops 4:5 to 3:4. Keep critical elements within center 1015px.

Color flow: Dark green → Warm food → Cream breathing → repeat. Never 3 dark posts in a row.

## PRODUCTION PIPELINE (per category)

> Architecture proven across 97 ads at 100% success rate. See `PRODUCTION-INTELLIGENCE.md` for full technical spec.

### Pipeline A: NANO Edit-First (CAT-01, 02, 04, 07, 08)
Reference image → 7-layer prompt assembly → NANO Banana Pro Edit (2K) → post-process → DONE.
- **Edit-first**: "Keep EXACT same layout. CHANGES — swap only [list]. PRESERVE — everything else."
- NANO renders ALL text, layout, illustrations, badges. PIL renders NOTHING visual.
- Single-pass only. Fix prompt if failed, don't reprocess output.
- ~$0.15/image, 44-92 seconds.

### Pipeline B: Food Photo Integration (CAT-02, 05, 06)
Food photo as INPUT → NANO multi-image [reference + food photo(s)] → post-process → DONE.
- Food photo is Image 2+, anchored FIRST in prompt: "Image 2 IS the [dish]. Do not generate AI food."
- Food is SACRED — never let AI alter food appearance.
- Treat food pre-input: center-crop to zone AR, warm tint 8%, desat 10%.

### Pipeline C: Raw/Lo-Fi (CAT-09)
Reference ad screenshot → NANO edit swap → post-process → DONE.
- Still uses edit-first NANO, but intentionally lo-fi aesthetic.
- "Ugly ads" philosophy — screenshot-style, WhatsApp-native feel.
- Bulk: 12/month via 3×3×3 testing matrix (3 concepts × 3 hooks × 3 formats = 27 ads).

### Pipeline D: Template Batch (CAT-03, 06, 10)
Reference-guided with variable data → diversity matrix → NANO edit → post-process.
- CSV data drives: dish name, price, benefit, persona, headline.
- Andromeda diversity: 6 dimensions enforced (persona, style, layout, color, funnel, food angle).

### Post-Processing Chain (ALL categories — exact sequence, do NOT reorder)
```
force_size(1080×1350)
→ editorial_grade(desat=0.08, contrast=1.06)    # less than BB — preserve food color
→ paper_texture(opacity=0.020)                    # less than BB — dark bgs need less
→ place_logo(gold on dark, beige on light, autocrop alpha bbox, 140px max, 40px padding)
→ grain(3.5)                                      # less than BB — preserve food clarity
→ sharpen(r=1.0, pct=85, thresh=4)
```
No heavy color filters. No PIL text. No multi-pass. Grain ALWAYS second-to-last. Sharpen ALWAYS last.
