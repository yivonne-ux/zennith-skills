# 1. INSTAGRAM

## 1.1 Grid Layout Strategy (9-Grid Planning)

### Row Rhythm Pattern: BOLD | PHOTO | CLEAN
Plan your grid in rows of 3. Each row has a visual identity:

| Row Type | Slot 1 | Slot 2 | Slot 3 |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|
| **BOLD** | Typography-led promo (PX Green bg, gold text) | Multi-dish hero carousel | Typography-led value prop |
| **PHOTO** | Editorial food close-up | Lifestyle/human moment | Flat-lay ingredient spread |
| **CLEAN** | Minimal quote/testimonial | Carousel educational | Single dish spotlight |

**Planning rules:**
- Never place 2 identical formats adjacent (horizontally or vertically)
- Alternate warm/cool tones across columns
- Every 9 posts = 1 complete visual "chapter"
- Use a grid planning tool (Planoly, Later, UNUM) to preview before posting
- Pin 3 best-performing posts to top of profile (rotate monthly)

### Color Distribution Across Grid
- Max 40% same color mood in any visible 9-grid
- PX Green backgrounds: 2-3 per 9 posts
- Cream/warm food shots: 3-4 per 9 posts
- Gold accent pieces: 1-2 per 9 posts
- Human/lifestyle: 1-2 per 9 posts

## 1.3 Reels Strategy

### Optimal Length by Content Type
| Content Type | Ideal Length | Why |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Quick hook/trend | 7-15 seconds | High completion rate, loop potential |
| Recipe/cooking demo | 30-60 seconds | Sweet spot for food tutorials |
| Brand story/BTS | 60-90 seconds | Deeper connection, share-worthy |
| Full tutorial/unboxing | 90-180 seconds | Only if pacing is tight throughout |

### Hook Patterns (First 1.5 Seconds)

**7 proven hook formulas for Pinxin:**

1. **The Reveal** — Start with the finished dish, steaming. "This isn't chicken. It's lion's mane mushroom."
2. **The Contrast** — Split screen: fast food vs Pinxin meal. "Same price. Zero cholesterol."
3. **The Question** — "Can plant-based rendang fool a meat lover?" (then show taste test)
4. **The Sound** — ASMR sizzle/crunch as first frame. No words needed for 2 seconds.
5. **The Timer** — "15 minutes from freezer to this" — show clock + final dish simultaneously
6. **The Myth Buster** — "Plant-based food is bland" (text) — CUT TO steaming, richly-colored curry
7. **The Number** — "11,000 Penang families switched to this"

### Trending Audio Approach
- Use trending sounds within 48 hours of emergence
- Malaysian/SG trending sounds > global sounds for local reach
- Original audio with ASMR cooking sounds performs extremely well for food

## 1.5 Carousel Strategy

### Optimal Slide Count
| Slide Count | Best For |
|-------------|----------|
| 3-5 slides | Quick product showcase, "3 reasons to try" |
| 7-8 slides | Recipe walkthrough, ingredient education |
| 10 slides | Full story arc, comparison guide, menu showcase |

### Story Arc Templates for Pinxin

**Template A: Problem-Solution (5 slides)**
1. Pain point ("Tired of bland frozen meals?")
2. Aggravate ("Most frozen food = preservatives + MSG")
3. Introduce solution (Pinxin hero shot)
4. Proof (USPs: organic sugar, lake salt, slow-cooked 3-4hrs)
5. CTA (order now + price)

**Template B: Menu Showcase (8 slides)**
1. Cover: "8 dishes that changed how we eat" (bold typography)
2-7. One dish per slide (editorial photo + 1-line description + CN name)
8. CTA: "WhatsApp us for today's combo deal"

**Template C: Education (10 slides)**
1. Hook question: "Is vegan food actually healthier?"
2-8. One fact per slide with data/visual
9. Brand connection to facts
10. CTA + social proof stat

# 2. FACEBOOK

## 2.1 Page vs Group Strategy — Use BOTH

| | Facebook Page | Facebook Group |
|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Official brand, ads, shop | Community, loyalty, organic |
| **Organic reach** | Very low (1-3%) | 5-10x higher than Page |
| **Content** | Polished brand posts, offers, Reels | Recipes, questions, customer photos |
| **Name** | "Pinxin Vegan Cuisine 品馨蔬食" | "Pinxin Plant-Based Kitchen 品馨素食厨房" |

## 2.2 Key Rules

- **NEVER post links in main caption** — algorithm suppresses by 50-80%. Put link in first comment.
- **FB Reels**: Same-day Reels get 50% more distribution (Oct 2025 update)
- **Cross-post ALL IG Reels to FB** (free distribution)
- **Group**: Private group creates exclusivity, higher engagement

## 2.3 Posting Frequency: 5-7 posts/week

| Content Type | Frequency |
|-------------|-----------|
| Reels | 3-4x/week |
| Photo/Carousel | 2-3x/week |
| Stories | Daily |

## 2.4 Cross-Posting Rules

**DO:** Cross-post Reels, cross-post Stories, use Business Suite for scheduling
**DON'T:** Auto-share every IG post, include IG hashtags in FB, use same caption verbatim, post links in caption

## 4.3 KOL/KOC Seeding Pyramid

```
         KOL (1-3/quarter)  — 50K+ followers — RM500-2,000/post
        /
       KOC (10-20/month)    — 1K-50K followers — RM100-300/post
      /
     UGC (ongoing)          — Real customers — discount codes, free samples
```

**KOC > KOL for Pinxin on XHS:** Higher trust, higher conversion, more cost-effective.
