## 3. THE PROVEN CREATIVE FORMULA (EVERY Winner Follows This)

### Static Winners DNA — Replicate This In Video

1. **Multiple dishes visible** — 6-16 dishes shown, NOT single dish hero
2. **Clear promo math** — 买X送X, RM pricing, percentage off (e.g., 买8送8, RM12++/盒)
3. **Urgency trigger** — 限时, X天限定, 最后XX套 (brand-created urgency > festival-dependent)
4. **ORDER_NOW CTA** → pinxinvegan.com
5. **9:16 vertical format** (1080×1920)
6. **Identity gimmick format** — Hijack a familiar UI (receipt, boarding pass, bank statement, calculator, food delivery tracker) and fill it with Pinxin content. This is the BREAKTHROUGH formula. TNG-PROMO = RM33 CPA on its best day.

### The Identity Gimmick Formula (Most Important)
- Take a REAL-WORLD format people recognize (receipt, boarding pass, Grab delivery, notification, bank statement)
- Fill it with Pinxin food + promo information
- Same offer, different identity hooks = Meta treats each as a new "entity" = more reach
- **Each hook NEEDS unique artwork** — NOT same image with different copy

### What Converts on Which Channel
- **Website (Purchase):** BOFU promo/urgency + format hijacks + raw lo-fi
- **WhatsApp (Conversations):** FOOD photos + MOFU social proof + video + BOFU retarget
- **KEY INSIGHT:** Same ad can fail on website but succeed on WhatsApp

## 4. VIDEO CREATIVE DIRECTION — WHAT TO MAKE

### The Gap: Static is Winning, Video is Losing
All current video (EGC gimmick shorts) = ZERO purchases. But static ads with the combo-showcase formula are converting at RM20-67 CPA.

**THE BRIEF: Make videos that follow the STATIC winner formula, not the gimmick video formula.**

### Video Concepts to Produce (Priority Order)

#### CONCEPT 1: "Multi-Dish Reveal" (BOFU — Website + WA)
**Why:** Best static ads show 6-16 dishes. Video should do the same but with motion.
- Open with a familiar "problem" (什么都不想煮 / don't know what to eat)
- Quick cuts revealing dish after dish (rendang, BKT, curry, sambal petai, asam fish...)
- Each dish 0.5-1s, steam/sizzle/close-up
- End card: promo math (买8送8 / RM12++盒) + pinxinvegan.com
- **Duration:** 8-15 seconds
- **Mood:** Appetizing, abundant, fast-paced
- **Music:** Upbeat, rhythmic (cuts on beat)
- **Text overlay:** Dish names flash as they appear, price reveal at end

#### CONCEPT 2: "Identity Gimmick — Animated" (BOFU — Website)
**Why:** Receipt/boarding pass/bank statement formats are the #1 static converters. Animate them.
- Receipt "printing" with items appearing line by line (each item = a Pinxin dish with price)
- OR boarding pass with destination = "品馨蔬食" and departure = "不知道吃什么"
- OR food delivery tracker animation (ordered → preparing → delivering → enjoying)
- OR calculator adding up savings (normal food RM25 → Pinxin RM12++ per meal, 买8送8)
- **Duration:** 8-12 seconds
- **Mood:** Clean, clever, recognizable format
- **Text:** The format IS the text (receipt lines, boarding pass fields, etc.)
- **End card:** CTA + promo

#### CONCEPT 3: "15-Minute Timer" (MOFU/BOFU — WA)
**Why:** Key USP = 15 minutes from frozen to table. Show it.
- Timer counting from 0:00 to 15:00 (sped up)
- Frozen packet → boiling water → plating → steam rising → family eating
- Split screen or time-lapse format
- **Duration:** 10-15 seconds
- **Mood:** Satisfying, practical, "this easy?"
- **Text:** "从冷冻到上桌，只需15分钟" / "Frozen to table in 15 minutes"
- **End card:** Promo + CTA

#### CONCEPT 4: "Price Comparison" (BOFU — Website)
**Why:** Malaysian consumers respond to value hooks. Show the math visually.
- Split: GrabFood order (1 meal = RM25-35) vs Pinxin (RM12++ per meal, 买8送8)
- Animated price tags / calculator / side-by-side
- Dishes stacking up on the Pinxin side while GrabFood side shows 1 sad meal
- **Duration:** 8-12 seconds
- **Mood:** Smart shopper energy, "you're paying HOW MUCH?"
- **Text:** Price numbers prominent, comparison clear

#### CONCEPT 5: "Abundance Overhead Shot" (MOFU — WA)
**Why:** FOOD photos drive WA conversations. Hero food photography in motion.
- Overhead table being set — dishes appearing one by one
- Hands placing bowls, steam rising, chopsticks reaching in
- Beautiful, warm, family table energy
- **Duration:** 10-15 seconds
- **Mood:** Warm, abundant, "wah so many dishes"
- **Text:** Minimal — let the food speak. Small logo + "品馨蔬食" + website at end
