## 4. PLATFORM STRATEGY

### 4.1 Instagram — The Brand Authority Hub

**Role**: Premium brand home. Grid = portfolio. Carousels = engagement engine. Reels = growth engine.

**Grid rhythm**: BOLD | PHOTO | CLEAN (repeating rows of 3)
- BOLD: Dark PX Green bg, massive CN typography, gold accents (CAT-01, 02, 07)
- PHOTO: Full-bleed editorial food photography, warm tones (CAT-02, 05)
- CLEAN: Cream bg, benefit cards, social proof, breathing space (CAT-03, 04)
- Max 40% same color mood in any visible 9-grid

**Format mix**: 40% Carousels (10.15% engagement avg) + 40% Reels (3-5x reach) + 20% Static

**Posting schedule**:

| Day | Time (MY) | Format | Theme |
|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| Mon | 9:00 PM | Carousel | Menu Monday — dish spotlight |
| Tue | 12:30 PM | Reel | Cooking process / recipe |
| Wed | 9:00 PM | Carousel | Education — health/ingredient |
| Thu | 7:00 PM | Reel | Customer story / taste test |
| Fri | 9:00 PM | Carousel | Weekend promo (combo deal) |
| Sat | 11:00 AM | Reel | Lifestyle / "What I eat" |
| Sun | 9:00 PM | Carousel | Social proof roundup |

**Stories**: 5-8/day. Morning poll (8-9 AM), lunch trigger (12-1 PM), interactive quiz (3-4 PM), social proof repost (6-7 PM), BTS storytelling (9-10 PM).

**Algorithm rules**: Watch time > everything. Saves + Shares > Likes. Reply to every comment within 1 hour. Keyword SEO in captions > hashtags. 3-5 hashtags max (#PinxinVegan #品馨蔬食 + 2-3 niche rotation).

### 4.2 Facebook — The Community + Conversion Engine

**Role**: Community building (Group), conversion (Page ads), 35+ audience reach.

**Dual strategy**:
- **Page**: "Pinxin Vegan Cuisine 品馨蔬食" — polished brand posts, Reels cross-posted from IG, ads, WhatsApp CTAs
- **Group**: "Pinxin Plant-Based Kitchen 品馨素食厨房" — private community, recipes, exclusive deals, customer photos, 5-10x organic reach vs Page

**Key rules**:
- NEVER put links in main caption (50-80% suppression). Link goes in first comment.
- Cross-post ALL IG Reels to FB (free distribution, same-day Reels get 50% more reach)
- Group exclusives: weekly recipe, member-only bundle pricing, first-access to new dishes
- 5-7 posts/week on Page, 3-4 discussions/week in Group

### 4.3 TikTok — The Growth Bomb (LAUNCH PRIORITY)

**Role**: Fastest organic growth. Lo-fi wins. 18.5M MY users. Gen Z frozen food discovery channel.

**Account type**: Start as Creator Account (11% avg engagement vs 2.5-4% Business). Full music library access. Switch to Business when TikTok Shop approved.

**Content pillars**:

| Pillar | % | Examples |
|--------|---|---------|
| FOOD PORN | 30% | ASMR eating, steaming close-ups, sizzle sounds |
| MYTH BUSTING | 20% | "Plant-based is bland" debunked, blind taste tests |
| TRANSFORMATION | 20% | Freezer → 15 min → beautiful plate |
| LIFESTYLE | 15% | "What I eat in a day", busy parent meals |
| BRAND STORY | 15% | Founder Audrey, kitchen BTS, Penang heritage |

**Posting**: 1-2x/day, 7 days/week. Peak times: 12-2 PM (lunch) + 8-11 PM (prime).

**Hook formulas** (first 3 seconds decide everything):
1. Visual shock — extreme close-up of steaming dish opening
2. Contrarian claim — "Plant-based rendang that meat lovers can't tell apart"
3. Sound-first — ASMR sizzle, no text for 2 seconds
4. POV — "POV: You come home to this after a long day"

**70% watch without sound. ALWAYS add text overlays.**

**TikTok Shop**: Frozen food is invite-only in MY. Apply immediately. Meanwhile route all traffic to WhatsApp via bio link.

### 4.4 XHS — The Premium Chinese Audience Play

**Role**: Core Chinese-speaking demo. 145% F&B growth. KOC seeding paradise. "Quiet luxury" perfectly fits XHS aesthetic.

**Content format**: 60% image notes (9 photos + 600-1,000 chars) + 40% video notes (30-60s, 3:4 vertical)

**Title formulas that work on XHS**:

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| 数字+效果 | "15分钟搞定的5道素食大餐！上班族救星" |
| 对比反差 | "素食也能这么香？！连肉食者都被骗了" |
| 测评/开箱 | "品馨冷冻素食开箱｜槟城手工慢炖4小时" |
| 种草/安利 | "回购了10次的素食仁当！终于忍不住分享了" |
| 痛点解决 | "减脂期不知道吃什么？这个冷冻素食救了我" |

**Algorithm**: Saves > Comments > Shares > CTR. First 1-4 hours engagement velocity is critical. 180+ day accounts get bonus exposure. 60%+ originality required.

**Posting**: 3-5 notes/week, 7-10 PM (MY/SG peak). CN only. Show RM price with SGD equivalent.

**Cross-border**: Tag "马来西亚", "吉隆坡", "槟城". Reference shared Chinese food culture. Festival content = peak search volume for vegetarian food.

## 6. CREATOR & UGC ENGINE

### The Math

Research data:
- UGC campaigns: 50% more engagement + 29% higher conversion than brand content
- Micro-influencers: 8.2% engagement (vs 5.3% macro, vs 0.48% brand average)
- Haidilao: 1.8B views from zero ad spend — 100% UGC-driven
- OLIPOP: Cut ALL paid ads, went 100% organic/creator, still growing
- 50% of TikTok Shop purchases come from influencer posts

### KOC Seeding Plan

**The Pyramid**:

| Tier | Who | Volume | Cost/Post | Monthly Budget | Platform |
|------|-----|--------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| **KOL** | 50K+ followers | 1-3/quarter | RM500-2,000 | RM1,500-6,000/quarter | IG, TikTok |
| **KOC** | 1K-50K followers | 10-20/month | RM100-300 | RM1,000-6,000/month | XHS, TikTok |
| **UGC Seeding** | Real customers | 30-50/month | Free product (RM15-25 cost) | RM450-1,250/month | All |

**Total monthly creator budget**: RM2,000-8,000

**KOC Seeding Workflow**:
1. Identify 20-30 food micro-creators on XHS + TikTok monthly (1K-50K followers, MY/SG-based, Chinese-speaking)
2. Send free product box (6-8 frozen meals, RM100-150 value at cost)
3. NO brief. Message: "Try it. If you love it, share it. No obligation."
4. Track who posts. Engage with their content. Repost best performers.
5. Offer ongoing partnership to top 3-5 performers each month
6. KOC content becomes brand content (repost with credit = 2x content output at 0 production cost)

### UGC Campaign Mechanics

**Hashtag Campaign: #PinxinPlatingChallenge**
- Mechanic: Customers plate Pinxin frozen meals and post with hashtag
- Prize: Monthly "Best Plate" wins RM200 Pinxin credit + featured on all brand channels
- Launch: Seed with 10 KOCs doing the challenge first (creates social proof before asking public)
- Target: 50 UGC posts in Month 1, 200+ by Month 3

**Always-On UGC**:
- Repost EVERY customer photo that tags Pinxin (within 24 hours)
- Screenshot WhatsApp testimonials (with permission) → Story content
- Monthly "Customer of the Month" feature (carousel on IG)
- Print #PinxinVegan ON packaging to drive organic UGC (Bibigo/Beyond Meat strategy)

Secondary North Star: **"Sendable" rate** — the % of content that gets DM-shared. This is the #1 algorithm signal on IG (confirmed by Mosseri, Jan 2025) and the best predictor of viral reach.

## APPENDIX A: PLANT-BASED MESSAGING — WHAT WORKS IN MALAYSIA

From Faunalytics SEA research — hardcoded into all content:

**WORKS (use always)**:
1. Health messaging: "lower sodium, zero cholesterol, organic ingredients"
2. Taste-first: "so delicious you can't tell it's plant-based"
3. Affordability: "feeds family of 4 for RM__"
4. Local/traditional: "Malaysian food that happens to be plant-based"
5. Religious alignment: Buddhism, Nine Emperor Gods (12% of MY plant-based motivation)
6. Convenience: "15 minutes from freezer to table"

**FAILS (never use)**:
1. "Meat replacement" framing
2. Environmental/animal welfare as PRIMARY message
3. Western vegan lifestyle imagery
4. Preachy/activist tone
5. "Vegan" as identity label in headlines (use as category descriptor only)

**Pinxin's positioning**: "Premium Malaysian cuisine, slow-cooked 3-4 hours, made with real ingredients. Also happens to be 100% plant-based."

## CLOSING: THE MANDATE

Pinxin has a world-class product trapped inside a below-average social media presence. The frozen food category on social media is a blue ocean — no Asian brand has claimed it. The playbook is clear:

1. **Give the brand a face.** Mdm. Audrey's story is the most powerful untold asset. Film her.
