# FINAL BATCH RULES — Every rule learned from v1 through v10

## NEVER AGAIN (hard failures from all iterations)

### Food
- ❌ NEVER generate AI food — use REAL Pinxin food cutouts ONLY
- ❌ NEVER show food that isn't Pinxin's actual dishes
- ❌ NEVER show packaging that isn't real Pinxin packaging PNGs
- ❌ NEVER have packaging floating in mid-air — must be ON surface, IN hand, or being opened
- ❌ NEVER forget rice on family table scenes
- ❌ NEVER use asam laksa (it's paste, not frozen SKU)

### Logo
- ❌ NEVER let NANO render brand text (品馨, PINXIN, Vegan Cuisine, LOGO)
- ❌ NEVER use combined stacked/horizontal logos — emblem OR logotype ONLY
- ❌ NEVER distort logo aspect ratio — verify bounds before paste
- ❌ NEVER place logo outside safe zone (must be inside center 1080x1080 on 9:16)
- ❌ NEVER overlap logo with other elements — check zone brightness/variance

### Technical
- ❌ NEVER apply editorial_grade, paper_texture, clarity, saturation on NANO output — LOGO + GRAIN ONLY
- ❌ NEVER force-resize to 9:16 (distorts) — use TWO-PASS system
- ❌ NEVER save _raw.png — finals only to save space
- ❌ NEVER use pink/Mirra colors — PX Green #1C372A + Gold #D0AA7F + Cream #F2EEE7
- ❌ NEVER render dense small text — NANO garbles it into AI artifacts
- ❌ NEVER use generic references for ALL designs — format-specific refs matched to each concept

### Strategy
- ❌ NEVER lead with "vegan" or "plant-based"
- ❌ NEVER use Tatler claim on frozen food (it's for paste/restaurant only)
- ❌ NEVER show old pricing 买5送5 / RM12.95
- ❌ NEVER have AI-generated full-body humans (uncanny valley)
