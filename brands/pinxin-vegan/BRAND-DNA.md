## Brand Identity

### Name & Meaning
- **品** (Pin) = quality of taste
- **馨** (Xin) = delectable aroma + founder Audrey's Chinese name
- Full: 品馨蔬食 (Pinxin Vegan Cuisine)

### Founder
Mdm. Audrey — mother, passionate chef, 8+ years running 100% plant-based restaurant in Georgetown, Penang.

### What We Sell
**3 product lines:**
1. **Frozen Ready-to-Eat Dishes** (18 SKUs) — defrost + heat 15 min
   - Signature: Rendang Hericium Mushroom, Classic Curry, BKT, Sambal Petai Tempeh, Black Vinegar, Spicy Asam Fish, Green Curry, Mei Cai, Sesame Ginger, Hokkien Chai Boey, Braised Tofu Radish, Ginger Fish, Asam Manis Fish
   - Soups: Lotus Root, Black Bean, Old Cucumber, Watercress, Vegan BKT
   - Combo Sets: Penang Treasure Kandar, Heritage Nyonya, Timeless Tradition Platter
   - Satay: Classic, Korean Gochujang, Spicy Mala, Dark Soy Sauce (hericium mushroom)

2. **Penang Soup Paste Series** (4 SKUs, ~380g jars, cooks 4-6 servings)
   - Penang Asam Laksa (CNN Global Food Top 50 #7)
   - Penang Har Mee
   - Penang Curry Mee
   - Green Tomyam

3. **Spicy Sauce Jars** (4 SKUs)
   - Sambal Chili, Nyonya Zesty Chili, Namyang Chili, Mala Chili

### USPs (non-negotiable in all marketing)
1. **No gluten-based mock meats** — real ingredients: hericium mushroom (lion's mane), kelp, tempeh
2. **Organic unrefined cane sugar** — no bleached white sugar
3. **Lake Salt Light** — 50% lower sodium, contains potassium + magnesium
4. **100% plant-based** — zero cholesterol, no meat/dairy/eggs
5. **Allium-free** — no garlic/onion, pure vegetarian safe
6. **Slow-cooked 3-4 hours** — "so delicious you can't tell it's plant-based"
7. **Signature ingredient: Hericium Mushroom (Lion's Mane)** — nerve growth factor, memory, anti-inflammatory

### Social Proof
- Tatler Asia: Top 10 Best Asam Laksa in Malaysia — ONLY plant-based entry
- New Straits Times: "Tastes better than the non-vegan version"
- CNN: Asam Laksa = #7 Global Food (traditional dish, PX makes vegan version)
- "1st in Malaysia" plant-based frozen meals badge
- 11,000+ Penang families served

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Target Persona
- **Primary**: Malaysian Chinese families valuing tradition, cultural heritage, festivals (CNY, Nine Emperor Gods)
- **Expansion**: Baba Nyonya, Malay, multi-ethnic Malaysian
- **Age**: 27-45 urban Malaysian-Chinese food lovers
- **Quote**: "We want to sell Pinxin to Malays too"

### Values
1. Family and Tradition — continuity across generations
2. Compassion and Inclusivity — extends to all beings
3. "We do Vegan not only because my family matters but all beings' families matter"

### Content Language
- **Primary**: Chinese (Simplified)
- **Secondary**: English
- **Style**: Bilingual CN/EN for most content

### Headline Patterns That Work
- Specific numbers: "15 minutes to table", "3-4 hours slow-cooked", "50% lower salt"
- Multi-generational: "三代人都爱吃", "whole family loves it"
- Persona-targeted: "Busy mom's lifesaver", "3-highs helper for parents"
- Authentic taste: "Authentic Penang flavor", "like grandmother made it"
- Health: "3-highs friendly", "low oil low salt"
- Heritage: "ancient flavor", "Nyonya style"
- No/without: "No MSG", "No garlic/onion", "No mock meat"

### Instant Rejection in Copy
- Vague health claims without specifics
- Generic food descriptions without brand personality
- Health benefits not connected to USP
- Generic convenience claims
