## BRAND CONSTANTS

**Voice:** Girlboss, unapologetic, first-person, Malaysian casual English. No exclamation marks. No diet culture language (calories, macros, "clean eating", "slim down"). She assumes intelligence — never preaches.

**Palette:** Blush #F8BECD | Dusty rose #EBAABD | Cream #FFF9EB | Warm white #FAF7F4 | Salmon #F7AB9F | Deep salmon #E88A7E | Crimson #AC374B | Near-black #252525

**Typography:** Awesome Serif (display) + Mabry Pro (body). Source Han Serif CN + FangZheng Hei (Chinese).

**Logo:** Actual `Mirra Social Media Logo.png` composited via PIL. 140px wide, bottom center, 45px from bottom. NANO must NOT render any brand name — logo added in post-production only.

**Logo safe zone:** Bottom 15% of canvas must stay clear for logo placement. No text/illustration in this zone.

**Post-processing (photos):** crop-to-fit 1080×1350 → grain(0.016) → PIL logo → DONE
**Post-processing (illustrations):** crop-to-fit 1080×1350 → grain(0.028) → PIL logo → DONE

**Typography:** Editorial serif style (Playfair Display / Cormorant Garamond / EB Garamond energy). NANO renders quote text as part of the illustration — must look editorial, not plain.

**Language:** EN primary (91% of revenue). CN secondary.

## 10 CONTENT CATEGORIES

Adapted from PX taxonomy + Mirra proven formulas + 2026 engagement research.

### CAT-01: FAKE UI (Conversion Machine)
**What:** Notes app, iMessage, WhatsApp, IG DM, AirDrop, Calendar, Calculator screenshots with Mirra narrative inside.
**Why:** EVERY Mirra ad winner uses this format. RM2.4-4.1/convo. Tricks the scroll — processed as "someone's real phone" before recognizing brand.
**Pipeline:** Edit-first (god mode). Reference = real UI screenshot → swap content/food/brand.
**Funnel:** BOFU (30%). Direct conversion.
**Frequency:** 3-4/week
**Ref folder:** `01-y2k-device-screen` + proven finals (`diet-bento-v1/`, `diet-v2/`)

### CAT-02: FOOD HERO (Consideration Anchor)
**What:** Single dish editorial photography. Real bento, warm salmon/coral scene, styled props. Aspirational "she eats well. on purpose." energy.
**Why:** Shows WHAT Mirra IS. Serves MOFU consideration role. Not a closer, but builds the mental image of the product.
**Pipeline:** Mood ref as Image 1 + food cutout as Image 2 → NANO editorial scene. System constants approach.
**Funnel:** Consideration (50%). Product education.
**Frequency:** 2-3/week
**Ref folder:** `mirra photo type` + `Mirra colour tone` + food library

### CAT-03: ILLUSTRATION / MIRRA GIRL (Brand World)
**What:** Korean semi-realistic illustrated girl(s) in relatable daily moments. NOT always about food. Pure girl content: office life, morning routines, inner monologue, friendships, self-care, overthinking, main character moments. The brand world is HER LIFE, not just her lunch.
**Why:** Builds brand personality and shareability. "Send this to your bestie who..." format. Creates the emotional world that makes followers WANT to follow Mirra — because she gets them.
**Pipeline:** 1 style ref → NANO generation mode (NOT edit). Crop-to-fit 4:5. Grain 0.028. PIL logo.
**Funnel:** Awareness (15%) + Advocacy (5%). Brand love.
**Frequency:** 4-5/week (PRIMARY content type)
**Ref folder:** `10-illustration-refs/` (6 locked Korean webtoon refs)
**Style rules:** Multiple girls (not one locked character). Dark straight-wavy hair, warm eyes, mature face (25-28), NOT Disney. Soft cel-shading with tapered brown outlines.
**Content source:** `03_research/VIRAL-GIRL-CONTENT-FORENSIC-2025-2026.md` — proven viral formats, NOT imagination.
**Formats:** Quote post, meme (holding card/sign), 3-panel comic, 4-panel comic, scene (desk/cozy/mirror), vibe post.
**Content split:** 60% pure girl life (no food mention) / 40% food-adjacent (lunch, eating, cooking humor).

### CAT-04: SPARKLE QUOTE (Mood + Share)
**What:** Glitter billboard, cinema marquee, sparkly surface with girlboss affirmation text. Real-world objects with sparkle overlay — mirrors, balloons, signage.
**Why:** Highest organic share format for lifestyle brands. "Screenshot and repost" energy. Connects Mirra to aspiration without selling.
**Pipeline:** Reference from sparkle/glitter library → NANO edit (swap text to Mirra copy, shift palette to blush/salmon).
**Funnel:** Awareness (15%). Reach + shares.
**Frequency:** 1-2/week
**Ref folder:** `02-glitter-billboard-quote` + `04-pure-vibe-sparkle`

### CAT-05: TYPOGRAPHIC QUOTE (Grid Breathing Room)
**What:** Pure typography on blush/cream gradient. Serif headline, Mirra voice. Minimal — just words and color.
**Why:** Grid palette cleanser between heavy visual posts. High save rate. Clean grid aesthetic.
**Pipeline:** Reference from typography library → NANO edit (swap copy, lock to Mirra palette).
**Funnel:** Awareness (15%). Brand voice.
**Frequency:** 1-2/week
**Ref folder:** `08-typographic-quote`
**Style variants:** Script on blush | Bold serif on gradient | Note card format | List format ("She is...")

### CAT-06: ATTITUDE MEME (Viral Potential)
**What:** Pop culture still / relatable scene with girlboss caption overlay. Movie stills, TV moments, editorial photos with text.
**Why:** Highest DM send potential. "Send this to..." format. Algorithm loves forwards > likes.
**Pipeline:** Source meme-worthy stills → add Mirra-voice text overlay via NANO. Keep raw/organic feel.
**Funnel:** Awareness (15%). Viral reach.
**Frequency:** 1-2/week
**Ref folder:** `03-attitude-meme-still`
**Voice:** "I'm not on a diet I just eat better than you" energy. Never preachy.

### CAT-07: BOLD GRAPHIC (Information Design)
**What:** Receipt format, calendar, checklist, comparison grid, process flow. Structured information in branded graphic design.
**Why:** Utility + design = save-worthy. "Practical pretty" content people bookmark.
**Pipeline:** Reference from graphic library → god mode edit-first (swap brand/content/palette).
**Funnel:** Consideration (50%). Product education through design.
**Frequency:** 1-2/week
**Ref folder:** `06-graphic-brand-design` + proven Chuck's refs

### CAT-08: RECIPE / HOW-TO CAROUSEL (Highest Save Rate)
**What:** Step-by-step carousel showing how to enjoy Mirra bentos. "3 ways to upgrade your lunch", "your mirra bento but make it fancy", meal prep hacks.
**Why:** 6.9% median engagement (carousel). Highest save rate format. Utility = shareability.
**Pipeline:** Each slide = different dish/preparation. Food hero pipeline per slide. Hook slide = illustration-led or bold typography.
**Funnel:** Consideration (50%). Product education + utility.
**Frequency:** 2/week (minimum)
**Rules:** Different dish + vessel + color per slide. Color flow across slides. Audit as swipe experience.

### CAT-09: MYTH-BUST / EDUCATIONAL CAROUSEL (2.3× More Saves)
**What:** "Things you think are healthy but aren't", "what your grab lunch actually costs you", "the lunch math nobody does". Fact-based, data-driven carousel.
**Why:** 2.3× more saves than static educational posts. Debate-triggering = comments. Identity-affirming = shares.
**Pipeline:** Hook slide (bold typography on brand color) → fact slides (food + data) → CTA slide.
**Funnel:** Consideration (50%). Authority building.
**Frequency:** 1-2/week
**Rules:** Each slide visually distinct. Hook must create curiosity gap. NO diet culture framing.

### CAT-10: THIS-OR-THAT / INTERACTIVE (Comment Trigger)
**What:** Binary choice ("Grab lunch vs Mirra bento"), tier list, bingo card, "rate your lunch on a scale of...". Interactive formats that demand response.
**Why:** Highest comment rate. Algorithm weights comments heavily. Creates community.
**Pipeline:** Bold graphic pipeline. Split screen or grid format. Clean, immediate visual.
**Funnel:** Awareness (15%) + Consideration (50%). Engagement + product comparison.
**Frequency:** 1/week
**Ref folder:** `06-graphic-brand-design` + proven T06 (binary choice)

```

```

### Pipeline 3: ILLUSTRATION (CAT-03) — viral-regen-v2 LOCKED
```
1. FIND viral reference (scrape IG/Pinterest for proven viral post)
2. ANALYZE reference (describe composition/layout/joke structure in TEXT)
3. GENERATE: 1 style ref as Image 1 → NANO generation mode
   API: aspect_ratio="4:5", resolution="2K"
   Prompt: ANTI-BRAND block + STYLE DNA + EXPLICIT FACE + layout description
4. POST-PROCESS: crop-to-fit 1080×1350 → grain(0.028) → PIL expert logo → DONE

Content: 60% pure girl life, 40% food-adjacent. Source from viral research ONLY.
Format: follows the viral reference's EXACT layout (not fixed templates).
Food: if shown, must be actual Mirra vegan bento (cream tray, pink rice, samosa, curry).
Logo: PIL only (NANO renders ZERO brand text). Smart zone detection.
Face: ALWAYS include explicit face rendering instruction in prompt.
Full pipeline doc: feedback_mirra_social_production_pipeline_locked.md
```

## MULTI-PLATFORM STRATEGY (beyond IG)

This system starts as IG-first but should expand. Research: NO meal delivery brand has cracked world-class social. The #1 spot is empty.

### Platform Roles
| Platform | Role | Priority | Content Adaptation |
|----------|------|----------|-------------------|
| **Instagram** | Brand authority hub. Grid = portfolio. | Phase 1 (NOW) | 4:5 feed, Stories, Reels |
| **TikTok** | Growth bomb. Lo-fi wins. 18.5M MY users. | Phase 2 (Month 2) | 9:16 vertical, sound-first, text overlay always |
| **Facebook** | Community + 35+ audience. WA CTA channel. | Phase 2 (Month 2) | Cross-post IG Reels. NEVER link in main caption. |
| **XHS** | CN audience play. If Mirra expands CN. | Phase 3 (if needed) | 3:4, 600+ char CN captions, diary tone |

### "One Piece → Multi-Platform" Repurposing
Every IG post should be adapted, not just cross-posted:
- IG Static (4:5) → extend to 9:16 for TikTok/Stories/Reels
- IG Carousel → individual slides become TikTok stills or Story sequence
- Illustration post → crop to square for FB, extend to 9:16 for Stories
- Fake UI post → native to all platforms (already looks like phone content)

### Algorithm Rules Per Platform
| Platform | #1 Signal | Key Rule |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| IG | DM sends/shares | Saves + Shares > Likes. Reply to every comment within 1 hour. |
| TikTok | Watch time | First 3 seconds = everything. 70% watch without sound → text overlay. |
| FB | Meaningful interactions | NEVER put links in main caption (50-80% suppression). Link in first comment. |

## METRICS THAT MATTER

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| DM sends | Highest priority | 2026 algorithm weights sends > likes (Mosseri confirmed) |
| Saves | Track per CAT | Saves = utility value (recipe, educational). Carousels = 10.15% avg. |
| Comments | Track per format | Interactive formats (CAT-10) should drive this |
| Shares | Track per format | Memes (CAT-06) + sparkle quotes (CAT-04) should drive this |
| Follower growth | Weekly | Overall brand health |
| Cost per WA conversation | <RM5 EN | Primary ad conversion metric |
| UGC posts/month | Target 10 → 30 → 50+ | Customer-to-brand content ratio |
| Series engagement | Track per series | Kill <1% after 4 episodes, scale >3% |

### Kill/Scale Criteria
| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Content series avg engagement < 1% after 4 episodes | KILL — replace with new series |
| Content series avg engagement > 3% | SCALE — increase frequency, allocate ad spend |
| Any single post > 10x avg engagement | Reverse-engineer formula, produce 5 variations within 48h |
| UGC post outperforms brand content | Boost as ad creative via Post ID |
