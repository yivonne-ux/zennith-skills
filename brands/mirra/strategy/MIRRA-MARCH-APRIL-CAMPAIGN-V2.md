## SECTION 12: COPY RULES (Non-negotiable)

1. **All lowercase** — brand voice. "mirra" not "Mirra" or "MIRRA" in body copy.
2. **No exclamation marks** — ever. Period replaces enthusiasm. Confidence > excitement.
3. **First person > second person** — "i switched my lunch" beats "switch your lunch" by 90% CTR.
4. **Price always specific** — "RM19" not "affordable". "50+" not "many".
5. **No brand buzzwords** — never say "plant-based" as lead. Never say "healthy meal delivery." Never say "subscription box."
6. **The invisible sell** — the ad should feel like a friend sharing, not a brand selling.
7. **Code-switch where natural** — "order了再说lah" works. Forced mixing doesn't.
8. **"From RM17++"** for Fit Fam. **"From RM19"** for Solo. Use whichever matches the ad's target.
9. **WhatsApp CTA always** — "chat us" / "whatsapp us" / "message us". Never "buy now" or "shop now".
10. **Special gift = curiosity gap** — mention it exists at 20+ meals. Never reveal what it is in the ad.
