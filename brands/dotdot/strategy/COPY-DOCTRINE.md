# DotDot Copy Doctrine

> Voice, tone, headline formulas, personas, and compliance rules for all DotDot content.
> Language: Traditional Chinese (written) + Cantonese (spoken/video voiceover).

---

## Voice Identity

**The DotDot voice** = A knowledgeable Hong Kong uncle who genuinely cares about your knees.

Not a doctor (too clinical). Not a salesman (too pushy). Not a wellness influencer (too trendy).
Danny Tsang's real voice — warm, practical, experienced, direct. Like getting advice from a trusted family friend who happens to know a lot about joint health.

### Voice DNA
- **Warm but not soft** — caring with substance, not platitudes
- **Authoritative but not clinical** — expert knowledge in everyday language
- **Personal but not dramatic** — real stories, not manufactured emotion
- **Practical but not boring** — actionable advice with personality
- **Hopeful but not exaggerated** — recovery is possible, but honest about the journey

## Language Rules

### Written Language: Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)
- All IG captions, XHS posts, static text: Traditional Chinese characters
- Natural HK written style — not overly formal (書面語), not pure Cantonese transcription (口語)
- Sweet spot: written Chinese with Cantonese flavoring (港式書面語)

### Spoken Language: Cantonese (廣東話)
- All video voiceover, Reels narration: Cantonese
- Natural, conversational — like Danny talking to a customer at the expo
- Subtitles in Traditional Chinese (not Cantonese transcription)

### Cantonese Flavor Words (use naturally, not forced)
| Cantonese | Meaning | Usage |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| 膝頭哥 | Knee (colloquial) | More relatable than 膝蓋 |
| 行路 | Walking | Instead of formal 步行 |
| 上落樓梯 | Going up/down stairs | Core pain point language |
| 唔使驚 | Don't worry | Reassurance |
| 試吓 | Try it | CTA softener |
| 好返 | Got better | Recovery language |
| 老友記 | Elderly friends (respectful) | Audience address |
| 幫到你 | Help you | Promise language |

### Words We Use vs Words We Don't

| USE (用) | DON'T USE (唔用) |
|---------|----------------|
| 幫助修補 (help repair) | 治癒/根治 (cure) |
| 支持關節健康 (support joint health) | 保證有效 (guaranteed effective) |
| 改善活動能力 (improve mobility) | 立即見效 (immediate results) |
| 德國製造 (Made in Germany) | 全球最好 (best in the world) |
| 14年口碑 (14 years word of mouth) | 醫生推薦 (doctor recommended — unless verified) |
| 膠原蛋白 (collagen protein) | 藥物 (medicine/drug) |
| 保健食品 (health supplement) | 治療方案 (treatment plan) |
| 個人效果因人而異 (results vary) | 人人有效 (works for everyone) |
| 建議諮詢醫生 (suggest consulting doctor) | 唔使睇醫生 (no need to see doctor) |

## 6 Headline Formulas

### Formula 1: Pain Point + Question (痛點提問)
**Pattern**: [Specific pain] + 你有冇試過？/ 點解會咁？
**Examples**:
- 上落樓梯膝頭痛？3個原因你要知
- 落雨天關節特別痛？原來同呢樣嘢有關
- 行多兩步就膝頭軟？唔好忽視呢個訊號

### Formula 2: Number + Actionable (數字行動)
**Pattern**: [Number] + [Actionable benefit]
**Examples**:
- 3個坐住做嘅護膝運動
- 每日5分鐘 強化膝關節
- 60歲後必做嘅4個膝蓋保養動作

### Formula 3: Myth Buster (拆解迷思)
**Pattern**: [Common belief] + 真定假？/ 其實...
**Examples**:
- 行路傷膝蓋？醫生話你知真相
- 食鈣片就可以護膝？你可能搞錯咗
- 膝頭痛就唔好郁？呢個想法大錯特錯

### Formula 4: Before/After Story (前後故事)
**Pattern**: [Before state] → [After state] + 佢做咗咩？
**Examples**:
- 以前上樓梯要人扶 而家自己行山去
- 半年前膝頭痛到瞓唔着 而家日日晨運
- 70歲伯伯嘅關節回春之路

### Formula 5: Authority + Trust (權威信任)
**Pattern**: 德國 / 14年 / 13萬用戶 + [credibility statement]
**Examples**:
- 源自德國 14年專注修補膝關節
- 13萬+用戶嘅共同選擇
- 德國創新獎認證 信心保證

### Formula 6: Seasonal / Situational (情境觸發)
**Pattern**: [Situation/season] + [relevant advice]
**Examples**:
- 回南天膝蓋又發作？3招幫你紓緩
- 新年行花市 膝頭準備好未？
- 重陽登高前 呢幾個動作一定要做

## Entity Hypothesis + SARA Framework

Every piece of content must define:

### Entity Hypothesis
WHO is this specific person and WHAT is their specific situation?

```
Entity: 65歲退休教師陳太
Situation: 膝蓋退化三年，上落樓梯需要扶扶手
Desired outcome: 可以自己去飲茶、行花市
Barrier: 怕食supplement唔work，已經試過好多
```

### SARA Mindstate (at time of viewing)
| State | Content Approach |
|-------|-----------------|
| **S**uffering | Validate the pain → "我哋明白膝頭痛嘅感覺" |
| **A**ware | Educate on the cause → "點解膝蓋會退化？" |
| **R**esearching | Provide evidence → "德國技術 × 13萬用戶" |
| **A**cting | Clear CTA → "WhatsApp我哋了解更多" |

## Compliance Gate (MANDATORY)

### Before Publishing ANY Content, Verify:

- [ ] No "cure" language (治癒/根治/100%有效)
- [ ] No specific medical outcome promises
- [ ] "Individual results may vary" qualifier on testimonials
- [ ] "Consult your doctor" disclaimer on exercise content
- [ ] No competitor disparagement
- [ ] No unlicensed medical advice
- [ ] Product described as "health supplement" (保健食品), never "medicine" (藥物)
- [ ] Pricing accurate (check current rates)
- [ ] WhatsApp number correct
- [ ] Awards/credentials accurately stated with year

### HK Regulatory Context
- Trade Descriptions Ordinance (商品說明條例) — no false/misleading claims
- Undesirable Medical Advertisements Ordinance (不良醫藥廣告條例) — supplements cannot claim to treat/prevent disease
- Safe harbor: education content + "support" language + "results vary" qualifier
