## IG Grid Rhythm (3 posts/day)

### Row Pattern (every 3 posts = 1 row on grid)

| Position 1 | Position 2 | Position 3 |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **BOLD** — Dark/teal bg, large Traditional CN headline, anatomy illustration | **PHOTO** — Product shot, Danny/user photo, clinic BTS, real-world setting | **CLEAN** — Light/white bg, exercise step-by-step, minimal card, infographic |

### Color Flow Across Rows
- Row 1: Teal bg → Photo → White card
- Row 2: Orange bg → Photo → Cream card
- Row 3: Dark green bg → Photo → Light teal card
- Rotate brand colors through Position 1 for visual grid harmony

### Grid Safety
- IG crops 4:5 to 3:4 in profile grid — keep critical elements within center 1015px
- Squint test: grid should read as coherent color pattern when text is unreadable

| Type | Volume | Duration | Style |
|------|--------|----------|-------|

| Education Explainer | 12 | 45-90s | 3D mascot explains anatomy/conditions with text overlays |
| Myth Buster / FAQ | 6 | 30-45s | Quick myth vs fact with mascot reactions |

**Cross-posted to**: IG Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts (same file, platform-native captions)

- @business.shorts: Pixar 3D character + bold text overlays + narrative voiceover
- @thegoodsuniverse: 3D animated objects with faces, health education, talking characters
- DotDot direction: 3D animated mascot character explaining knee health topics

## Content Variety Pre-Flight Checklist

Before finalizing any weekly plan, verify:

- [ ] All 10 categories represented across the week (minimum 7 of 10)
- [ ] ACCA funnel ratios maintained (20/45/25/10)
- [ ] Grid rhythm BOLD → PHOTO → CLEAN maintained
- [ ] No more than 3 consecutive same-category posts
- [ ] At least 5 carousels per week (IG)
- [ ] Exercise demos spread across the week (not clustered)
- [ ] Product/brand content max 20% (not overselling)
- [ ] Different body parts/conditions featured (not all knee, mix in hip/back/general mobility)
- [ ] Cantonese voice consistent across captions
- [ ] Festival check: any upcoming HK festivals requiring content?

## Festival Calendar (HK-Specific)

### Core Festivals (deep campaign — 3-5 posts + video)
- **Chinese New Year** (Jan/Feb) — family reunion meals, mobility for temple visits, gift bundles
- **Mid-Autumn Festival** (Sep) — walking to lantern events, family outdoor activities
- **Chung Yeung / Elderly Day** (Oct) — 重陽節 — **THE most brand-aligned festival**. Respect for elders, hiking tradition, joint health awareness
- **Mother's Day / Father's Day** — gifting angle (adult children buying for parents)

### Cross-Cultural (1 respectful greeting)
- Christmas, Easter — brief greeting, no campaign
- National Day — acknowledge only

### Commerce Events
- **HK Brands & Products Expo** (工展會, Dec-Jan) — DotDot's biggest offline event, heavy content
- **Hong Kong Book Fair** (Jul) — skip, no brand fit
- **11.11 / Black Friday** — only if running online promo

### Skip Entirely
- Hari Raya, Deepavali, Thanksgiving — no audience connection
- Valentine's Day — low brand fit unless gift angle

## Shareability Strategy

**2026 algorithm priority**: DM sends > saves > comments > likes

### High-Share Formats for DotDot
1. **"Send this to your parents"** exercise carousels — adult children share with elderly parents
2. **Myth vs Fact** cards — shareable debate starters
3. **"Which one are you?"** knee pain type quiz — interactive, taggable
4. **Before/After mobility stories** — inspirational shares
5. **Stair climbing do's & don'ts** — universal, practical, saveable
6. **3 exercises you can do sitting down** — shareable utility
7. **"Is your knee telling you something?"** symptom checker — save for reference

### Engagement Hooks
- "Tag someone who needs this" (標記一個需要嘅人)
- "Save this for later" (收藏呢篇)
- "Share with your family group chat" (分享去家庭群組)
- "Which exercise works best for you?" (邊個動作最適合你?)

## Naming Convention

```
DD-{CAT##}-{PLATFORM}-{LANG}-{TOPIC}-{FORMAT}-{VERSION}
```

Examples:
- `DD-CAT01-IG-TC-meniscus-anatomy-single-v1`
- `DD-CAT05-VID-TC-chair-exercise-reel-v1`
- `DD-CAT03-XHS-TC-walking-myth-carousel-v1`

Where:
- `DD` = DotDot
- `CAT##` = category number (01-10)
- Platform = `IG`, `VID`, `XHS`
- `TC` = Traditional Chinese, `CANT` = Cantonese voiceover
- Format = `single`, `carousel`, `reel`, `short`
