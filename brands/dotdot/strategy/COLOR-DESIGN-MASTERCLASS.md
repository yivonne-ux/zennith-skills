# Color Design, Typography & Visual Layering Masterclass

> 82 production rules. Researched from Ritual, AG1, OLLY, WCAG, HK/TW commercial design,
> and 6 rounds of DotDot calibration. Date: 2026-03-29.

---

## 1. COLOR LAYERING — Product-on-Background Contrast (17 Rules)

**Rule 1.** Orange product = teal background. Complementary colors = maximum contrast.
**Rule 2.** Never place product on bg within 30° of its hue. Minimum 60° separation.
**Rule 3.** 70/20/10 Brightness Split. Bg at 70% or 20% brightness. Product at natural. Accent at the extreme.
**Rule 4.** Dark product on light bg, light product on dark bg. Value contrast > hue contrast for elderly.
**Rule 5.** 60% = background/dominant. Sets mood. DotDot: teal, cream, white, or dark teal.
**Rule 6.** 30% = secondary. Where product sits. DotDot: orange (on teal bg) or teal (on cream bg).
**Rule 7.** 10% = accent pop. CTA badges, numbered circles. DotDot: gold, trust green, white on dark.
**Rule 8.** If product IS the 30%, background CANNOT be same color family. Orange box = NEVER orange bg.
**Rule 9.** German Collagen (orange): use teal, deep teal, cream, or near-black bg. NEVER warm tones.
**Rule 10.** Silicon/Glacier Milk (teal): use cream, warm sand, soft coral, or white bg.
**Rule 11.** German Apple (pink): use sage green, cream, or deep forest green bg.
**Rule 12.** German Misha (yellow): use deep navy, teal, charcoal, or cool grey bg.
**Rule 13.** Warm product = cool bg. Cool product = warm bg.
**Rule 14.** Shadows opposite temperature of dominant light. Warm image = blue-grey shadows.
**Rule 15.** Solid bg needs micro-texture. Pure flat = cheap. Add subtle paper grain 0.015-0.02.
**Rule 16.** Gradient bg must move AWAY from product color. Orange product = cream→teal gradient.
**Rule 17.** Max 2 dominant colors + 1 accent (3 total hues besides product).

## 2. SMART LOGO PLACEMENT (12 Rules)

**Rule 18.** Default: top-right, 35px from edges. Locked for all DotDot social.
**Rule 19.** BOFU/promo: top-center with full wordmark.
**Rule 20.** Logo max height: 80px at 1080px width.
**Rule 21.** Never place logo over faces, product, or headline text. Reserve 120x80px safe zone.
**Rule 22.** Busy bg: white pill backing (16px radius, 85% opacity).
**Rule 23.** Solid bg: no backing needed.
**Rule 24.** Transparent PNG logo, never JPEG.
**Rule 25.** If bg brightness stddev > 30% under logo zone, add pill backing (programmatic check).
**Rule 26.** One logo mode per batch.
**Rule 27.** PIL places logo. NANO never renders brand marks.
**Rule 28.** Logo on RAW output BEFORE pad-fit resizing.
**Rule 29.** Test at 3x zoom out (33% = grid thumbnail).

## 3. PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY POST DESIGN (14 Rules)

**Rule 30.** Product occupies 40-60% of frame height.
**Rule 31.** 3/4 angle at 15-20° tilt. Slight tilt adds dimension.
**Rule 32.** Product base at vertical center or lower third. Grounded, not floating.
**Rule 33.** Never AI-generate product packaging. Always composite real PNG.
**Rule 34.** Drop shadow: 45° angle, 15-25px offset, 30-40px blur, 20-30% opacity.
**Rule 35.** Contact shadow: tight blurred ellipse under product. Anchors to surface.
**Rule 36.** Glow effect: dark backgrounds ONLY. Brand orange at 10-15% opacity.
**Rule 37.** Reflection: only on glossy/premium surfaces. 20% opacity mirror.
**Rule 38.** 3-layer stack: bg (AI/solid) + product PNG with shadow + text overlay.
**Rule 39.** Partial product cropping: 10-15% beyond frame edge for dynamism.
**Rule 40.** Multiple products: max 2 per post. Hero at 100%, secondary at 60%.
**Rule 41.** Product casts color onto adjacent surface. Orange box = warm reflection on nearby surface.
**Rule 42.** Headline above product, details below. Eye reads top-to-bottom.
**Rule 43.** Price/CTA badge overlapping product edge (40% overlap). Accent color (gold/green).

## 4. CHINESE/HK TYPOGRAPHY (15 Rules)

**Rule 44.** 2-3 colors WITHIN one headline. Benefit words teal, action words orange, rest black.
**Rule 45.** TC characters as GRAPHIC ELEMENTS. Fills, outlines, shadows, dimensional effects.
**Rule 46.** Extreme size hierarchy: headline 4-5x body text. 20-30% frame height.
**Rule 47.** Black weight or heavier for TC headlines. Thin strokes = illegible for elderly.
**Rule 48.** Key info in colored pill shapes. Plain text for key info = invisible.
**Rule 49.** Numbered steps: 60px+ diameter teal circles. Visual anchors.
**Rule 50.** Ribbon/stamp for trust signals. "德國No.1" stamp. "14年信心保證" ribbon.
**Rule 51.** TC headline dominant, English at 30% size below.
**Rule 52.** Never mix Simplified + Traditional on same post. Exception: XHS hashtags.
**Rule 53.** Line spacing: 150-200% of character size for TC body.
**Rule 54.** 15-25 characters per line max for TC body.
**Rule 55.** Text wraps around illustrations. Text is part of composition, not floating.
**Rule 56.** Vertical text for accent: short 2-4 char TC in margins.
**Rule 57.** Max 2 typefaces per post. One display + one body.
**Rule 58.** Teal #4DBFB8 on white = 2.4:1 (FAIL for body). Save for LARGE headline only.

## 5. ELDERLY VISUAL HIERARCHY (14 Rules)

**Rule 59.** Min headline: 48pt (64px). For 20/40 vision at phone distance.
**Rule 60.** Min body: 28pt (37px). Smaller = decorative only.
**Rule 61.** Min badge text: 24pt. Below this, TC characters indistinguishable.
**Rule 62.** Line height: 1.8x for TC body text (not 1.5x).
**Rule 63.** Body text: 7:1 contrast min (WCAG AAA). Black on white = 17.4:1 ✓.
**Rule 64.** Large headline (48pt+): 4.5:1 min. Brand colors OK for large text.
**Rule 65.** Never rely on color alone. Add text labels + icons.
**Rule 66.** Teal text on white: HEADLINE ONLY, never body. 2.4:1 ratio.
**Rule 67.** Max 3-4 information elements per post.
**Rule 68.** ONE primary message per post.
**Rule 69.** Size hierarchy dictates reading order. Biggest first.
**Rule 70.** Simple concrete icons. Walking figure = exercise. Abstract swirl = nothing.
**Rule 71.** CTA explicit: "WhatsApp 94626554 查詢" with phone icon.
**Rule 72.** Key message on slide 1. Elderly rarely swipe past slide 1.

## 6. STYLE ANCHOR — Batch Consistency (10 Rules)

**Rule 73.** ONE Image 1 for entire batch. Content varies via prompt only.
**Rule 74.** Best Image 1 = own approved output.
**Rule 75.** If no approved output, ONE locked Pinterest ref for all calibration.
**Rule 76.** Never change Image 1 mid-batch.
**Rule 77.** Lock the style sentence. Unchanged across every prompt.
**Rule 78.** Lock hex codes in every prompt.
**Rule 79.** Lock post-processing chain values.
**Rule 80.** Calibration: 3-6 tests → human picks ONE winner → that = Image 1 for batch.
**Rule 81.** Change ONE variable per iteration.
**Rule 82.** Squint test: all posts at 25% zoom. Same brand at a glance = pass.
